# Welcome | VyFinance

**What is VyFinance?** VyFinance is Decentralized Finance protocol built on Cardano that includes features such as a decentralised exchange (DEx), redistributive mechanism (BAR), governance, lottery and token/NFT Vaults.

**What is the VYFI token?**

VYFI is a Cardano Native Token (CNT), used across the VyFinance ecosystem for utilities including Governance, The Lottery and Token Vaults.

[](#before-you-connect-to-the-vyfiance-app)

_**BEFORE YOU CONNECT TO THE VYFIANCE APP**_

_We have recently come across a few errors when users stake VyFi/NFTs through a CCVault address, in order to fix this, users can use the following steps to set their wallet to “Single address mode (for Nami / CNTools)”_

\- Open your CCVault wallet

\- Locate the “Settings” tab

\- Select the slider next to “Single address mode (for Nami / CNTools)” to active this setting

**You are now ready to navigate to VyFinance:** [**https://app.vyfi.io**](https://app.vyfi.io)

\- Select “Connect Wallet” at the top right corner of the page

\- Connect your wallet by Selecting “CCVault.io”

\- Select “Connect to Site”

\- Your wallet will now be connected

\- Now, you will see your wallets address reflected in the top right change corner

# An unexpected error has occurred | VyFinance

**1)** Make sure both your browser of choice and your wallet version is up to date

**2)** Disable any adblocker programs

**3)** Disable any and all VPNs

**4)** Disconnect and reconnect wallet

**5)** Unset wallet collateral and ctrl+f5

**6)** Reset wallet collateral and ctrl+f5

**7)** Restart your browser

**8)** Restart your machine

# Wallet Fracked | VyFinance

[PreviousNo Collateral](/support/common-errors/no-collateral)
[NextAn unexpected error has occurred](/support/common-errors/an-unexpected-error-has-occurred)

Last updated 7 months ago

The “Wallet Fracked” error message can appear for users with a considerable amount of token/NFTs in their wallet.

This is due to the smart contract (CPU/mem limits) being confronted with the UTXO output bundle size.

_Whenever interacting with defi is always recommended to have a wallet with as few assets as possible._

When confronted with this error you can try a couple of different methods to fix the issue.

**Firstly,** send all the assets needed for the transaction (along with 10ADA) to yourself, this will create a brand new UTXO that may fix the problem

_If this does not help your wallet might be exceptionally fracked._

In this case you can use a tool like [**https://unfrack.it/**](https://unfrack.it/)
 to help recover your wallet to a healthier state

_For long term wallet use, you can turn on the “Token Fragmentation” feature in the settings to help keep your UTXO management in a better place_

_Some wallets (such as ETERNL) have a feature to keep your UTXOs managed correctly, this can help avoid fracked wallet issues_

# Tokenomics | VyFinance

[PreviousTeam](/team)
[NextVyFi Token Swap Instructions](/important/token-swap)

Last updated 1 year ago

_Tokenomics Emissions Rate:_ [_https://vyfi-docs.s3.amazonaws.com/emissionrate.pdf_](https://vyfi-docs.s3.amazonaws.com/emissionrate.pdf)

_Tokenomics as set out in our white papers:_ [_https://vyfi-docs.s3.amazonaws.com/whitepaper.pdf_](https://vyfi-docs.s3.amazonaws.com/whitepaper.pdf)

**Total Supply/Full Diluted Market Cap: 450 million**

In the beginning there will be a large supply that will enable Yield Farming for an extended period of time. Around 85% of the token supply will be locked to yield farms. Around 8% of the supply will be locked to our Treasury (Controlled by the governance token). The team will receive a 2.5% share of supply, and this will be vested over a period of 4 years. Our expected market cap at launch will be between 0.5% and 1.5% of supply (depending on how well our ICO preforms). This would give us a market cap between 2.25 – 6.75 million tokens on launch. Any amount that is left from this 1.5% after ICO will be locked equally between farms and the treasury.

It is worth noting that this will be with the Cardano token decimal count of 6. This is opposed to the typical 8 seen in most Cryptocurrencies.

**Inflation**

Since ownership of the token is the only way to stake at the VyFi Bar, there is an inbuild distributive income from holding the token. As a percentage of all fee’s/investment earnings on the site will be used to purchase the tokens on a weekly basis, the price will inflate with use of the platform. The liquidity provision pay-out ratio for the yield farms will decrease with time, also creating a reduced supply as time passes. Furthermore, the combined income from the Auto-Harvester will be distributed as VYFI, creating a continuous upward pressure on our token from the liquidity aggregator.

**How does VYFI enter circulation?**

Through the yield farms. VYFI will be circulating with every block – at a decreasing rate until all 450 million have enter circulation.

Around 0.15% of total supply will be awarded through marketing, pre-ICO, ICO and airdrops. VYFI will also be obtainable through lending against the margin.

**Distributive Token**

VYFI can be staked at the Bar so users can share in the profits of the entire ecosystem. This incentivizes yield-farmers to hold the coin, as they gain access to income through doing so. Access to pools for other tokens across the Cardano ecosystem further incentivizes users to hold our token.

Why is the Swap Needed?


--------------------------------------------------------

The ability to make tokens on Cardano has come long before smart contracts. That being said, airdrops, ICO, and giveaways require something, so we gave a V1 Native Token, which was before the decimal function was known.

You may have noticed your VyFi lacks decimal spaces! The V2, known as VYFI (all caps) will have its proper 6 decimals.

**So what happens to my VyFi?**

Your VyFi is 1:1 swapped to the V2, which is "VYFI". Notice the usage of Uppercase Characters in the V2. You also receive back the ~1.2ADA-1.8ADA Carrying Fee that goes with tokens.

VyFi -> VYFI

[](#swapping-your-vyfi-to-vyfi)

Swapping Your VyFi to VYFI:


----------------------------------------------------------------

_Make sure you have connected your wallet the holds the V1 $VYFI_

\- A pop-up should appear to prompt the token swap. I_f the popup does not appear_ you can select the “VYFI Token Swap” button to start the swap

`_**SKIP THE NEXT STEP IF YOU HOLD YOUR V1 TOKENS IN A NAMI WALLET**_`

Users who hold their V1 VyFi in a **non-NAMI** wallet will be required to send their tokens to the provided address

\- Copy the address provided

\- Send your V1 VyFi from the wallet you hold your tokens to the address provided

\- You'll receive your V2 VYFI tokens in a few minutes

\- Select “Swap”

\- Select “Sign”

\- Enter your NAMI “Spending Password”

\- Select “Confirm”

\- Once your transaction is complete this pop-up will appear to confirm it has been submitted

\- Within a few minutes you will receive your V2 VYFI tokens

_You can view the policy ID for V2 VYFI -_ [CLICK HERE](https://cardanoscan.io/token/804f5544c1962a40546827cab750a88404dc7108c0f588b72964754f56594649)

_You can also view the policy ID for_ _**OLD**_ _V1 VyFi -_ [CLICK HERE](https://cardanoscan.io/token/defca4b95934aec82b818cbafd4bd0ab45511c1d690b58759f07d9b156794669)
