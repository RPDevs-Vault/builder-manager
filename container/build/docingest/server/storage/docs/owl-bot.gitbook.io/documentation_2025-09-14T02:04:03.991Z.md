# Chain alerts | OWL

Track sophisticated wallet movements through funding chains. When fresh wallets receive SOL from other fresh wallets instead of directly from exchanges, it creates a "chain" - often indicating coordinated buying activity or smart money moving through multiple wallets.

#### 

[](https://owl-bot.gitbook.io/docs/alert-channels/chain-alerts#chain-alerts-help-you-spot)

Chain alerts help you spot:

*   Multi wallet type of strategies where buy flows through multiple addresses
    
*   Potentially coordinated buying pattern
    
*   Signaling and detecting smart supply accumulation strategies
    

Each alert shows the chain depth (how many wallets the funds passed through) and traces back to the original funding source. Perfect for identifying more complex trading patterns that single-wallet monitoring might miss.

Filters include max 16 SOL original funding, tokens under 15 hours old, market cap under $50k.

![](https://owl-bot.gitbook.io/docs/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3684067616-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FKONlh6aLxwzduDvNLRxX%252Fuploads%252FB2eEC2duJxRv0fbUYFjd%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D92c2321a-4dae-4771-adab-88556e983865&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=25f0365d&sv=2)

# Jupiter DCA Alerts | OWL

![](https://owl-bot.gitbook.io/docs/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3684067616-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FKONlh6aLxwzduDvNLRxX%252Fuploads%252FSLqoDulINe11jpH6BFKz%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dad3d66e0-17c2-44e1-b7d4-8fa4758b5128&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=ae27ffa8&sv=2)

Monitor automated dollar-cost averaging orders on Jupiter, one of Solana's most important DEX aggregators. These alerts reveal when wallets commit to systematic buying or selling strategies, often this will indicate high-conviction positions and longer-term market sentiment. Track total order sizes, frequency patterns, and cycle counts so you can identify smart money accumulation strategies. Here's what each element means:

#### 

[](https://owl-bot.gitbook.io/docs/alert-channels/jupiter-dca-alerts#symbol-meanings)

Symbol Meanings:

**📊 Active** - The DCA order status. Shows the order is currently running and will execute trades according to schedule.

**🔄 x Cycles** \- The number of individual trades that will be executed. In this case, the order will make x separate purchases.

**⏰ x min** - The frequency between each trade. A new purchase will happen every x minutes.

    💵 Og Funding: 10.0090 SOL - Binance3 - (14m 21s)

### 

[](https://owl-bot.gitbook.io/docs/decoding-alerts/freshie-metrics-breakdown#freshie-indicators)

Freshie Indicators 🐣

**Symbol / Icon**

**Meaning**

🐣

Number of freshies currently holding the token.

🔴

Number of freshies who have fully sold their position.

🟠

Number of freshies who partially sold their position (e.g., 10%).

🤲

Percentage of the token’s total supply currently held by freshies.

* * *

### 

[](https://owl-bot.gitbook.io/docs/decoding-alerts/freshie-metrics-breakdown#notes)

Notes 📝

*   **Freshies** = New wallets entering a coin.
    
*   Helps you understand **retail trader behavior** in real-time (buying, selling, holding).
    
*   The **“On: 💊”** section still shows which **platform the token was launched on** (emoji mapping rules explained in Launch Platform Emojis 🎭).

# Fees & Tips | OWL

### 

[](https://owl-bot.gitbook.io/docs/transaction-details/fees-and-tips#detailed-explanations)

Detailed Explanations 📝

**⏳ Age:** `**~1m ago**` **→ Token Age**

*   This shows **how long ago the token was created** or launched on-chain.
    

* * *

### 

[](https://owl-bot.gitbook.io/docs/transaction-details/fees-and-tips#fee-and-tip)

**💵 Fee & Tip**

*   **Fee: 0.000205 SOL** → This is the **total network fee** used in the transaction.
    
    *   It includes both **base network fee** and **priority fee** combined.
        
    
*   **Tip: 0.001000 SOL (0slot)** → This is an **external fee** paid to third-party providers such as **Jito 0slot**.

# Fees & Tips | OWL

### 

[](https://owl-bot.gitbook.io/docs/transaction-details#detailed-explanations)

Detailed Explanations 📝

**⏳ Age:** `**~1m ago**` **→ Token Age**

*   This shows **how long ago the token was created** or launched on-chain.
    

* * *

### 

[](https://owl-bot.gitbook.io/docs/transaction-details#fee-and-tip)

**💵 Fee & Tip**

*   **Fee: 0.000205 SOL** → This is the **total network fee** used in the transaction.
    
    *   It includes both **base network fee** and **priority fee** combined.
        
    
*   **Tip: 0.001000 SOL (0slot)** → This is an **external fee** paid to third-party providers such as **Jito 0slot**.
