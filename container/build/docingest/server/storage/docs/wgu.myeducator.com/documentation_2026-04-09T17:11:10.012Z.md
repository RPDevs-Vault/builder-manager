Meet Your AI Assistant
----------------------

Proven solutions for increasing engagement and performance in any classroom setting!

### **Your AI Teaching Assistant**

The built-in AI assistant supports your students by answering questions, explaining concepts, and guiding them through the course in real time—reducing routine questions and freeing up your time.

### **You’re in Control**

You control when and where AI tools are available. Enable or disable each AI-powered feature with specificity or turn it off entirely when you need to. Flexible settings let you align AI use with your teaching approach.

### **Teach with Your Voice**

Make the assistant reflect your course by training it on your syllabus, policies, and other materials. It answers students based on your guidance—keeping them aligned with your expectations.

    [Understanding Microlearning: Benefits and Applications for Today’s Learners\
