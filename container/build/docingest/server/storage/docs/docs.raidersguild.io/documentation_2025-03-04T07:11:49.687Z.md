[](#what-are-raids)

What are Raids?


----------------------------------------

A **RAID** is a collective effort involving multiple participants working together to achieve a specific goal. This could involve promoting content, increasing visibility for a post, or supporting an account's growth. The main objective of a RAID is to generate a significant impact, often across multiple interactions, with the collective actions of all participants contributing to the raid's success.

Raids can be created for unique campaigns, such as boosting a particular post, gaining more followers, etc.

[](#what-are-tasks-interactions)

What are Tasks/Interactions?


------------------------------------------------------------------

A **TASK/Interaction** refers to the individual actions each participant takes within a RAID. These tasks are the micro-level interactions that drive the success of the RAID. Common tasks include liking a post, retweeting, replying to a tweet, or following an account. Each completed task contributes directly to the goal of the RAID.

Tasks are essential for completing a RAID, as they represent the individual actions that help achieve the larger goal. Your ability to perform tasks/interactions and how many within a RAID is limited by your asset holdings + Level. When a Raid is fully completed you will receive XP + Reward. Your reward will be pooled and will be held until raid completion is verified.

• You'll earn XP for every task/interaction you complete. Completing all required tasks within a Raid and successfully completing the entire Raid will yield a reward. Task completion is incredibly efficient since you don't need to leave the site; everything can be accomplished seamlessly through Raiders Guild's integrated social media channels.

# Create Raid | Raiders Guild (RG)

* * *

[PreviousRaids/Tasks](/raids-tasks)
[NextRaid Participation](/raids-tasks/raid-participation)

Last updated 2 months ago

### 

[](#the-raid-creation-process-is-open-to-everyone-regardless-of-your-community-or-asset-holdings.-whethe)

The **RAID creation** process is open to everyone, regardless of your community or asset holdings. Whether you're new or experienced, you can create a RAID and invite others to join in on the action.

**There's no restriction on who can participate — anyone is welcome to engage in Raids, regardless of whether you own Mallard Order assets or not.**

However, owning **Mallard Order assets** comes with exclusive benefits. Specifically, asset holders can enjoy **discounted rates** when creating Raids. This means that if you own Mallard Ducks, Relics, or Invitation Letters, you can access special pricing when setting up your Raid.

[](#heres-how-it-works)

Here’s how it works:


-------------------------------------------------

Start by clicking the "Create Raid" button or head to [https://app.raidersguild.io/raids/create](https://app.raidersguild.io/raids/create)

1.  **Raid Creation**: You’ll provide key details like the Raid name, description, and the number of raiders allowed to join, The social media platform to Raid and the Link to the post.
    
2.  **Participation**: Once the Raid is created, anyone can join, ensuring your content or campaign gets the engagement it needs.
    
3.  **Discount**: If you’re an asset holder, you’ll automatically benefit from a discount when creating your Raid.
    

It's important to note that you are responsible for researching and ensuring the validity of the tasks you create. Tasks that do not meet platform approval may be deleted.

[](#raid-costs)

Raid Costs


-------------------------------

Each raid action (like, repost, comment, follow) comes with a different cost, which is determined by the amount of engagement or number of raiders you want to participate in the activity. The minimum number of raiders for each raid is set at 50 raiders. Here's how it works:

*   Like: The cost for having a like action performed by 50 raiders will differ from that of other functions based on the engagement level desired.
    
*   Repost: Reposting comes with a different cost, which factors in the number of shares requested.
    
*   Comment: Commenting will have a variable cost based on the number of commentaries needed to increase engagement.
    
*   Follow: The cost for each follow action is also variable and depends on how many followers are targeted.
    

These variable costs are based on the level of engagement you want for your post. More raiders and higher engagement levels result in higher costs but greater reach and interaction.

By structuring raid costs this way, the platform ensures that rewards are appropriately distributed, assets holders are incentivized, and the platform can sustain its ongoing growth while promoting active community participation.

[](#raid-pooled-rewards)

Raid Pooled Rewards


-------------------------------------------------

As a **Raid Creator**, it’s important to understand how your funds are allocated when you pay for a Raid. The total amount you spend goes through a defined distribution process that ensures fairness for both participants and platform development. Here's how the funds are split each time you create a Raid:

**1\. 70% to Raiders' Rewards Pool**

The largest portion of your payment, **70%**, is directed to the **Raiders' Rewards Pool**. This pool is **evenly distributed among all participants** in the Raid, based on the number of raiders involved. The amount each participant receives depends on the total number of raiders, but **all participants share equally**. The reward pool is calculated based on the **creator’s budget**, the number of participants, and the tasks they engage in.

**2\. 10% to Revenue Share for Asset Holders**

**10%** of the total Raid payment is allocated to holders of **Mallard Order assets** (such as Mallard Ducks, Relics, and Invitation Letters). These holders receive a share of the Raid funds gradually, ensuring that long-term supporters benefit from their holdings. The more assets someone holds, the more gradual rewards they receive from their assets.

**3\. 18% to Platform Share**

**18%** of your Raid payment goes to the **Platform Share**, which is used to cover operational costs, ongoing development, and system improvements. This portion helps to maintain platform performance and ensure that the system is evolving with new features, enhancing the user experience. Impact on Distribution: When a discount is applied, the amount deducted comes from the Platform Share, while the Raiders’ Rewards Pool and Revenue Share for asset holders remain unaffected. This allows you to reduce your costs as a creator without negatively impacting the rewards for participants or asset holders.

**4\. 2% Developer Fee**

A **2%** fee is allocated to the **developer wallet**. This fee supports the team responsible for maintaining and developing the platform, including bug fixes, system upgrades, and new feature implementations.

[](#finalizing-your-raid-creation)

**Finalizing Your Raid Creation**


-------------------------------------------------------------------------

Once you have completed setting up your Raid, you will be prompted to **confirm your Raid details**. At this stage, you will be directed to the **Draft Page**, where you can make any final adjustments before moving forward.

### 

[](#heres-what-you-can-do-on-the-draft-page)

Here’s what you can do on the Draft Page:

*   **Review All Details**: Double-check the **Raid Name**, **Description**, number of participants, and selected platform features to ensure everything is accurate.
    
*   **Adjust the Budget**: If necessary, you can modify the number of raiders and the associated budget to align with your goals.
    

Once you’ve finalized the details on the Draft Page, simply **press the "Approve Transaction" button** to complete the process.

As soon as the transaction is approved:

*   Your Raid will be **instantly added to the Active Raids list**, making it visible to participants.
    
*   Raiders can begin engaging with your posts based on the selected tasks (like, retweet, reply, etc.).
    
*   The system will track participation and interactions automatically.
    

This seamless process ensures your Raid goes live without delays, allowing you to start gaining traction and engagement immediately.

Select any Active Raid to view its details, including the available interactions/tasks. If your social account isn't connected, you will be prompted to connect it before proceeding. Each action you complete—such as liking, reposting, commenting, or following—counts as **one interaction**. The number of interactions you can perform daily is determined by your **NFT holdings** and **level**, and there are specific daily limits in place.

For example, **non-holders** are limited to **2 interactions per day**.

This provides full transparency, allowing you to assess the success of your Raid and understand the value generated through participant engagement.

Your overall rewards are always visible on the Navigation Bar, your Account Page, or the Home Page. The more Raids you complete the more rewards you accrue!

# Experience Points | Raiders Guild (RG)

* * *

[](#xp-per-raid-function)

XP per Raid Function


---------------------------------------------------

Each action in a Raid earns you a specific amount of XP. Below is the breakdown of XP rewards based on different interactions (Following details are for X/Twitter):

*   **Like:** 10 XP
    
*   **Comment:** 35 XP
    
*   **Repost:** 15 XP
    
*   **Follow:** 25 XP
    

### 

[](#xp-requirements-per-level)

XP Requirements per Level

To level up in the Raiders Guild, you'll need to accumulate a certain amount of XP for each level. The required XP increases as you progress through the levels:

*   **Level 1 to 2:** 1500 XP
    
*   **Level 2 to 3:** 1800 XP
    
*   **Level 3 to 4:** 2160 XP
    
*   **Level 4 to 5:** 2592 XP
    
*   **Level 5 to 6:** 3110 XP
    
*   **Level 6 to 7:** 3732 XP
    
*   **Level 7 to 8:** 4478 XP
    
*   **Level 8 to 9:** 5374 XP
    
*   **Level 9 to 10:** 6449 XP
    

As you continue to level up, the XP requirements increase, offering more challenges and rewards.

[](#rank-milestone-utility-unlocks)

Rank Milestone Utility Unlocks


-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Progress through levels by accumulating XP to unlock valuable rewards and additional utility. Each milestone provides increasing benefits, giving both holders and non-holders opportunities to engage further within the platform.

**Level 1**

*   No additional rewards at this stage.
    

**Level 2**

*   **Unlock 1 Additional Task/Day**: Increase your daily interactions and earn more XP by completing an extra task each day.
    

**Level 3**

*   **Unlock 1 New Task/Day**: Gain access to a new interaction type (e.g., Follow, Like, Comment, or Repost) to diversify your tasks and boost engagement.
    

**Levels 4-6**

*   **Unlock +1 Additional Task/Day**: Gradually increase your daily task limit with each milestone, allowing you to complete more interactions and earn more XP.
    

**Levels 7-8**

*   **Unlock +2 Additional Tasks/Day**: Expand your capacity for tasks significantly, helping you climb the ranks faster and maximize rewards.
    

**Level 9**

*   **Unlock +3 Additional Tasks/Day**: A significant boost to your daily tasks, providing more opportunities to engage and increase your XP.
    

**Level 10**

*   **Unlock +4 Additional Tasks/Day**: At the final level, gain the maximum task bonus, allowing a total of **4 extra tasks per day**, greatly increasing your earning potential and XP accumulation.

Raiders Guild now offers benefits for all Mallard Order assets, enhancing both participation in and creation of raids. Enjoy exclusive perks whether you're engaging in raids or creating your own!

**Don't have a Mallard Order NFT? No problem!** You can still access Raiders Guild and enjoy full functionality as a non-holder. However, you may miss out on key benefits, including the ability to complete more raids, shorter reward withdrawal time limits, and discounts on raid creation.

[](#xp-boost)

**XP Boost**


-------------------------------

XP boosts in Raiders Guild are designed to reward asset holders with increased XP for participating in raids. Here’s how it works:

*   **Mallard Ducks**: Each Mallard Duck you own gives you a 2% XP bonus. The more ducks you have, the higher your XP earnings from completing tasks.
    
*   **Relic Sets**: Holding a Relic Set provides a 1.5% XP bonus per full set. The more sets you collect, the more you’ll boost your XP rewards.
    
*   **Invitation Letters**: Each Invitation Letter grants a 5% XP bonus, allowing you to level up faster when participating in raids.
    

These XP bonuses stack, helping asset holders progress more quickly, earn more rewards, and unlock additional benefits within Raiders Guild. **Up to a maximum of 40% XP boost per user, based on all their assets combined.**

[](#raid-creation-discounts)

Raid Creation Discounts


---------------------------------------------------------

Raiders Guild offers discounts based on your holdings of Mallard Order assets. The more assets you own, the greater the discount you can receive when creating raids. Here's how the discounts break down:

**Mallard Ducks:**

*   2% discount per duck.
    

**Relic Sets:**

*   1% discount per relic set.
    

**Invitation Letters:**

*   4% discount per invitation letter.
    

**Total Discount Cap:**

The maximum discount you can receive is 10% when combining Mallard Ducks, Relic Sets, and Invitation Letters.

These discounts will automatically apply when creating a raid, allowing you to save based on your asset holdings.
