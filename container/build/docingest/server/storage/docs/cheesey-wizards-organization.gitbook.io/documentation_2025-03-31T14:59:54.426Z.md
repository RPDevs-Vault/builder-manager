LOOPING/ POV STEAL / REPEATING PHRASES/ GENERAL WEIRDNESS TIPS

so... _you tried lowering your temperature to 0.4_, didn't work, now you're stuck. sad, but true. r1 is annoying. happens to the best of us. here's some possible tips that could help get your sanity back.

**FIRST AND FOREMOST: keep your context window small, maximum 16k.** why?

with a big context window, R1 has too much to consider and it will glaze over your custom prompt. Here is a beautiful schema made by grinch

1\. Adjust2\. Paragraph Length3\. Change your message4\. OOC5\. Fact check6\. New chat

_deleting the old, keeping the good_

for this, you MUST either generate a roleplay summary in Chat Memory (with Auto Summary) OR [keep track of events by using a Chat Memory template like this](/masterlist/prompts-and-troubleshooting/general-advice#chat-memory)
. After you've done that, follow this.

**Steps, in order:**

1.  **Lower** context size to **~ 300**. Yes, you've heard right. This is to check for this:
    

The perma token count is **3598\. The formula is:** Limited Prompt + Persona Details(UserAppearance) + Char Personality + Chat Memory + Scenario + Custom Prompt = **x tokens. We can also see my last message was 404 tokens.**

Phew, okay. So what does this all mean? _**It means Deepseek has a lot to think about and it's glazing over your Custom Prompt**_. It's okay, we'll give it a break.

1.  **Up your context to the last few tokens.** In this case, I would increase it to ~ **4000 tokens.**
    
2.  **Hard Delete your message.** Copy it, delete it and send it again for a reset. _you can always raise back context when the issue is fixed, and keep it at_ _**6k-10k**__._
    

_stop the yapping_

1.  **LOWER** temp to 0.4. **(OpenRouter only. Official API DOES NOT support temp on Janitor.)**
    

3.  **ALTERNATE OPTION:** add `Keep replies to a MAXIMUM of 4 paragraphs.` in **Chat Memory**.
    

_it's not you, it's me_

**you may have ended your reply with TOO much focus on yourself, and r1 is trying to continue YOUR message. Write some more!**

**e.g1** The scene seems to have ended, and the last sentence of your reply is this:

_{{user}} sighed, hanging up the phone, eyes scanning the room for anything that would remind them of {{char}}. "Damn, I'm such an idiot. I made everything about myself."_

_R1 will try to continue off you._ **Make it so it involves {{char}} a little more actively.**

_{{user}} sighed, hanging up the phone, eyes scanning the room for anything that would remind them of {{char}}. But who else could satisfy that but {{char}} themselves? It's been too long moping, it was time to seize the moment. {{user}} stands and drives over to {{char}}, ready to continue this roleplay with their pov intact._

**e.g2** Your reply was too short.

_{{user}} nods. "Oh, that's crazy. For real?"_

R1 has nothing to work with. :(

_{{user}}'s eyes widen, taken aback by the new information they've just gathered._ _**Too short? I never thought– didn't even consider...**_ _"I'm sorry, {{char}}. All this time and I was blind to such a thing," {{user}} sighs, reaching over to clasp {{char}}'s hand into their own. "I promise to be better. I promise I will never let my pov be stolen ever again."_

_aka let's spray the AI with a water bottle method_

**example prompts:**

*   `Ensure correct pov is maintained after next response generation.`
    
*   `Avoid narrating for {{user}}. Focus your pov on {{char}}.`
    
*   `Avoid outputting narration for {{user}} at all costs.`
    

_assert dominance over r1_

**this is generally good advice for when you believe r1 has forgotten something or has gotten confused (looking at you, NSFW scenes). Prompt OOC**

*   `Recall API rules.`
    
*   `Pop quiz: tell me the narrative rules given.`
    

**START A NEW CHAT.** yep, that's all i've got. you just need to start anew.

# wtf is deepseek r1? | Saturnine's Deepseek Masterlist

> "The study of reasoning model draws its inspiration from the observation that reasoning is one of the main intelligent activities of human beings, and of the ways by which human thinking comes from one idea to a related idea." – [Science Direct](https://www.sciencedirect.com/topics/computer-science/reasoning-model#:~:text=In%20computer%20science%2C%20a%20reasoning,to%20represent%20and%20approximate%20reasoning.)

ty, smart person.

Deepseek-R1 is a reasoning model (unlike GPT-4o, Sonnet3.5, JLLM, etc. When it thinks about your prompt and instruction, this is its thought process (relative):

1.  First, I should set the scene...
    
2.  Next, sensory details...
    
3.  I need to show their internal state…
    
4.  Ending with a poignant image...
    
5.  Let me check if I'm covering all elements...
    

\[More information in this article: [DeepSeek R1 vs V3 on datacamp.com](https://www.datacamp.com/blog/deepseek-r1-vs-v3)\
\]

The result:

*   thinking output(because of CoT= _Chain of Thinking,_ **aka: "Okay, let me think about this..."**)
    
*   less flexible approach— it loves code (e.g JSON, YAML, XML– coding languages), which is why well-structured prompts work incredibly well for it
    
*   stricter adherence to API— you need to be specific about exactly WHAT and HOW you want it to act.
    
*   however, when it understands you, it cooks a full-course meal
    

For more troubleshooting and setup tips, [here is a very good rentry for you.](https://rentry.org/ai-models/)
