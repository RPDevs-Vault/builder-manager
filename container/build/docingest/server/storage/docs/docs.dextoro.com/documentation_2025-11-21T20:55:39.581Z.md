Overview

dextoro Launch is the institutional-grade tokenization and capital formation platform built for founders, businesses, and investors who are serious about building in Web3.

Built on Solana and deeply integrated into the broader dextoro ecosystem, dextoro Launch enables any company — whether a Web2 startup, established business, or Web3 project — to raise capital, issue tokens, and access liquid secondary markets with zero technical overhead.

Unlike traditional launchpads using static presale models, dextoro Launch uses a **dynamic bonding curve engine** to create fair, liquid, and programmable token launches. This shifts token distribution from speculation-driven hype to transparent, market-driven price discovery.

🪂 Legacy DTR Airdrop

As part of dextoro’s token relaunch, we’re honoring our early supporters from the **original $DTR token** with a structured airdrop of the new **$DTR token** on Solana.

This process ensures that legacy holders are rewarded fairly, while maintaining a balanced, deflationary, and investor-ready new token economy.

#### 

[](https://docs.dextoro.com/usddtr/legacy-usddtr-airdrop#purpose-of-the-airdrop)

Purpose of the Airdrop

The Legacy $DTR Airdrop exists to:

*   **Reward early community members** who believed in DexToro from the beginning
    
*   **Transition the dextoro ecosystem** from the old $DTR contract to the new $DTR token, which is deployed on the newly improved Solana-based Token-2022 Program
    
*   **Preserve value** while correcting the flaws of the previous “fair launch” model (no vesting, no supply controls)
    
*   **Protect investors** through structured, transparent distribution and capped supply
    

#### 

[](https://docs.dextoro.com/usddtr/legacy-usddtr-airdrop#snapshot-and-eligibility)

Snapshot & Eligibility

*   **Snapshot Date:** A fixed snapshot will record the official **conversion price** based on the **90-day Volume-Weighted Average Price (VWAP)**.
    
*   **Eligibility:** Anyone holding the original $DTR token and who follows the [claim process](https://docs.dextoro.com/usddtr/legacy-usddtr-airdrop#claim-process)
     will be eligible to claim the new $DTR.
    
*   **Conversion Rate:** New tokens will be issued based on the **USD value** of your legacy $DTR at the time of the snapshot, which uses a 9**0-day (VWAP)** for fairness.
    

> 📘 _Example:_ If your old $DTR holdings were worth $1,000 at the snapshot, you’ll receive $1,000 worth of the new $DTR tokens, valued at the launch price.

Why It’s Structured This Way

The original dextoro (legacy) launch suffered from the classic fair-launch issues:

*   Early accumulation at ultra-low prices
    
*   Rapid dumping post-launch
    
*   No vesting or investor protections
    

The **new airdrop plan fixes this** by introducing:

*   A **3-month linear vesting** for fair distribution
    
*   A **hard supply cap** to prevent dilution
    
*   A **claim-based redemption** that verifies true holders
    

Together, these measures make the relaunch more equitable, deflationary, and sustainable.

[](https://docs.dextoro.com/usddtr/legacy-usddtr-airdrop#airdrop-and-ecosystem-impact)

*   **Legacy holders** gain exposure to the improved $DTR model
    
*   **Buy pressure increases** as new tokens are distributed over time
    
*   **Reduced circulating supply** through vesting and capped issuance
    
*   **Ecosystem stability** is strengthened ahead of listings and staking
    

The result: a healthier launch, long-term growth, and a unified dextoro community moving forward together.

Legacy DTR Valuation & Conversion Basis

#### 

[](https://docs.dextoro.com/usddtr/legacy-usddtr-airdrop/legacy-usddtr-snapshot#overview)

Overview

To ensure transparency and fairness for all legacy token holders, the valuation and conversion rate between the **legacy $DTR** and the **new $DTR** token are determined using a **90-day Volume-Weighted Average Price (VWAP)**.

This approach aligns dextoro’s migration process with industry-standard financial practices used by major exchanges, launchpads, and institutional investors.

#### 

[](https://docs.dextoro.com/usddtr/legacy-usddtr-airdrop/legacy-usddtr-snapshot#why-vwap-not-simple-average)

Why VWAP (Not Simple Average)

While both 60- and 90-day averages were analyzed, dextoro chose the **90-day VWAP** because it provides a **truer reflection of actual market activity**. VWAP weights each day by its trading volume — meaning days with higher trading activity influence the result proportionally more than thinly traded days.

> **VWAP = (∑ Price × Volume) ÷ ∑ Volume** _(Volume-weighted mean price over the observed period)_

This ensures that the conversion rate reflects **the real market consensus** — not just a flat average of daily prices. It’s a more sophisticated, data-driven method that investors can independently verify using open market data.
