# Unknown

Trading Fees The following terms apply to all markets on the exchange, apart from specific products listed below, which have their own fee schedule. Trading fees are only charged for orders that are immediately matched with orders sitting on the orderbook. Trading fees are not charged for orders placed that are not immediately matched and are instead left as resting orders on the orderbook unless they are included in our “Maker Fees” section.
