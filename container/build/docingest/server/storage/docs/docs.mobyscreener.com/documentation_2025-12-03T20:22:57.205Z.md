1.  **Invite friends:** Share your unique referral link.
    
2.  **They trade:** Every time your referrals make trades on Moby, you earn.
    
3.  **You get paid:** Rewards are credited directly to your account in real time.
    

### 

[](https://docs.mobyscreener.com/getting-started/rewards-program/publish-your-docs#tiers-and-commissions)

Tiers and Commissions

Our referral system is tiered so you can earn from your direct invites and from their network.

As your referrals invite others, you unlock additional earning layers.

**Tiers:**

**Level 1:** **30%** of the platform fees generated by your referrals

**Level 2: 3%** of the platform fees they generate

**Level 3:** **2%** of the platform fees they generate

**Example**: You invite Alice (Level 1). She invites Bob (Level 2). Bob invites Carol (Level 3).

Whenever Alice, Bob, or Carol trades, you earn a percentage of the platform fees.

Your rewards scale as your network grows.

*   Everyone starts at **10%** $MOBY-back.

*   Tiers never reset, so your progress is locked in.

Moby is built to be the definitive hub for discovering, analyzing, and trading every on-chain asset—memecoins, prediction contracts, utility tokens, RWAs, and whatever comes next. As capital and data move on-chain, blockchains become the richest source of market intelligence. [](https://docs.mobyscreener.com/getting-started/usdmoby#flywheel)

Flywheel


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Our goal is to make $MOBY the quinessential definition of an Internet Capital Markets token. To this, we continue to roll out for features to deeply intertwine $MOBY with our Moby products. As our Moby products see more success → these network effects should reflect in the token.

Our tokenomics consist of two ever-growing parts, **Buyback Mechanisms** and **Holding Mechanisms**.

### 

[](https://docs.mobyscreener.com/getting-started/usdmoby#buyback-mechanisms)

Buyback Mechanisms

Fees generated used to buy back $MOBY off the market:

*   **50% of swap fees** → from all Moby products go towards buying back $MOBY.
    
*   **100% of banner and Pro upgrade fees** → on MobyScreener.com go towards buying back $MOBY (DexScreener made $50 million+ off of this business model in 2024)
    

### 

[](https://docs.mobyscreener.com/getting-started/usdmoby#holding-mechanisms)

Holding Mechanisms

Hold **$MOBY** to unlock:

*   **Trending (5m & 30m)** — early momentum views. **Req:** 10,000 $MOBY
    
*   **Active + Global DCAs** — near real-time accumulation/selling. **Req:** 15,000 $MOBY
    
*   **50% Off Token Upgrades** — Pro boosts & custom banners. **Req:** 20,000 $MOBY
    

_$MOBY is a fair-launched coin, deployed initially on Pump Fun, with 100% of the supply circulating._

_The Holding Requirements to access the Premium features above will roll out later this year._

📖 Full spec: $MOBY [Whitepaper](https://odd-chopper-fec.notion.site/MOBY-Whitepaper-20a2df75803e80e5af6edd22d8efb1b5)

[](https://docs.mobyscreener.com/getting-started/usdmoby#flywheel-effects)

Flywheel Effects


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Buyback Mechanism Flywheel:**

1.  **Better product** \-> more users
    
2.  **More users** → more trading volume, upgrades, and fees
    
3.  **More fees** → more $MOBY buybacks.
    
4.  **Better product ->** more users
    

**Holding Mechanism Flywheel:**

1.  **Better product** \-> more users
    
2.  **More users** → more demand to hold $MOBY to unlock features
    
3.  **More holding** → less available supply
    
4.  **Better product ->** more user
    

We will continue to integrate flywheels that deeply intertwine our Moby products and $MOBY.
