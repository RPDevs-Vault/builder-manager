### Swing Traders

For more serious traders who act upon shorter term opportunities in the market, trading with the 4H timeframe is a must-have.  
  
Statistically speaking, M.A.E.V.E our AI trader has never lost and has the highest average trade PNL on the 4H timeframe, along with having the smallest drawdown from entry when compared to other timeframes. The 4H trades don't react as often to the market as the smaller timeframes, but their accuracy for swing trading is unmatched.
