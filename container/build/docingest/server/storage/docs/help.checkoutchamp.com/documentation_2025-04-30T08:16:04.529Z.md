To edit the funnel’s settings, open the funnel within Checkout Champ and then click the **gear** button on the top left of the funnel visualizer

**Name**: The internal name used to identify the funnel.

**Title**: The title of your page will appear on the browser tab when you are previewing your pages and when your Funnel is published.

**Favicon**: You can customize the icon on your Funnel's browser tab by uploading a Favicon. Favicons must be uploaded in .ico format. There are many free available tools which will convert your existing images into .ico files. Once you have loaded specified a Favicon, the visitors to your site will be able to see this on their browser tab.

**Logo**: You can place your company logo into this section and it will be applied throughout the funnel, preventing you from needing to upload the logo onto each page. The uploaded logo will replace any image on the page containing the “fk-logo” css class. The majority of our templates contain placeholder logos containing the “fk-logo” class.

**Campaign**: Specify which Checkout Champ CRM campaign products will be sold on your funnel. For more details on Checkout Champ CRM Campaign Management, see our help article here: [Campaigns](/crm/crm-setup/campaign-setup)

Creating a Funnel

On your Checkout Champ Dashboard, click **Create a Funnel**, to get started!

Now you will select your **Goal**, which is what do you plan to use the funnel for. The available options are:

*   Collect Emails -Build an email prospect list
    
*   Sell Products - Sell your products or services
    
*   Host Webinar - Showcase your next webinar.
    

**Collect Emails**

When you select **Collect Emails**, you will select the lead template you prefer

**Sell Products**

When you select **Sell Products**, you can select the **Type** of funnel you are building:

*   Sales Funnel: Sell your products or services
    
*   Product Launch: Sell your new product with video pages
    
*   Membership: Provide products or services with a monthly membership
    
*   Shopify: Route your Shopify cart to FunnelKonnekt for checkout
    

**Host Webinar**

When you select **Host Webinar**, you can select the **Type** of funnel you are building:

*   Live Webinar: Showcase your live webinar
    
*   Webinar Replay: Replay a webinar for lead generation.
    

There will be a selection of funnel templates. Choose which You may filter the types and categories at the top of the page.

When you have found the template you like, click the box and then click "Chose Templage".

Once you have selected the Funnel Template and the Funnel Type, you can **Name** and **Build** your funnel.

Alternatively, you can go to your Funnels list and click the “Add A New Funnel” option on the left side of this page.

# Style Manager | CheckoutChamp

Last updated 7 months ago

Select Style Manager to quickly reveal the style management pane. Edit an element’s style.

**Settings** - Each element has different settings

**Dimension** - Adjust the margins and padding of the element

**Font** - Adjust the font type, color, size, etc

**Border** - Adjust the border for the element

**Shadow** - Apply a shadow to the element

**Background** - Control the background style of the element

**Extra** - Adjust the opacity of an element

**Flex** \- Control flex properties of the element

Within Checkout Champ we provide a number of templates for each page type. Many people get bogged down in design, so hopefully these will save you much time! We recommend you use our templates for several reasons:

*   They will save you time as designing your own page from scratch can take some time to perfect.
    
*   Templates are easy to edit and designed to work in the Checkout Champ platform.
    
*   They provide great examples of the design possibilities within Checkout Champ.
    
*   They have been tested and approved for use.
    
*   Superior support for templates compared to custom pages.
    

_Don't see a template that fits your needs?_ **Let us know!**

We are always designing more based on feedback!

### 

[](#pagetemplates-selectingatemplate)

Selecting a Template

When you drag a new page type onto the visualizer, you will need to select a template first. Do this by selecting the page settings cog icon.

From here you can select a template to edit, or select the first blank template (in red below) if you want to build your page from scratch.

Once you select a template you can edit the images and text to fit your needs. Templates are designed to need minimal editing. Just replace our info with yours and you’re ready to start selling!
