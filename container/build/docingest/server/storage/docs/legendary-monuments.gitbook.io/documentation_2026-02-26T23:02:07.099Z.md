# Zapdos | Legendary Monuments Wiki

![Page cover](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252F4gwhMYRrg91w25imKKRj%252Fzapdos.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D5dbadcb4-996b-4800-ad03-489713efce8b&width=1248&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=21cd1667&sv=2)

To obtain **Zapdos** you need to craft a "[**Urn of Storms**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/other-items-mechanics-blocks/urns-and-galarian-urns)
" and defeat 50 electric-type pokemon, either by using melee or through pokemon battle, whilst you have the urn in your inventory. (You can't fill multiple urns at the same time).

Once the urn is filled all you need to do is right-click with it in your hand and a level 40 **Zapdos** will spawn, with a 2% chance of it being a shiny.

You will also receive a "Zap Stone" upon using the filled urn, which you'll be able to put together with the other 2 bird stones to craft the "Vortex Stone" to obtain [**Lugia**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-2/lugia)
.

To obtain the Galarian Form simply use a "[**Galarian Urn**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/other-items-mechanics-blocks/urns-and-galarian-urns)
" instead and make sure you have a pack that adds the Galarian Forms such as AllTheMons, otherwise it will spawn a Galarian bird but with Kantonian model.

# Mew | Legendary Monuments Wiki

![Page cover](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252FNYc7oYvTdvzy9TKs5n9q%252Fmew.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D4127d8e5-9f16-49a2-8e8a-c80b16b74ce1&width=1248&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=26e90526&sv=2)

To obtain **Mew** you need to find an "Old Sea Map", which has a 50% of being found in Jungle Temple chests. (note: Old Sea Map does not function as a Minecraft map)

Once you have the map, you need to find the [**Final Island**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/structures/overworld-structures/final-island)
 (you can track it using the [**Arc Phone**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/other-items-mechanics-blocks/arc-phone)
) and place the map at the Mew Pedestal and a level 40 **Mew** will spawn, with a 2% chance of it being a shiny.

You will also receive a "Tuft of Mew Hair" which is currently has no function.

# Suicune | Legendary Monuments Wiki

![Page cover](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252F3TjXDIgbUJWaKrWudlHW%252Fsuicune.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D2a42ed96-9b8b-449c-a4ff-0eb45c021302&width=1248&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=91e6c275&sv=2)

To obtain **Suicune** you need to craft a "Suicune Treat", for which you will require multiple cobblemon berries and a "Poketreat Box" which the [**Entrepreneur Villager**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/other-items-mechanics-blocks/entrepreneur-villager)
 sometimes sells.

Once you have the treat, you need to find the [**Burned Tower**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/structures/overworld-structures/burned-tower)
 (you can track it using the [**Arc Phone**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/other-items-mechanics-blocks/arc-phone)
) and place the treat at the Suicune Pedestal and a level 40 **Suicune** will spawn, with a 2% chance of it being a shiny.

You will also receive a "Blue Feather" upon using the pedestal, which you'll be able to put together with the other 2 feathers to craft the "Rainbow Feather" to obtain [**Ho-Oh**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-2/ho-oh)
.

# Regirock | Legendary Monuments Wiki

![Page cover](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252F29KwTxz00khEltisBYM5%252Fregirock.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D0adc57f4-4ec0-4e2a-9dfb-42b369663172&width=1248&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=e2988733&sv=2)

To obtain **Regirock,** you need a "Rock Golem Key" craftable with 1 Rock Gem and 4 "Rock Golem Ingots". A batch of 4 "Rock Golem Ingots" is crafted using 1 Rock Gem and 4 "Golem Scraps" from furnace smelting "Ancient Rubble" found in caves at Deepslate level and below.

Once you have a "Rock Golem Key", you can unlock the "Regirock Lock" found in [**Snowpoint Temple**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/structures/overworld-structures/snowpoint-temple)
 (you can track it using the [**Arc Phone**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/other-items-mechanics-blocks/arc-phone)
). Behind the lock will be a trial where each block on the floor is lined with sand blocks and pressure plates. Right click the Note Block beside the entrance and the Redstone Lamps on the ceiling will light a path safe to walk on and any plate deviated from the path will fall to the water below with a ladder back at the entrance. Theres a ladder at the end of the path that leads to Regirock's summon room.

In the summon room, step on each circle to light it up. Once each circle is lit, a level 40 **Regirock** will spawn, with a 2% chance of it being a shiny.

You will also receive a "Regirock Tablet" upon lighting the floor which can be combined with the other 4 Tablets to craft the "Titan Key", so you can obtain [**Regigigas**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-4/regigigas)

# Regigigas | Legendary Monuments Wiki

To obtain **Regigigas** you must first summon [**Regirock**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-3/regirock)

In the summon room, step on each circle to light it up. Once each circle is lit, a level 70 **Regigigas** will spawn, with a 2% chance of it being a shiny.

You will also receive a "Titan Core" upon lighting the floor which you can use to craft the [**Titan Pauldron**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/other-items-mechanics-blocks/regi-pauldrons)
 or [**Titan Hammer**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/other-items-mechanics-blocks/titan-hammer)

To obtain **Meltan** you must craft a "Meltan Box" and place it on the floor, after that, you need to fill the box with a total of 50 Metal Value, with the possible items being:

*   Iron Ingot -> 2 points
    
*   Gold Ingot -> 3 points
    
*   Netherite Ingot -> 50 points
    

After that, just right-click the box and a level 5 **Meltan** will spawn, with a 2% chance of it being a shiny (The Box won't be consumed), after that to evolve your **Meltan** into **Melmetal**, thanks to Mega Showdown you must get another 75 Meltans and release them, which will grant you an item (x75) to evolve Meltan by giving it to him.

# Arc Phone | Legendary Monuments Wiki

The Arc Phone is a multi-purpose device with its main function being used to track Legendary Structures. Right click phone in hand to open its UI and using mouse to select different apps

To track Legendary Structures, open the tracking app and you will see a dropdown box that has a list of all structures the phone can track. Each structure requires a specific item (items can may be from Vanilla Minecraft, Mega Showdown, or Legendary Monuments) and if the item is not present in the player's inventory, it will be greyed out in the dropdown list, but you can still select the greyed out structure and it will briefly display the item needed to track that structure; once you have the required item, the structure name in the list will be white in text. When selecting an available structure, it will attempt to search for one in your current dimension (this my take a while as structures may spawn far away) and when found, it will display its coordintates, the distance, and the general Cartesianal direction of the structure from your location.

# Regieleki | Legendary Monuments Wiki

![Page cover](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252FJIqZXwTDTf8bXjsEhXiE%252Fregieleki.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D246b70a3-dd9d-473d-acac-548738a116ee&width=1248&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=43a20e7f&sv=2)

To obtain **Regieleki** you need either an "Electric Golem Key" or a "Dragon Golem Key". The "Electric Golem Key" is craftable with 1 Electric Gem and 4 " Golem Ingots" and batch of 4 "Electric Golem Ingots" is crafted using 1 Electric Gem and 4 "Golem Scraps" whereas the "Dragon Golem Key" is craftable with 1 Dragon Gem and 4 " Golem Ingots" and batch of 4 "Dragon Golem Ingots" is crafted using 1 Dragon Gem and 4 "Golem Scraps". "Golem Scraps" from furnace smelting "Ancient Rubble" found in caves at Deepslate level and below.

Once you have either an "Electric Golem Key" or a "Dragon Golem Key", you can unlock the "Elekidrago Lock" found [**Snowpoint Temple**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/structures/overworld-structures/snowpoint-temple)
 (you can track it using the [**Arc Phone**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/other-items-mechanics-blocks/arc-phone)
). Behind the lock will be a trial where you must rotate the four arrows in each of the colored item frames to match each of the arrows in the colored walls in each of the four corner crevices that you get to through a jumping obstacle course. Once each arrow matches, the wall between the middle two arrows will open the path that leads to Regieleki's and Regidrago's summon room.

In the summon room, step on each circle to light it up. Once each circle is lit, both a level 40 **Regieleki** and a level 40 [**Regidrago**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-8/regidrago)
 will spawn, with a 2% chance of each being a shiny.

You will also receive a "Regirock Tablet" which can be combined with the other 5 Tablets to craft the "Titan key", so you can obtain [**Regigigas**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-4/regigigas)
.

# Regi Pauldrons | Legendary Monuments Wiki

![Page cover](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252FwOm6DZrxmcHrZuT4OQMO%252Fwear.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D7c58690d-83e9-42ca-b823-01bf1d695342&width=1248&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=fd542f37&sv=2)

There are a total of 6 Regi Pauldrons:

*   Dragon Pauldron -> Gives +3 damage
    
*   Electric Pauldron -> Gives +20% speed
    
*   Rock Pauldron -> Gives the haste effect
    
*   Ice Pauldron -> Gives the luck effect
    
*   Steel Pauldron -> Gives +3 armor

# Regidrago | Legendary Monuments Wiki

![Page cover](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252Few0maiDBUNEaCsTUeuJM%252Fregidrago.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D489c2dfd-6258-40d8-94aa-76802acac1d2&width=1248&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=7f9164b7&sv=2)

To obtain **Regidrago** you need either an "Electric Golem Key" or a "Dragon Golem Key". The "Electric Golem Key" is craftable with 1 Electric Gem and 4 " Golem Ingots" and batch of 4 "Electric Golem Ingots" is crafted using 1 Electric Gem and 4 "Golem Scraps" whereas the "Dragon Golem Key" is craftable with 1 Dragon Gem and 4 " Golem Ingots" and batch of 4 "Dragon Golem Ingots" is crafted using 1 Dragon Gem and 4 "Golem Scraps". "Golem Scraps" from furnace smelting "Ancient Rubble" found in caves at Deepslate level and below.

Once you have either an "Electric Golem Key" or a "Dragon Golem Key", you can unlock the "Elekidrago Lock" found [**Snowpoint Temple**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/structures/overworld-structures/snowpoint-temple)
 (you can track it using the [**Arc Phone**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/other-items-mechanics-blocks/arc-phone)
). Behind the lock will be a trial where you must rotate the four arrows in each of the colored item frames to match each of the arrows in the colored walls in each of the four corner crevices that you get to through a jumping obstacle course. Once each arrow matches, the wall between the middle two arrows will open the path that leads to Regidrago's and Regieleki's summon room.

In the summon room, step on each circle to light it up. Once each circle is lit, both a level 40 **Regidrago** and a level 40 **Regieleki** will spawn, with a 2% chance of each being a shiny.

You will also receive a "Regirock Tablet" which can be combined with the other 5 Tablets to craft the "Titan key", so you can obtain [**Regigigas**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-4/regigigas)
.

# Does Legendary Monuments work with X Datapack or Mod? | Legendary Monuments Wiki

There are currently no known incompatible mods/datapacks with Legendary Monuments.

What you may find is a datapack or rarely a mod that adds a structure for a Legendary/Mythical that we also add, however this shouldn't cause issues besides having multiple structures for the same pokemon, something you can easily fix by deleting the specific structure files. If you do that, its recommended that you delete the other mod/datapack structure since LM has code connected to its structures and will most likely crash if any of them is removed, meaning if its possible, it will require advanced coding knowledge, since the mod isn't currently open-source.

# Southern Island | Legendary Monuments Wiki

![Page cover](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252FMBdFStUAntsNnyttY2tO%252Fsouthern.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D58e509ea-3c97-472e-9e2f-19827c178ded&width=1248&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=d8d023fe&sv=2)

The Southern Island is the structure where you can obtain either [**Latias**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-3/latias)
 or [**Latios**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-3/latios)
, where you will have to choose to either spawn one or the other, which means to get both, you must find another Island, there also 2 hidden chests within the island.

The Southern Island spawns in Deep Ocean Biomes, namely (The modded biomes are optional and not having said mods won't affecting anything in your game):

• #minecraft:is\_deep\_ocean • #c:is\_deep\_ocean • wythers:deep\_icy\_ocean

# Snowpoint Temple | Legendary Monuments Wiki

![Page cover](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252F9kVwk5dPeRZrrgslkML0%252Fdragoeleki.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dbd5fafcb-716e-4b7a-a86e-a1df6147cd2d&width=1248&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=51bc9d9b&sv=2)

The Snowpoint Temple is the structure where you can obtain all 5 Regis: [Regice](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-3/regice)
, [Regirock](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-3/regirock)
, [Registeel](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-3/registeel)
, [**Regidrago**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-8/regidrago)
**,** [**Regieleki**](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-8/regieleki)
, and [Regigigas](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/how-to-obtain-all-legendaries-mythicals/generation-4/regigigas)
 with each Regi locked behind a room.

The Snowpoint Temple spawns in Minecraft's Snow Plains biome
