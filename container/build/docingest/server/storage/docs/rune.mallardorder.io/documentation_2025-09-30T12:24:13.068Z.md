# RUNE V2 intro | RUNE Ecosystem

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   Raiders Guild Economy RUNE V2 is the currency for creating and finalizing Raids, with a portion of all platform fees allocated toward buybacks and burns. Users of our social fi platform Raiders guild and soon to be a modular game are able to farm Rune V2 all the while being tasked with promoting the platform.

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/social-fi#what-is-raiders-guild)

What is Raiders Guild?

Raiders Guild is our flagship social engagement platform where users complete "raids" - social tasks like Twitter engagement, Discord participation, and community challenges. The platform rewards active participation with Rune Points that convert directly to RUNE V2 tokens. With the V2 launch, Raiders Guild becomes fully integrated with RUNE V2 - offering massively discounted raid creation rates for token holders while sharing 30% of all platform fees back to token stakers. It's social-fi that actually pays you to engage. Unlike gambling platforms or zero-sum memecoin speculation, Raiders Guild creates genuine value for all participants. Raid creators invest in marketing spend to get real engagement, brand awareness, and community growth. Participants earn meaningful rewards for their time and genuine social activity. It's a positive-sum ecosystem where creators get measurable marketing results, participants get compensated for their efforts, and the platform facilitates mutually beneficial exchanges - no one loses for others to win

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/social-fi#how-rune-v2-powers-raiders-guild)

How RUNE V2 Powers Raiders Guild

https://app.raidersguild.io

All economic actions on Raiders Guild require RUNE V2, ensuring continuous demand:

*   Create Raids – Pay fees in RUNE V2 to launch custom quests and competitions.
    
*   Join Raids – Raids reward participants with RUNE V2, prize pool access, and future earning boosts.
    
*   Boost Visibility – Pay extra RUNE V2 to advertise on RG.
    

This design keeps the token flowing through the system rather than sitting idle, while fees are routed to benefit token holders. **RUNE V2 allows for raids to be created at a discounted rate vs using $ADA.** Any raid creation, participation, or leaderboard entry on Raiders Guild can be accomplished using RUNE V2.

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/social-fi#fee-redistribution-to-token-holders)

Fee Redistribution to Token Holders

*   30% of all platform fees is pooled and airdropped back to RUNE V2 holders.
    

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/social-fi#weighting-formula)

Weighting formula:

*   Token balance = baseline weight (the more RUNE V2 held, the higher the share).
    
*   Mallard ecosystem assets (Ducks, Letters, Relics, Comic# 2) provide a multiplier bonus for calculating yield (% boost per tier of asset holdings).
    

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/social-fi#platform-burn-mechanic)

# Rewards | RUNE Ecosystem

RUNE V2 staking provides access to four distinct revenue streams that compound together to create substantial yield opportunities. Unlike traditional staking that offers only token emissions, RUNE V2 stakers capture value from every profitable activity within the Mallard Order ecosystem.

![](https://rune.mallardorder.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3729992950-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252Fu4VMFSzPDBzeg7itqDCl%252Fuploads%252F7GkusABACM5V37JRYyOm%252F7.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D7c8e59b6-7d06-4e12-ada9-a364ecc7085e&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=5c714798&sv=2)

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/rewards#revenue-stream-breakdown)

Revenue Stream Breakdown


---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/rewards#token-staking-pool)

\- Token Staking Pool

**18% allocation (99.9M RUNE V2) distributed over 18 months**

The primary reward mechanism distributes tokens according to a front-loaded emission curve, with 50% of rewards distributed in the first six months. This creates exceptionally high yields for early stakers while the total staked supply remains low.

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/rewards#treasury-profit-sharing)

\- Treasury Profit Sharing

**10-20% of treasury profits paid monthly in ADA**

The treasury actively deploys capital through AI-driven trading strategies, protocol-owned liquidity, and yield farming across multiple protocols. Monthly profits are distributed proportionally to stakers based on their weighted stake amount and duration.

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/rewards#raiders-guild-platform-revenue)

\- Raiders Guild Platform Revenue

**30% of platform fees distributed in RUNE V2**

All economic activity on Raiders Guild generates fees that are partially redistributed to RUNE V2 stakers. As platform adoption grows, this revenue stream provides increasing returns paid in additional RUNE V2 tokens.

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/rewards#print-shop-business-profits)

\- Print Shop Business Profits

**15% of Print Shop profits paid in ADA**

The existing Print Shop business generates consistent revenue from physical merchandise and services. A portion of these profits flows directly to RUNE V2 stakers, providing income independent of crypto market conditions.

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/rewards#protocol-revenue)

**\- Protocol Revenue**

DEX fees and other platform activities contribute to the revenue pool

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/getting-started/rewards#staking-duration-impact)

Staking Duration Impact

Your chosen staking duration applies a multiplier to ALL four revenue streams simultaneously

# Burn Pit | RUNE Ecosystem

The Asset Burn Pool offers holders of specific ecosystem assets an alternative to traditional holding or trading: **permanent destruction in exchange for immediate RUNE V2 tokens**. This mechanism provides instant liquidity while creating deflationary pressure that benefits remaining asset holders.

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/staking/burn-pit#why-burn-instead-of-hold)

Why Burn Instead of Hold?

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/staking/burn-pit#immediate-liquidity)

Immediate Liquidity

Rather than waiting for buyers on secondary markets, burning provides guaranteed, instant conversion to RUNE V2 tokens that can be immediately staked or traded.

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/staking/burn-pit#guaranteed-rates)

Guaranteed Rates

Burn values are fixed and transparent, eliminating price uncertainty and market timing concerns that affect traditional NFT sales.

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/staking/burn-pit#strategic-flexibility)

Strategic Flexibility

Convert static collectibles into active utility tokens that generate ongoing rewards through RUNE V2 staking and revenue sharing.

### 

[](https://rune.mallardorder.io/staking/burn-pit#deflationary-benefits)

Deflationary Benefits

Every burned asset is permanently removed from circulation, increasing scarcity and potential value for remaining holders of the same asset types.

*   **Base rewards** go to **RUNE V2 token stakers** based on how much they lock and for how long.
    
*   **Mallard asset holders** who stake RUNE V2 **get additional bonuses** through trait-based yield boosts (not from a separate supply, but by gaining a larger share of this emission pool).
    
*   **If you don’t stake RUNE V2 tokens**, you don’t get emissions assets alone won’t qualify for this. Same with the boost rewards from Mallard Order NFT's
