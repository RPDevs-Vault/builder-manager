# Market Intelligence | AIRAA

Market Intelligence tracks unique signals across both social platforms and onchain activity to help users understand what is happening in crypto and why. Instead of raw feeds of data, it highlights events that carry meaningful context.

Currently, this is divided into three views:

*   **Breakouts**: Identifies projects that have recently seen a sharp increase in attention. The system connects this rise to specific reasons drawn from community discussions.
    
*   **Sentiment Shifts**: Surfaces when sentiment around a project changes direction, together with the conversations that triggered the move.
    
*   **Alphas**: Highlights newer or smaller projects that are gaining traction among smart accounts and showing signs of consistent progress.
    

The purpose is to reduce noise and give users a structured way to follow narratives, sentiment, and adoption signals without needing to manually piece together information from multiple platforms.

# Aura Boards | AIRAA

The Aura Boards are campaign specific leaderboard that ranks contributors based on their actions across socials and onchain. Each campaign has its own Aura Board, with scoring rules defined by the partner project running it.

The Aura Board serves two purposes:

*   **For contributors**: it shows how actions translate into measurable influence and where they stand compared to others.
    
*   **For projects**: it provides a transparent way to evaluate community engagement and distribute rewards fairly.
    

Interested in running your own campaign? Reachout to us [here](https://t.me/lightwaslost?text=hey%20light%2C%20would%20love%20to%20learn%20more%20about%airaa%campaigns)
.

#### 

[](https://airaa.gitbook.io/docs/products/editor#how-aura-is-calculated)

How Aura is Calculated

Aura is currently built from three data sources: **X, Discord, and onchain activity**. The final scoring weights and criteria are fully modular, allowing each project to decide which actions matter most. This creates a unified leaderboard that reflects contributions across platforms rather than isolating them.

#### 

[](https://airaa.gitbook.io/docs/products/editor#key-components-of-the-aura-board)

Key Components of the Aura Board

*   **Socials**: shows which platforms a contributor is active on
    
*   **Multiplier**: displays the boost earned from on-chain usage or other campaign-defined actions
    
*   **Aura %**: the contributor’s share of total campaign aura
    
*   **Connections**: highlights the contributor’s links to other community members

# Why Airaa exists? | AIRAA

Crypto communities are noisy and fragmented. Narratives move from Telegram chat, spread on X, get debated on Discord, and only later show up onchain. By then, most teams are already late.

Today, data is scattered across platforms. Analysts spend hours exporting CSVs, scraping messages, and switching between dashboards. Instead of answers, teams are left with dozens of non-curated data sources spread across random platforms. Each shows only a slice of the picture, which makes the problem worse instead of solving it.

Airaa exists to solve this problem. Airaa brings data from your favourite platforms in one place, making it possible to track narratives, research faster, and act on data backed signals.

![](https://airaa.gitbook.io/docs/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F238999426-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252Fdmwne6oUXEkyAPUnE5DZ%252Fuploads%252F0MOfxgSd9Czoxgrrv7wv%252FScreenshot%25202025-08-28%2520at%252012.59.01%25E2%2580%25AFAM.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D90c10b5f-bae1-4bfc-8d62-8b8e26a5f7a1&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=f753ecec&sv=2)

# Aura Boards | AIRAA

The Aura Boards are campaign specific leaderboard that ranks contributors based on their actions across socials and onchain. Each campaign has its own Aura Board, with scoring rules defined by the partner project running it.

The Aura Board serves two purposes:

*   **For contributors**: it shows how actions translate into measurable influence and where they stand compared to others.
    
*   **For projects**: it provides a transparent way to evaluate community engagement and distribute rewards fairly.
    

Interested in running your own campaign? Reachout to us [here](https://t.me/lightwaslost?text=hey%20light%2C%20would%20love%20to%20learn%20more%20about%airaa%campaigns)
.

#### 

[](https://airaa.gitbook.io/docs/products#how-aura-is-calculated)

How Aura is Calculated

Aura is currently built from three data sources: **X, Discord, and onchain activity**. The final scoring weights and criteria are fully modular, allowing each project to decide which actions matter most. This creates a unified leaderboard that reflects contributions across platforms rather than isolating them.

#### 

[](https://airaa.gitbook.io/docs/products#key-components-of-the-aura-board)

Key Components of the Aura Board

*   **Socials**: shows which platforms a contributor is active on
    
*   **Multiplier**: displays the boost earned from on-chain usage or other campaign-defined actions
    
*   **Aura %**: the contributor’s share of total campaign aura
    
*   **Connections**: highlights the contributor’s links to other community members
