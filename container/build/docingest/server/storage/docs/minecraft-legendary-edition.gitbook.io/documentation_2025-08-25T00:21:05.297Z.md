# The Stronghold | Minecraft Legendary Edition

#### 

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/the-quest-for-the-eyes/legendary-quest/the-stronghold#you-can-locate-the-stronghold-using-any-eye-from-the-legendary-quest-normal-eyes-of-ender-will-not-k)

You can locate The Stronghold using any eye from the Legendary Quest; normal Eyes of Ender will not know the way, nor will they be useful to open the portal. The End Frames of the portal might already have some normal Eyes of Ender placed on them. This doesn’t mean that you can place Eyes of Ender in the frame, but these actually do help in opening the portal.

This also means that you might not need to collect 12 out of the 16 eyes to open the portal to the End. Additionally, the loot at the location of these eyes is the best you will find during your adventure.

# Nether & End | Minecraft Legendary Edition

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/temperature/nether-and-end#survival-on-the-nether)

Survival on the Nether


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Nether reaches temperatures much higher than 35.0°C, meaning that you will constantly receive damage by Heat Stroke if you come here unprepared.

To survive the temperatures of the Nether, combine the effects of Cold Food and Cold Drink and make sure to get high tiers of these effects such as Cold Food III and Cold Drink III. Craft a full Diamond armor that has natural heat reistance and reforge its leggins and chesplate to Masterful, this quality tier will grand +1°C of thermal resistance on each piece for a total of +2°C of thermal resistance, fnally apply cooling coat.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/temperature/nether-and-end#survival-on-the-end)

Survival on the End


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The End drops the temperature below 0°C, meaning that you will constantly receive damage by hypothermia if you come here unprepared.

To survive the freezing temperatures of the Nether, combine the effects of Hot Food and Hot Drink and make sure to get high tiers of these effects like Hot Food III and Hot Drink III. Craft a full Netherite armor set that has natural cold resistance, coat it and reforge it to masterful.

Now that you can brew potions thanks to the materials obtained in the nether you can brew Cold Resistance Potions.

Chests and crates from the **@Lootr** mod have a distinct appearance compared to vanilla versions. Lootr crates feature a **"?"** symbol, while Lootr chests have **yellow edges**. These visual differences indicate that the containers generate random loot upon opening.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/loot-and-luck#loot-generation)

Loot Generation


---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The loot within Lootr chests and crates comes from a manually created loot pool. This means that even if a structure's loot is randomly generated, the contents will vary between different structures. Additionally, loot found in **mythical dungeons** is often of higher quality

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/loot-and-luck#mutiplayer-loot-access)

Mutiplayer Loot Access


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Lootr chests and crates can be opened by multiple players, with each player receiving their own unique loot. This makes them ideal for multiplayer gameplay.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/loot-and-luck#breaking-lootr-chest-and-crates)

Breaking Lootr Chest and Crates


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

These special containers **cannot** be broken using standard methods. To break a Lootr chest or crate, you must **sneak and break** it.

* * *

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/loot-and-luck#how-to-get-better-loot)

How to Get Better Loot


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Improving your Luck will result in slighty better loot inside of these chests and crates, you can brew luck potions or craft a Jade Amulet.

# How to Cook | Minecraft Legendary Edition

Craft and place a Crock Pot, then add any fuel—such as coal, wood, or logs—to its fuel slot. Next, choose a recipe from JEI by searching @CrockPot , next fill the 4 slots for food with the respective mix of ingredients and wait until your food is ready.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/cooking-and-farming/crockpot/how-to-cook#how-to-read-a-recipe)

How to Read a Recipe


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Recipes from Crockpot will ask for various ingredients, these can be a specific Ingredient or any Ingredient from some category.

A recipe must reach a certain requirement of Food Value and this must be met for every Food Category in the recipe, these instructions will use symbols such as "greater than" (>), "less than" (<), and "less than or equal to" (≤) to easily show the requirements.

# Quality Equipment | Minecraft Legendary Edition

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/upgrades/quality-equipment#what-are-qualities)

What are Qualities


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Tools and armors can have a modifier called quality, these modifiers are different between items and can be benefitial, have drawbacks or be completely unfavourable for you.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/upgrades/quality-equipment#reforging-station)

Reforging Station


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Reforging Station from the mod @Quality Equipment will allow you to reforge using the repair item of the tool or armor that you would use to reapir it on an anvil, this is usually the main material used to craft the tool or armor.

### 

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/upgrades/quality-equipment#pixie-dust)

Pixie Dust

You can also reforge anything using Pixie Dust.

#### 

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/upgrades/quality-equipment#how-to-get-pixie-dust)

How to get Pixie Dust

Pixies may drop pixie dust when they die, but a better approach is to capture them inside jars ( use the jar from the mod:@Ice and Fire Blocks), when place a jar with a pixie inside it will drop pixie dust over time.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/upgrades/quality-equipment#masterful-quality)

Masterful Quality


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The Masterful Quality is exclusive of Armor and can be applied only to leggins and chestplates, besides giving the best stats it will 1.0°C of Thermal Resistance. This is especially useful to travel to the Nether or the End as Thermal Resistance protects you from the Heat and the Cold at the same time, you can have 2.0°C of Thermal Resistance if you have this quality on your chestplate and your leggins.

# Relics | Minecraft Legendary Edition

Relics are powerful Baubles, each one of them with unique abilities and randomized stats with an unique leveling system that will upgrade the Relic's stats and unlock more abilities of the Relic.

# What are Baubles | Minecraft Legendary Edition

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/baubles/what-are-baubles#baubles)

Baubles


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Baubles are powerful items that can be equipped in your slots to acquire special effects and benefits. Some can be used as normal items with powerful or useful capabilities.

You can equip Baubles in your inventory, with each requiring a specific slot. However, some baubles can be equipped in multiple different slots. A player has several available slots, including one for the head, necklace, back, body, bracelet, charm, spellbook, trinket, and an extra slot. Additionally, there are two slots each for hands, rings, and feet.

There are two major mods that add baubles: @Artifacts and @Relics. You can find artifacts by killing mimics underground and relics by looting chests. Unlike artifacts, relics have a complex leveling-up system integrated into them. Because of this, we will **divide** the explanation into two different sections: one for @Artifacts and another for @Relics.

# Creatures | Minecraft Legendary Edition

List of mount-less creatures

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/creatures#gorgons)

Gorgons


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Found in temples, a gorgon will turn anything that looks into their eyes to stone. You can avoid this by crafting and wearing a blindfold, a Gorgon will drop its eat that will allow to turn monsters into stone. A Gorgon head can only be used once

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/creatures#cyclopes)

Cyclopes


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Found around the world, the Cyclope will hunt anything that see as a potential threat. You can shoot an arrow to its eye to blind the monster and then attack it with your other weapons, if you do not do this the Cyclop will put you from the ground and eat you...

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/creatures#trolls)

Trolls


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Trolls can be found undergorund in caverns, they hunt by wielding stone pillars,tree logs and rudimentary axes, these weapons can sometimes be dropped by the troll on death but most importantly, all variant of trolls drop tusks and leathery hides that can be used to craft an armor that bestows 75% reduction of projectile damage.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/creatures#pixie)

Pixie


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Pixies are usually peaceful creatures, they will sometimes steal items from people who are not staring directly at them. Pixies produce pixie dust that can be used to reforge items or craft ambrosia, you can harvest it in large amounts by killing a pixie or by entrapping a pixie in a jar. Jarred Pixies produce a single pixie dust every three days.

Alternatively, if you wish to befriend a pixie you can drop cake down near pixies, having a pixie as a friend will make them follow you and give you bonus potion effects, depending on the color of the pixie.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/creatures#sirens)

Sirens


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Sirens will hypnotize any adventurer that hear their singing, they will be lead to their doom by slowly coming closer to the sirens, to avoid this craft and wear earplugs.

If you kill a siren, you can use their siny scales to brew water breathing potions.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/creatures#death-worms)

Death Worms


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

They can be found in desserts, with variants of diverse longitudes reaching up to a maximum of 20 block longs in the most rare cases, rushing from the underground to attack their prey, they are deadly dangerouns.

If you manage to kill one of these beasts, you can use its chitin to craft armor with extra toughness, killing a Death Worm might drop an egg, these "tamed" death worms cannot be controled but they will refuse to attack their owner and will roam around a 50 blocks radius the place where they were born.

You might be able to mount and commant a tamed or wild worm by using two fishing poles.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/creatures#myrmex)

Myrmex


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Myrmex can be found in the jungle, upon death myrmex drop myrmex chitin that can be used to craft extremely strong weapons and armor, a myrmex stinger can be used to create poisonous swords that are more powerful than normal myrmex weapon, additionally myrmex resin chunks can also be dropped by myrmex of all caste.

### 

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/creatures#myrmex-resin-chunks)

# Dragons | Minecraft Legendary Edition

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/dragons#how-to-get-a-dragon-egg)

How to Get a Dragon Egg


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Dragon eggs can only be obtained by raiding the lair of a Stage 5 female dragon, these can only be found undeground meaning that all dragons on the surface are not guarding eggs. When you kill one of these dragons and explore their loot, you might be able to find eggs.

### 

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/dragons#hatch-a-dragon-egg)

Hatch a Dragon Egg

### 

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/dragons#fire-dragon-eggs)

Fire Dragon Eggs

Caracterized by a look of fiery-earthly color, fire dragon's eggs can be hatched by putting on a netherack block and lighting the block underneat on fire using flint & steel, this will make the egg on the block move, in three days you will have a dragon.

### 

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/dragons#ice-dragon-eggs)

Ice Dragon Eggs

Caracterized by a look of white-blueish color, ice dragon's eggs can be hatched by sumerging them underwater. This will make the egg naturally form ice around it, in three days you will have a dragon.

### 

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/dragons#lighting-dragon-eggs)

Lighting Dragon Eggs

Caracterized by a dark-purpleish color, ice dragon's eggs can be hatched by putting them under the open sky and waiting for rain, this is possibly the hardest dragon to hatch as it will depend on the weater.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/dragons#how-to-grow-a-dragon)

How to Grow a Dragon


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Mounts | Minecraft Legendary Edition

There are multiple tameable creatures that will allow you to fly, move fastly on water or that will grant you protection.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/mounts#hyppogryphs)

Hyppogryphs


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

They can be found in multiple biomes but they are attracted to hilly biomes no matter the local climate, they might attack you but if you drop Rabbit Feets near they might stop to take a bite, after eating some you will gain their trust. You need to put a saddle on a Hyppogryph to fly on them, you can also craft an special armor for them as well as to put a chest on them.

Hyppogryphs can be made to sit by interacting with them wilst holding a stick.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/mounts#hyppocampi)

Hyppocampi


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A very passive creature that can be tamed with kelp and breed with prismarine crystals. You can ride them on water and underwater grantind water breathing. Hippocampi can be made to sit by interacting with them wilst holding a stick.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/mounts#cockatrices)

Cockatrices


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Cocatrices are aggresive creatures,to tame a wild Cockatrice you will need to trap it and continously stare at it taking breaks in between to avoid your death, alternatively a chicken might lay rotten eggs that have a chance of spawning a Cockatrice when trown making the trower the owner of the cockatrice.

Cockatrices will protect chickens in their general area, except if hirt by their owner, a cockatrice will also protect their owner.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/ice-and-fire/mounts#amphitheres)

Amphitheres


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

They are passive creatures found in jungles, to tame one you will need to shoot them with a bow and ride them, while you try to tame an amphiteres they will try to attack you, if you somehow manage to break the creature it will now belong to you. They can be breed with cookies.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You can find scrolls in chest across the world, these scrolls will belong to a school, either Blood Spells,Ender,Evocation,Fire,Divine,Ice or Nature, there is also a hidden school called the Eldritch School that you will be able to access in the future. They require mana to be used and will have a cooldown before you can use the same scroll, scrolls can only be used one time before be consumed.

You can see a extensive list of all Scrolls/Spells in the mod here: [https://iron.wiki/1\_19/spells/](https://iron.wiki/1_19/spells/)

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/iron-spells-n-dungeons/scrolls-and-spellbooks#inscription-table-and-spellbooks)

Inscription Table & Spellbooks


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Some Spellbooks alredy come with some spells but to add spells to your tome you will need to combine a tome with the respective spell in an inscription table, this will bind the scroll to the tome turning it into a spell, you can use it infinitely after a small cooldown.

An extensive list of all spellbooks and their unique benefits can be found here: [https://iron.wiki/1\_19/spellbooks/](https://iron.wiki/1_19/spellbooks/)

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/iron-spells-n-dungeons/scrolls-and-spellbooks#scroll-forge)

Scroll Forge


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Used to craft scrolls, the ink used determines the rarity of the spell. You can adquire this ink by finding it on chest, especially chest from structures of Iron Spells 'N dungeons and by killing mobs from this same mod, most of them will drop ink being epic and then legendary the highest of tiers. You cannot use an ink that is less rare than the minimum rarity of the scroll you wish to craft, you can check this rarity on JEI.

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/iron-spells-n-dungeons/scrolls-and-spellbooks#arcane-anbil)

Arcane Anbil


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Used to upgrade scrolls, upgrade equipment, and imbue weapons

[](https://minecraft-legendary-edition.gitbook.io/minecraft-legendary-edition/iron-spells-n-dungeons/scrolls-and-spellbooks#alchemist-cauldron)

Alchemist Cauldron


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Can be used to recycle scrolls and receive the rarity of its ink in return, upgrade ink, craft elixirs like mana potions and brew other potions.
