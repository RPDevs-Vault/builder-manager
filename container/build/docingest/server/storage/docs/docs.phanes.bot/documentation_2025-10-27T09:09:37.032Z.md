Phanes


-----------------------------------------------------------

Can I buy and sell coins through Phanes?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#can-i-buy-and-sell-coins-through-phanes)

No, Phanes is not a trading bot.

Why is Phanes not working in my Group?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#why-is-phanes-not-working-in-my-group)

1.  Phanes has been added to a Group, not a Channel.
    
2.  Phanes has been promoted to admin with sufficient permissions.
    
3.  Your Group has not been blacklisted. You can verify this by sending the `/start` command.
    
4.  Be aware that Phanes has limited functionality with certain [Group commands](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/commands#group-commands)
    , such as: `/lbonly`, `/contracts`, `/cashtag`
    

Is Phanes listening to other bots?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#is-phanes-listening-to-other-bots)

No, Phanes only responds to human input and does not interact with other bots. Please note that automated queries are not permitted and may result in a ban.

I am only interested in Phanes Leaderboard, not the detailed price queries.[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#i-am-only-interested-in-phanes-leaderboard-not-the-detailed-price-queries)

You are able to archive that with `/lbonly <on/off>`.

How can I show Phanes charts?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#how-can-i-show-phanes-charts)

By default chart images are turned off. To enable them type Telegram:`/chart on`.

How can I turn off X/Twitter link scraping in my Group?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#how-can-i-turn-off-x-twitter-link-scraping-in-my-group)

By default scraping X/Twitter links is enabled. To disable it type `/xfix off`.

Can I integrate Phanes using deep links?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#can-i-integrate-phanes-using-deep-links)

*   Price queries: `https://t.me/Phanes_bot?start=price_[contract]`
    

*   Top Holders: `https://t.me/Phanes_bot?start=th_[contract]`
    

*   Recycled X: `https://t.me/Phanes_bot?start=twitter_[username]`
    

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#leaderboard)

Leaderboard


---------------------------------------------------------------------

Why did my Calls suddenly disappear?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#why-did-my-calls-suddenly-disappear)

If your group calls have disappeared, it's likely because your group was automatically upgraded (by Telegram) to a [Supergroup](https://tginfo.me/groups_vs_supergroups-en/)
, resulting in a new group ID. Unfortunately, this change is irreversible, and existing calls cannot be transferred to the new group. We provide multiple warnings about this and always encourage users to upgrade their group proactively.

Why am I shown as "@GroupAnonymousBot" on the leaderboard?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#why-am-i-shown-as-groupanonymousbot-on-the-leaderboard)

Telegram offers an option for Admins to appear anonymously. For best functionality we recommend to turn that off. To do that follow these steps: _Go into your group settings » Administrators » Right click the user you want to edit » Edit admin rights » Toggle off "Remain anonymous"_. To find out more [click here](https://telegram.org/blog/filters-anonymous-admins-comments/de?setln=en#anonymous-group-admins)
.

Why am I shown as "None" or "Anonymous" on the leaderboard?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#why-am-i-shown-as-none-or-anonymous-on-the-leaderboard)

That's because you didn't set a Telegram Username. Please set one in your Telegram settings.

Why can't I access my user stats using `/stats`?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#why-cant-i-access-my-user-stats-using-stats)

Phanes always verifies that the user requesting statistics is a member of the group, to prevent unauthorized access. **To ensure this security measure works effectively, Phanes requires admin privileges in your group.**

Am I able to export the Leaderboard?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#am-i-able-to-export-the-leaderboard)

Yes, you are able to export the LB data to a CSV file. To find out more click [here](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/leaderboard#export-leaderboard)
.

Can I reset/modify the Leaderboard?[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/faq#can-i-reset-modify-the-leaderboard)

No, that is not possible.

# Rate Limits | Phanes Docs

To ensure fair usage and prevent abuse, Phanes implements rate limits.

*   Each user has a limit on how frequently they can use commands.
    
*   Some commands may have different rate limit rules.
    
*   For security reasons, we don't publicly disclose the exact rate limit thresholds. However, users engaging with Phanes in a normal manner will never encounter these limits.
    
*   It is crucial to read and understand our [Terms](https://phanes.bot/legal#terms)
    , especially Section 5 which contains usage restrictions.
    

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/rate-limits#adaptive-system)

Adaptive System


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Phanes uses an intelligent adaptive system that adjusts based on usage patterns:

*   Users who frequently hit rate limits may experience longer cooldown periods.
    
*   Repeated violations within a short time window will result in progressively longer timeouts.
    
*   The system automatically adjusts back to normal after a period of normal usage.
    

1.  Users who consistently trigger rate limits may be permanently restricted from using Phanes.
    
2.  Using any form of automation on Phanes may result in your account or group being banned.

# Scammer Detection | Phanes Docs

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/scammer-detection-system#overview)

Overview


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

An [automated scammer detection and protection system](https://dapp.phanes.bot/sds)
 for Telegram and Discord groups. It combines AI-powered analysis with trusted scam databases to quickly identify and manage malicious users and content.

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/scammer-detection-system#how-to-protect-your-group)

How to Protect your Group


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   [Add Phanes](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/add-phanes)
     to your group
    
*   Promote Phanes to Admin with at least the following permissions:
    
    1.  Ban users
        
    2.  Delete messages
        
    

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/scammer-detection-system#how-it-works)

How It Works


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The scammer detection system operates through:

1.  **AI-Powered Analysis:** Advanced machine learning algorithms to identify known scam techniques.
    
2.  **Known Scammer Database:** Automatically bans users who are already identified as scammers.
    
3.  **Pattern Recognition:** Detects suspicious messages containing malicious bot links, drainer websites, and common scam indicators.
    

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/scammer-detection-system#automatic-actions)

Automatic Actions


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When a scammer is detected, the system will:

*   Automatically attempt to ban the user.
    
*   Delete the suspicious message.
    
*   Send a warning notification to the group.
    

* * *

### 

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/overview/scammer-detection-system#reporting-scams)

Reporting Scams

**If you encounter a scam that wasn't automatically detected, please use** `/report` to report it immediately.

This helps improve our AI detection system and protect the community. The system continuously learns and adapts to new scam techniques while maintaining high performance.

# DApp | Phanes Docs

The [Phanes DApp](https://dapp.phanes.bot/)
 offers a sleek and intuitive platform for tracking and analyzing calls, seamlessly integrating with your group data to deliver instant updates and in-depth analytics.

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/dapp#how-to-access)

⭐️ How to Access


---------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   Access the Phanes DApp by typing `/dapp` in your group.
    
*   Access links are valid for 1 hour, and each logged-in session expires after 2 weeks.
    
*   You can enable / disable the DApp with `/dapp_active <on/off>`.
    

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/dapp#leaderboard)

🏆 Leaderboard


-----------------------------------------------------------------------

*   View all calls ranked by their performance
    
*   Track performance over different time periods (1 hour to 1 month)
    
*   Get detailed statistics like hit rates and average returns
    

### 

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/dapp#smart-filtering)

Smart Filtering

*   **Time Filters**: Choose from 1 hour, 6 hours, 12 hours, 1 day, 2 days, 3 days, 1 week, 2 weeks, or 1 month
    
*   **Blockchain Filters**: Focus on specific chains like Ethereum, Solana, BSC, etc.
    
*   **Caller Filters**: See calls from specific people or search by name
    
*   **Performance Filters**: Filter by return ranges (like "show me only 5x+ calls")
    
*   **Market Cap Filters**: Filter by coin market cap ranges
    

### 

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/dapp#charts)

Charts

1.  **Performance Charts**: View interactive charts showing how calls performed over time
    
2.  **Return Distribution**: See how returns are spread across different performance ranges
    
3.  **Success Rate Charts**: Track hit rates and success percentages over time
    
4.  **Caller Performance**: Compare different callers' performance with visual charts
    
5.  **Chain Analysis**: See which blockchains are performing best with pie charts and bar graphs
    
6.  **Market Cap Breakdown**: Analyze performance by different market cap ranges

⚡️ Core Performance Metrics


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/leaderboard#mvp)

**Top Callers**

The Top Callers system is based on the user rankings point system. It highlights the most successful callers based on their accumulated points from all calls within the specified timeframe. Points are awarded according to the [points system](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/leaderboard#points-system)
.

### 

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/leaderboard#hit-rate)

Hit Rate

The hit rate shows how consistently a caller finds tokens that achieve significant gains. It's calculated as the percentage of calls that reach at least 2x return. For example, a 40% hit rate means that 4 out of 10 calls reached or exceeded 2x return. This metric helps identify callers who consistently spot potential winners rather than those who might get lucky with one big call.

### 

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/leaderboard#average-return)

**Average Return**

The average return (mean) represents the typical performance across all calls. It's calculated by adding up all returns and dividing by the number of calls. While this gives a good overall performance indicator, it can be heavily influenced by extremely successful calls. For instance, one 100x call among many smaller returns will significantly boost the average, which might not represent the typical performance.

### 

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/leaderboard#median-return)

**Median Return**

The median return provides the middle value when all returns are ordered from lowest to highest. Unlike the average, it's not skewed by extremely high or low returns, making it often a better representation of typical performance. If a caller has a median return of 3x, it means half their calls performed better than 3x and half performed worse. This metric is particularly useful for understanding a caller's consistent performance level.

> _To learn more about median_ [_click here_](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/m/median.asp)
> _._

Together, these metrics provide a comprehensive view:

*   **Top Callers** shows relative performance ranking based on the point system.
    
*   **Hit Rate** shows consistency in finding winners.
    
*   **Average Return** shows overall performance including big wins.
    
*   **Median Return** shows typical performance excluding outliers.
    

Using all these metrics gives the most accurate picture of a caller's performance, as each metric compensates for the limitations of the others.

🖥️ Ranking System


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The user rankings are calculated using a point-based system that rewards or penalizes users based on the performance of their calls. Here's how points are assigned:

### 

[](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/leaderboard#how-it-works-1)

**How It Works**

*   Each user starts with 0 points
    
*   Points are accumulated for each call based on its performance
    
*   Poor calls result in negative points to penalize bad performance
    
*   Higher multipliers earn exponentially more points to reward exceptional calls
    
*   Final rankings are sorted from highest to lowest total points

🎮 Price Queries


----------------------------------------------------------------------

Phanes monitors all messages in chat and if a **ticker**, **token contract** or **LP address** is mentioned, it will trigger Phanes to spawn an embedded message.

🕹️ Commands

🔒 Security Tab

🐲 Emoji Guide

⭐️ Example

*   `$sol`
    
*   `$fartcoin sol` (specify the chain, can be anything like eth, sol...)
    
*   `0x6982508145454Ce325dDbE47a25d4ec3d2311933`
    
*   `https://dexscreener.com/ethereum/0xa43fe16908251ee70ef74718545e4fe6c5ccec9f`
    

*   Add a `.` **in front of the query** and Phanes will ignore it
    
*   Add a `.` **at the end of the query** and Phanes will send a trimmed version
    
*   Add a `,` **at the end of the query** and Phanes will send a detailed version
    

**Group Commands**

*   Turn charts on/off: `/show_charts on/off`
    
*   Turn $cashtags on/off: `/cashtag on/off`
    
*   Turn banners on/off: `/show_banner on/off`

🛡️ Group Commands


-------------------------------------------------------------------------

In Groups you are able to further fine-tune Phanes.

Command

Description

`/lang <lang>`

Set the bot’s interface language for your group.

`/admin_only <on/off>`

Only group admins are allowed to query prices (make calls).

`/dapp_active <on/off>`

Toggle the [Phanes DApp](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/dapp)
.

`/lbonly <on/off>`

Leaderboard only. **Every query will only be shown once!**

`/xfix <on/off>`

Toggle [X Link Scraper](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/fix-links#x-twitter)
.

`/xsimple <on/off>`

Use a simpler view ([FxTwitter](https://fxtwitter.com/)
).

`/xlang <lang>`

Change the language. Find out more [here](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/fix-links#translate-tweets)
.

`/tiktokfix <on/off>`

Toggle [TikTok Scraper](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/fix-links#tiktok)
.

`/shortca <on/off>`

Shorten CA queries.

`/compact <on/off>`

Compact queries, less noise. This can also be triggered by appending a `.`

`/detailed <on/off>`

Detailed queries, showing more info. This can also be triggered by appending a `,`

`/show_chart <on/off>`

Show chart images in queries.

`/show_buttons <on/off>`

Show buttons in queries.

`/show_banner <on/off>`

Show the dex paid banner in queries.

`/contract <on/off>`

Toggle if Phanes should listen to contract addresses.

`/cashtag <on/off>`

Toggle if Phanes should listen to `$cashtags`.

`/alert_x_return <on/off>`

Toggle X multiplier alerts ([boost required](https://docs.phanes.bot/premium/group-boost#x-multiplier-alerts)
).

`/alert_migration <on/off>`

Toggle migration alerts ([boost required](https://docs.phanes.bot/premium/group-boost#migration-alerts)
).

`/alert_dex_paid <on/off>`

Toggle dex paid alerts ([boost required](https://docs.phanes.bot/premium/group-boost#dex-paid-alerts)
).

`/alert_news <on/off>`

Toggle market moving news ([boost required](https://docs.phanes.bot/premium/group-boost#market-news-and-insights)
)

`/idle_tracker <on/off>`

Toggle idle tracker ([boost required](https://docs.phanes.bot/premium/group-boost#idle-tracker)
).

`/export_elb <on/off>`

Allow users to [export leaderboards](https://docs.phanes.bot/phanes/leaderboard#export-leaderboard)
.
