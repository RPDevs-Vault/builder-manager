# Yield Items | Harbor Finance

In Harbor Finance, Yield Items represent the core engine of the game’s reward mechanics. These items are tokenized as **ERC-721 NFTs** and take the form of fishers/Boats/Aquafarm Platform that autonomously generate $FISH over time. Each item type offers a unique trade-off between yield, duration, and slot efficiency, allowing players to craft their own strategic setup based on their Territory constraints and gameplay goals.

All Yield Items:

*   Can be **deployed** using Fuel
    
*   Are **upgradeable** up to **Level 5**
    
*   Can be **repaired** at the end of their lifecycle
    
*   Are restricted by **slot usage** within Territory Zones
    

Upgrades cost **10% of the item’s base price per level**, and increase yield differently depending on the item type.

# Upgrade System | Harbor Finance

All Yield Items in Harbor Finance can be upgraded from Level 1 to Level 5, with each level offering enhanced performance and better yield or ROI potential.

The upgrade system introduces a long-term strategy where players decide whether to compound their boats or reinvest their $FISH rewards to accelerate growth.

* * *

### 

[](#key-upgrade-principles)

Key Upgrade Principles:

*   Fixed upgrade cost: always based on the item's base price.
    
*   Yield or ROI scaling:
    
    *   Fisher: +2% yield per level
        
    *   Boat: +5.5% yield per level
        
    *   Platform: +10% ROI per level
        
    
*   No Fuel required for upgrades.
    
*   Upgrades affect future yields without resetting the current cycle.
    

* * *

### 

[](#mechanics)

Mechanics:

*   **Max Level:** 5
    
*   **Upgrade Cost:** 10% of the item's base price, paid in $FISH
    
*   **Fuel Cost:** None
    
*   **Progression:**
    
    *   Fisher: +2% yield per level
        
    *   Boat: +5.5% yield per level
        
    *   Platform: +10% ROI per level

**Overview & Management**

The Yield Items page gives you full visibility and control over all your active and inactive assets:

*   **Type & Status Filters:** Sort your Fishers, Boats, and Platforms by status (deployed, idle, damaged).
    
*   **Quick Stats:** Track how many items you own, how many are currently deployed, and how many require repairs.
    

Each item card includes:

*   **Daily Yield**: Your current output, based on the item’s level and territory boost.
    
*   **Repair Timer**: Time left before the item becomes inactive due to wear.
    
*   **Upgrade Cost**: The $FISH needed to increase the item’s level (and its yield).
    
*   **Status**: Shows where the item is staked or if it’s idle.
    
*   **Actions**: Instantly upgrade, repair, stake, or unstake any item.
    

* * *

#### 

[](#upgrades-and-repairs)

Upgrades & Repairs

*   **Upgrades** improve your item’s yield permanently by +10% per level.
    
*   **Repairs** are required after the item’s fixed lifespan ends. If not repaired, the item stops producing $FISH.
    

Staying on top of repairs and upgrades ensures your setup remains fully optimized and your daily production doesn’t stall.

* * *

#### 

[](#strategy-tip)

Strategy Tip

Balance your fuel, nets, and slot usage to ensure you’re getting the most out of every deployed item. Even one broken item can significantly drag down your total production.

* * *

[](#key-fuel-principles)

Key Fuel Principles:

*   Fuel is **consumed** for deploying Yield Items and claiming rewards.
    
*   Upgrades **do not consume Fuel** (paid only in $FISH).
    
*   Fuel **regenerates passively** over time at a steady rate.
    
*   Maximizing actions per Fuel regeneration cycle is a critical optimization layer.

Fuel Regeneration Protocol:

**Territories** are specialized NFTs that serve as the foundation of your operation. Think of them as fishing zones each one defines the space where you can deploy your yield-generating items and determines the overall efficiency of your setup.

*   Each territory provides a unique **yield boost**, increasing the performance of the items you place inside.
    
*   Territories also come with a **slot system**, limiting how many and which types of items you can deploy simultaneously.
    
*   The stronger the territory, the more it rewards you but also the more strategic your deployments must be.
    

Players are free to choose the territory that fits their goals, whether it’s low-risk entry or high-efficiency scaling.

**Yield Items** are the backbone of $FISH production. These are the assets you deploy into your territory to start generating rewards over time.

*   Each item has distinct characteristics: **cost**, **daily yield**, **slot usage**, and **upgrade potential**.
    
*   Items range from basic units (easy to acquire and deploy) to advanced structures (high investment, high return).
    
*   Items can be **repaired** and **upgraded**, allowing you to extend their lifespan and boost performance progressively.
    

Your strategy depends on how you compose your item setup across the available slots in your territory. A smart layout maximizes ROI while adapting to your fuel and claiming capacity.

**Fishing Nets** are consumable tools used to **claim your accumulated $FISH** from territories.

*   You’ll need a certain number of nets each time you want to claim rewards this requirement scales with the type of territory you own.
    
*   Nets are **burned** upon use and must be repurchased regularly.
    
*   The claiming system ensures that token emissions remain under control and adds a layer of planning for active players.
    

Claiming at the right time and with the right territory can greatly affect your net yield. Nets are not optional; they’re your only way to secure earned rewards.
