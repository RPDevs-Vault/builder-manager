# Quick Start Guide | Polygun Docs

circle-info

**PolyGun is optimized for speed and reliability out of the box.** Before trading, take a moment to review your execution settings so everything behaves exactly how you expect.

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/quick-start-guide#get-started-in-4-simple-steps)

Get started in 4 simple steps

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/quick-start-guide#id-1.-access-the-polygun-telegram-bot)

1\. Access the PolyGun Telegram Bot

Open the **Telegram bot link** and enter the chat.

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/quick-start-guide#id-2.-initialize-the-bot)

2\. Initialize the Bot

Tap **START**.

PolyGun will automatically create your secure smart wallet and set up your trading environment. No manual wallet linking or configuration required.

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/quick-start-guide#id-3.-review-your-settings)

3\. Review Your Settings

Before your first trade, check how PolyGun executes orders.

**Trading Modes** Choose how fast market orders execute:

*   **Cautious** – Always requires confirmation
    
*   **Standard** – Confirms only orders above $100
    
*   **Expert** – Executes instantly with no confirmation
    

Select the mode that fits your trading style.

**Manual Refresh** Use Refresh anytime to update prices, market data, and your portfolio.

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/quick-start-guide#id-4.-start-trading)

4\. Start Trading

You’re ready to trade.

*   Browse markets directly inside the bot
    
*   Paste any Polymarket link to trade instantly
    
*   Place Market or Limit orders
    
*   Cancel orders and monitor your PnL
    

All actions happen directly inside Telegram.

**You’re ready to trade.**

# FAQ | Polygun Docs

circle-info

**Got questions?** You’re not alone.

Common answers about deposits, fees, order execution, PnL behavior, and wallet security.

If something isn’t covered here, check the relevant feature section or reach out to the team.

* * *

chevron-rightCan I deposit Polymarket shares or tokens into PolyGun?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#can-i-deposit-polymarket-shares-or-tokens-into-polygun)

No. PolyGun does not support external deposits of market shares. All trades are executed directly through Polymarket via the bot.

chevron-rightI sent USDC but it’s not showing up. What should I do?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#i-sent-usdc-but-its-not-showing-up.-what-should-i-do)

First, tap **Refresh** in the bot. If the balance still doesn’t update after a few minutes, confirm the transfer was sent to your PolyGun wallet address and that the transaction is confirmed on-chain.

chevron-rightWhat fees does PolyGun charge?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#what-fees-does-polygun-charge)

PolyGun applies a **1% fee per executed trade**, in addition to Polymarket’s standard fees.

chevron-rightDoes PolyGun support Market and Limit orders?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#does-polygun-support-market-and-limit-orders)

Yes. PolyGun supports both **Market** and **Limit** orders for buying and selling positions.

chevron-rightWhy is my order asking for confirmation?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#why-is-my-order-asking-for-confirmation)

This depends on your **Trading Mode**:

*   **Cautious**: Every order requires confirmation
    
*   **Standard**: Orders above your set threshold require confirmation
    
*   **Expert**: Orders execute instantly with no confirmation
    

The default is **Standard**, but this can be adjusted anytime in the bot settings.

chevron-rightWhat is the Trading Threshold?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#what-is-the-trading-threshold)

The threshold is the dollar amount where PolyGun asks for confirmation in **Standard Mode**. The default is **$100**, but this can be adjusted anytime in the bot settings.

chevron-rightWhy did my trade fail?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#why-did-my-trade-fail)

Common reasons include:

*   Insufficient balance
    
*   Price moved beyond acceptable range
    
*   Market liquidity changed
    
*   Network congestion
    

Refreshing and retrying usually resolves it.

chevron-rightWhy does my PnL look different than expected?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#why-does-my-pnl-look-different-than-expected)

PnL is calculated based on:

*   Average entry price
    
*   Current market price
    
*   Fees incurred
    

PnL updates when you refresh or after trades complete.

chevron-rightI can’t see my position. Where did it go?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#i-cant-see-my-position.-where-did-it-go)

Check your **Closed** positions filter. Settled or sold positions are moved out of Open Positions automatically.

chevron-rightCan I export my wallet from PolyGun?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#can-i-export-my-wallet-from-polygun)

Yes. You can export your private key from **Settings → Wallet Security**.

⚠️ Once exported, wallet security becomes your responsibility.

chevron-rightDoes PolyGun hold my funds?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#does-polygun-hold-my-funds)

PolyGun creates a safe wallet for you, but you retain full control. Funds are not custodied by PolyGun.

chevron-rightHow do referrals work?[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/overview/faq#how-do-referrals-work)

**Earn trading fee rewards** when users you refer trade on PolyGun. Earnings are tracked automatically and paid directly to your PolyGun wallet.

Learn more in the [**Referrals**](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/referrals)
 section.

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/trading-on-the-bot/selling-positions#how-to-sell)

How to Sell

Selling positions on PolyGun is handled directly from your Portfolio. You can close positions instantly at market price or place limit sells at your preferred exit price.

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/trading-on-the-bot/selling-positions#id-1.-open-your-position)

1\. Open Your Position

To start selling:

*   Open **Portfolio** from the main menu
    
*   Select the market position you want to sell
    

Each position shows your size, average price, current price, and PnL.

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/trading-on-the-bot/selling-positions#id-2.-choose-your-sell-type)

2\. Choose Your Sell Type

Select how you want to exit the position:

*   **Sell** Executes a **Market Sell** at the best available price.
    
*   **Limit Sell** Places a **Limit Order** at your chosen price and waits for execution.
    

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/trading-on-the-bot/selling-positions#id-3.-select-amount)

3\. Select Amount

Choose how many shares to sell:

*   Use percentage buttons like **25%, 50%, 75%, or 100%**
    
*   Or enter a **custom share amount** manually
    

You’ll see the estimated value before confirming.

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/trading-on-the-bot/selling-positions#id-4.-confirm-or-auto-execute)

4\. Confirm or Auto-Execute

Your **Trading Mode** determines the behavior:

*   **Cautious** – Always asks for confirmation
    
*   **Standard** – Confirms only above your threshold
    
*   **Expert** – Executes instantly with no confirmation
    

Orders that meet your settings will fire immediately.

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/trading-on-the-bot/selling-positions#id-5.-transaction-updates)

5\. Transaction Updates

Once submitted, PolyGun will show:

*   When the sell order is submitted
    
*   When it completes
    
*   Shares sold
    
*   Average sell price
    
*   Amount received
    
*   Profit or loss
    
*   Transaction hash with a Polygonscan link
    

Your Portfolio updates automatically after completion.

* * *

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/trading-on-the-bot/selling-positions#good-to-know)

💡 Good to Know

*   Partial sells are supported
    
*   Limit sells remain open until filled or cancelled
    
*   Closed positions move to the **Closed** filter
    
*   Manual **Refresh** is available if needed
    

circle-info

**Tip:** Use **Limit Sell** to lock in exits without watching the market constantly.

*   Tap a **Quickbuy preset** for instant sizing

*   **Cautious** – Requires confirmation for every order
    
*   **Standard** – Confirms only orders above your set threshold
    
*   **Expert** – Executes instantly with no confirmation
    

Orders that meet your settings will fire immediately.

####

*   Shares received, average price, and total cost

All updates happen directly inside Telegram.

###

Default Threshold

*   **$100 USD**
    

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/trade-threshold#editing-your-threshold)

Editing Your Threshold

To update your threshold:

1.  Open **Settings** from the main menu
    
2.  Select **Threshold**
    
3.  Enter a new amount in USD
    

Changes apply immediately to future trades.

* * *

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/trade-threshold#what-threshold-does)

What Threshold Does

When **Standard mode** is enabled:

*   Trades **below** your threshold execute instantly
    
*   Trades **above** your threshold require manual confirmation
    

This lets you move quickly on small trades while staying cautious on larger ones.

* * *

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/trade-threshold#how-threshold-works-in-practice)

How Threshold Works in Practice

Example behavior with a $100 threshold:

*   Buying $25 or $50 → Executes immediately
    
*   Buying $150 or $300 → Requires confirmation before execution
    

* * *

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/trade-threshold#good-to-know)

💡 Good to Know

*   Threshold only applies in **Standard mode**
    
*   Cautious mode always requires confirmation
    
*   Expert mode ignores threshold entirely
    
*   Threshold affects **market orders only**
    

* * *

circle-info

**Tip:** Increase your threshold if you trade frequently, lower it if you want tighter control over larger positions.

# Trading Mode | Polygun Docs

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/trading-mode#available-modes)

Available Modes

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/trading-mode#cautious)

🛑 Cautious

*   Requires confirmation for **every** market order
    
*   Best for new users or large, deliberate trades
    
*   Prevents accidental buys or sells
    

Use this if you want maximum control before execution.

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/trading-mode#standard-recommended)

✅ Standard (Recommended)

*   Orders **above your set threshold** require confirmation
    
*   Smaller trades execute instantly
    
*   Default mode for most users
    

This mode balances safety and speed, making it ideal for everyday trading.

#### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/trading-mode#expert)

⚡ Expert

*   Executes market orders **instantly**
    
*   No confirmation prompts
    
*   Designed for speed-critical trades
    

Use this only if you are comfortable trading without confirmations.

* * *

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/trading-mode#how-trading-mode-works-in-practice)

How Trading Mode Works in Practice

When you place a **Market Buy** or **Market Sell**:

*   PolyGun checks your current Trading Mode
    
*   If confirmation is required, you’ll see a prompt
    
*   If not, the order fires immediately
    

* * *

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/settings/trading-mode#good-to-know)

💡 Good to Know

*   Trading Mode only affects **market orders**
    
*   You can switch modes at any time
    
*   Threshold settings work together with **Standard mode**
    
*   Expert mode is best paired with Quickbuy Presets for speed
    

circle-info

**Tip:** Start with **Standard**, then move to **Expert** once you’re comfortable with execution speed.

🤝 What is Copy Trading


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Copy trading allows you to follow another trader’s strategy in real time. When they open or close a position, PolyGun performs the same action for you using your allocated balance.

**This is ideal if:**

*   You want exposure without constant monitoring
    
*   You trust a trader’s decision making
    
*   You prefer a semi-passive trading setup

# Copy Trade Sizes | Polygun Docs

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-sizes#what-this-step-does)

🎯 What this step does


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Copy trade sizing controls the amount of money that goes into every copied position. It determines your exposure and risk level for each trade.

PolyGun gives you three sizing modes so you can match different trading styles and risk preferences.

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-sizes#available-sizing-modes)

⚙️ Available sizing modes


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-sizes#fixed-amount)

💵 Fixed Amount

You set a fixed USDC value for every copied trade.

**How it works:** No matter how big or small the trader’s position is, PolyGun always uses the same amount.

**Example:** If you set **10 USDC**, every copied trade will buy **10 USDC**, even if the trader buys 1,000 USDC.

Best for:

*   Strict budget control
    
*   Predictable exposure
    
*   Beginners who want simple sizing
    

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-sizes#percentage)

📊 Percentage

You copy a percentage of the trader’s position size.

**How it works:** Your trade size is proportional to the trader’s.

**Example:** If set to **50%** and the trader buys **100 USDC**, you buy **50 USDC**.

Best for:

*   Scaling with the trader
    
*   Following aggressive or conservative strategies
    
*   Keeping proportional exposure
    

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-sizes#portfolio-weighted)

📈 Portfolio-Weighted

You copy trades based on portfolio size instead of trade size.

**How it works:** If the trader uses a certain percentage of their portfolio, you use the same percentage of yours.

**Example:** If the trader uses **10% of their portfolio**, you also use **10% of your portfolio**, regardless of account size.

Best for:

*   Long-term copy trading
    
*   Risk-balanced setups
    
*   Matching the trader’s portfolio strategy
    

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-sizes#how-to-complete-this-step)

🛠 How to complete this step


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  **Choose one of the three options:**
    
    *   **Fixed**
        
    *   **Percentage**
        
    *   **Portfolio-Weighted**
        
    
2.  **Tap** your preferred mode
    
3.  **PolyGun will move you to the** [**next step**](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/max-copy-usd-amount-per-trade)
     to configure its values
    

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-sizes#important-notes)

⚠️ Important notes


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   This setting directly **affects your risk and capital usage**
    
*   **Choose conservatively** if you are new
    
*   Portfolio-Weighted **is the most advanced option**
    

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-sizes#good-to-know)

💡 Good to know


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   Fixed is the **safest and most predictable**
    
*   Percentage is the **most flexible**
    
*   Portfolio-Weighted is the **most professional setup**

# Slippage Tolerance | Polygun Docs

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/slippage-tolerance#what-this-step-does)

🎯 What this step does


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Slippage tolerance defines the maximum difference between the price you expect and the price your trade is actually filled at.

Markets can move fast. If the price shifts too much:

*   With low tolerance, the trade is skipped
    
*   With higher tolerance, the trade still goes through
    

This setting balances execution success vs price protection.

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/slippage-tolerance#available-options)

⚙️ Available options


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You can **choose a preset** percentage:

*   1%
    
*   2%
    
*   5%
    
*   10%
    

**Or type your own value:**

*   Example: 12 or 12%
    

**Special modes:**

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/slippage-tolerance#exact-price)

🎯 Exact Price

Only execute if the price is exactly what the trader entered. No slippage allowed. Safest, but trades may fail more often.

### 

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/slippage-tolerance#any-price)

⚡ Any Price

Execute regardless of price movement. Maximum flexibility. Highest risk.

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/slippage-tolerance#how-to-complete-this-step)

🛠 How to complete this step


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Choose **one of the preset percentages**, or
    
2.  **Type a custom percentage**, or
    
3.  Select **Exact Price** or **Any Price**
    

Once selected, PolyGun moves you to the [final step.](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/reviewing-your-copy-trade-settings)

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/slippage-tolerance#important-notes)

⚠️ Important notes


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   **Low slippage** = safer pricing, fewer filled trades
    
*   **High slippage** = more filled trades, worse pricing risk
    
*   **Exact Price** is strict and may miss many entries
    
*   **Any Price** removes price protection entirely
    

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/slippage-tolerance#good-to-know)

💡 Good to know


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   **Use lower slippage** in stable markets
    
*   **Use slightly higher slippage** in fast-moving or volatile markets
    
*   **Combine with price range** for best protection
    

circle-info

Good rule of thumb

*   **0.5%** Best for deep, liquid markets. Clean execution with minimal price impact.
    
*   **1%** More aggressive, but still solid for most markets. Good balance between fills and price quality.
    
*   **2% or more** Use when you’re trading bigger size or the market is thin
    

Just understand you’re accepting worse execution to make sure the trade goes through.

# Copy Trade Price Range | Polygun Docs

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-price-range#what-this-step-does)

🎯 What this step does


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The price range acts as a filter. PolyGun will only copy trades if the market price falls within your chosen range.

If the trader enters a trade outside this range, **PolyGun will skip it for your account.**

This helps you avoid:

*   Buying too high
    
*   Entering late during price spikes
    
*   Getting bad fills in fast markets
    

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-price-range#how-price-ranges-work)

⚙️ How price ranges work


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Prediction markets use prices like cents on the dollar, for example:

*   40c means $0.40
    
*   80c means $0.80
    

A range of **40c–80c** means:

*   Copy trades only when the price is between $0.40 and $0.80
    
*   Skip trades below or above that range
    

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-price-range#how-to-complete-this-step)

🛠 How to complete this step


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Choose a preset range:
    
    *   2c–98c
        
    *   5c–95c
        
    *   10c–90c
        
    *   30c–70c
        
    

**Or,**

1.  Type your own custom range in the chat Example:
    
    *   40c–80c
        
    

**Or,**

1.  Select **🚫 No Filter** to copy trades at any price
    

After choosing, PolyGun moves you to the [next step.](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/slippage-tolerance)

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-price-range#important-notes)

⚠️ Important notes


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   Narrower ranges = fewer trades, safer entries
    
*   Wider ranges = more trades, higher risk of bad pricing
    
*   “No Filter” removes all price protection
    
*   Price ranges do not affect position size, only entry eligibility
    

* * *

[hashtag](https://polygun.gitbook.io/polygun-docs/telegram-bot-user-guide/copy-trading/copy-trade-price-range#good-to-know)

💡 Good to know


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   Many users start with:
    
    *   **10c–90c or 30c–70c**
        
    
*   Tight ranges are **useful in volatile markets**
    
*   Wider ranges are better if you trust the trader’s timing
