Importing an Existing Wallet: Critical Information

If you choose to import an existing wallet instead of generating a new one, **please proceed with caution**. While the CABAL bot offers innovative trading tools and strategies, **we strongly advise against using your primary wallet for this purpose.**

**⚠️ Why You Shouldn’t Use Your Main Wallet**

*   **Telegram Security Risks**: If your Telegram account is compromised, an attacker could gain access to your imported wallet.
    
*   **CABAL Bot’s Responsibility**: While we provide the tools, **we cannot guarantee the security of your wallet in case of Telegram breaches.**
    

**✅ What You Should Do**

*   Use a **secondary wallet** specifically for trading with CABAL.
    
*   **Keep your primary wallet separate** for storing long-term investments or significant assets.
    
*   Always store your wallet's **secret phrase securely** and never share it with anyone.
    

**🚨 Key Reminder**

Once imported, the bot will not save your secret phrase, so ensure that you have securely backed it up. By using a secondary wallet, you minimize the risks associated with potential account breaches and keep your main assets safe.

Your wallet’s security starts with your choices. Make the smart move and protect your assets.

# Increased Speed | CabalBot Docs

When it comes to trading, speed matters. Here’s why our Telegram bot outpaces competitors with cutting-edge technological implementation:

**Blazingly Fast Backend Powered by Rust** The core backend of our bot is built in Rust, a low-level programming language renowned for its performance and efficiency. This enables the bot to execute complex operations at lightning speed, minimizing delays and maximizing responsiveness.

**Webhooks for Instant Response** Unlike most bots that rely on the slower polling method to interact with Telegram, we use Webhooks. Instead of waiting for Telegram to "ask" for updates, Webhooks allow our bot to proactively “inform” Telegram of actions. This approach eliminates unnecessary latency, providing near-instant response times for user commands.

**Load Balancing Across Five Regions** Our Telegram frontend interface is load-balanced across five global regions. This setup ensures that multiple bots are working seamlessly together within one main interface. What does this mean for you? You don’t need to switch to a region-specific bot – no matter where you are, the nearest node ensures optimal performance and lightning-fast execution.

With these technologies, we deliver an unparalleled trading experience in terms of speed and reliability. Whether you're placing trades or managing orders, you can trust that our bot will keep you ahead of the curve.

# 🥪 Auto MEV Protection | CabalBot Docs

Cabal ensures that all user transactions are shielded from sandwich attacks—a common exploit in which malicious bots place a buy order before, and a sell order immediately after, the user’s transaction. This forces users to purchase tokens at inflated prices, significantly reducing their profits.

Our **custom smart contract**, which has been audited by a reputable third-party firm, provides robust protection against such attacks. It achieves this by **concealing your slippage settings**, leaving no room for attackers to exploit the transaction.

To guarantee the security of user transactions, **this feature cannot be disabled**. Rest assured, Auto MEV Protection is seamlessly integrated and does not affect the **lightning speed** of your transactions, ensuring both top-tier security and market-leading performance.
