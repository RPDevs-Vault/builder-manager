# Introduction | LORE 

LORE emerges as the crucial missing link in the Solana memecoin ecosystem—the bridge between a token's graduation from PumpFun and its potential moonshot trajectory. When a token graduates from PumpFun, it receives a coveted "seal of approval," signifying successful emergence from the initial launch phase. At this pivotal moment, tokens face their greatest challenge: standing independently in the volatile Solana memecoin market without the automatic visibility provided by launch platforms.

The journey of a memecoin before graduation benefits from significant "free marketing" through various channels:

*   DEX interfaces like BullX, Photon, and GMGN featuring new listings
    
*   Telegram trading bots highlighting low-cap opportunities
    
*   Initial launch platforms providing structured visibility
    
*   Trading communities focused on newly launched tokens
    

However, once a token graduates, this promotional ecosystem often falls silent, leaving even promising projects struggling to maintain momentum. Holders frequently ask the question: "We've graduated... what now?"

Survival Section


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](https://docs.lorevision.ai/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F766448884-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252Fa5WBJ0H8fdWgiTGEmMya%252Fuploads%252FpwBkH4OjlflHpHxjOAUg%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D460efc9b-b558-421d-83ff-873ce958397b&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=4d8c23bf&sv=2)

The post-graduation phase is often marked by what we call "post-bond jitters"—a period when early gamblers begin taking initial profits or full clipping their gains. This common pattern can significantly impact a token's trajectory, and the Survival Section is designed to highlight tokens experiencing these critical moments.

Tokens enter "survival mode" when their market cap drops below a specific threshold. This threshold is calculated based on the 24-hour average market cap of all graduated tokens, ensuring it remains responsive to overall market conditions. The Survival Section serves dual purposes:

1.  Drawing attention to tokens that require support to recover from dips
    
2.  Highlighting tokens potentially headed for a quick demise
    

When marked as "survival," a token has exactly 5 minutes to regain a market cap above the threshold. A real-time countdown shows exactly how much time remains before potential failure. If successful in recovering, the token returns to the main graduated list, continuing its journey. However, no token is immune from future relegation to survival status should its market cap fall below the threshold again.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.lorevision.ai/terminal/graduation-lore-vision#critical-threshold-and-immediate-failure)

Critical Threshold and Immediate Failure

Tokens that plummet below the critical market cap threshold—defined as the PumpFun starting market cap plus 10%—are immediately marked as failed without entering the survival period. This swift action helps maintain the quality of the main list by quickly filtering out tokens showing signs of catastrophic decline.

[hashtag](https://docs.lorevision.ai/terminal/graduation-lore-vision#token-graveyard)

Token Graveyard


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](https://docs.lorevision.ai/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F766448884-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252Fa5WBJ0H8fdWgiTGEmMya%252Fuploads%252FIcAhB2bNts8mzeNSY6Uk%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3De200b3af-b092-4f8d-8cd9-63b61652c28c&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=98142043&sv=2)

The Token Graveyard serves as the final resting place for tokens that have failed to maintain minimum viability. While it might seem merely archival, the Graveyard actually serves important functions within the LORE ecosystem:

1.  **Quality Control**: It keeps the main list focused on viable, actively traded tokens
    
2.  **Trend Intelligence**: It provides valuable data for trend analysis—when clusters of similar tokens enter the graveyard within a close timeframe, this signals potential shifts in market sentiment or reactions to real-time events
    
3.  **Historical Reference**: It maintains a record of failed narratives and approaches, creating learning opportunities for traders and developers alike
    

The Graveyard includes comprehensive failure information for each token, including the exact time of failure and the specific reason (market cap drop, survival period exceeded, long-term inactivity, etc.).

[hashtag](https://docs.lorevision.ai/terminal/graduation-lore-vision#seven-day-tracking-window)

Seven-Day Tracking Window


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

To maintain focus on current opportunities, LORE actively tracks graduated tokens for a 7-day period before archiving them in the LORE hall of fame. This timeframe was carefully selected to balance comprehensive monitoring with the platform's emphasis on fresh opportunities. The graduation page is specifically designed for real-time trading discovery rather than historical analysis.

As LORE evolves, we may explore options to track tokens for longer periods and provide analysis on a wider range of tokens beyond just those that have graduated from PumpFun. For now, this focused approach ensures clean data research without interference from bespoke Solana contracts and the wider market.

Selection Criteria

The Featured Agent makes its selections based on a sophisticated multi-factor analysis looking at:

*   Substantial and growing holder counts
