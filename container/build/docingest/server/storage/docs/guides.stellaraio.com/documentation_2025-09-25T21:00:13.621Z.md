# Getting Started with StellarAIO | Stellar

So you just got Stellar and don't know where to start? You're in the right place! We will let you know step by step on how to get started with StellarAIO

### 

[](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar#what-is-stellar)

What is Stellar?

Stellar AIO is an all in one retail automation software built to help members purchase high demand products from major retailers at retail price. Our goal is to simplify the process of securing items like trading cards, collectibles, consoles, GPUs, toys and more so users can either keep or resell them

We support dozens of major retailers including Amazon, Walmart, Target, Best Buy, Pokémon Center, and many others with built in features like serverside monitoring, automatic checkout and real time Discord support

At Stellar, we focus on retail automation and restock monitoring, we are not a cook group. We do not offer product information, resale predictions or financial advice

Whether you’re just starting out or already experienced in the game, Stellar is built to be beginner friendly, powerful and always improving

If you're new here, the rest of this guide will walk you through how to install, set up and get the most out of Stellar

**We also have all the guides referenced in the step by step instructions below!**

*   Download Stellar [Windows](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/installation-uninstallation/how-do-i-download-stellaraio-windows-and-mac#h_d523604013)
     or [Mac](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/installation-uninstallation/how-do-i-download-stellaraio-windows-and-mac#h_6126dd8c70)
    
*   You're going to need to grab your license key from our [Stellar Whop page](https://whop.com/stellaraio/license-key-management-JVLVXWY5PXkmM2/app/)
     copy your key and paste it into Stellar
    

![](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1122101074-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MQL0BOlyqRRf7zyNKSf%252Fuploads%252FCUznfLih39dDAx53Ze6S%252F3.0verifyinglicense.PNG%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D0155f81e-92b7-498e-9a31-b2576b542b78&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=5ebc7cea&sv=2)

*   Now it's time to start setting up your Stellar [settings](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/navigating-stellaraio/what-is-the-settings-tab)
    ! It's very important to go through the settings tab and enter any information you can. For example, we highly recommend setting up your [Discord Webhook](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/navigating-stellaraio/what-is-the-settings-tab/how-to-set-up-discord-notifications)
     so you're notified when Stellar successfully checked out and other notifications
    

![](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1122101074-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MQL0BOlyqRRf7zyNKSf%252Fuploads%252FgJQAO90VIPS2KCmLBLWK%252F3.0generalsettings.PNG%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dd55f3b5d-616f-47ac-b46c-bd07f863d3ce&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=bfbbb410&sv=2)

*   Setup your [Captcha Harvester/Solver](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/navigating-stellaraio/what-is-the-captchas-tab/how-do-i-set-up-the-captcha-harvester)
     so you'll be able to solve Captchas manually if needed. For example, some modules like Pokemon Center require you to solve some Captchas manually and you'll need Google Chrome installed on your PC
    

![](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1122101074-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MQL0BOlyqRRf7zyNKSf%252Fuploads%252FUdVP8LxytOcqJgTGNmVF%252Fcaptchaharvester.PNG%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D5f88b16c-80cb-425f-b0d2-fd1d8b1452de&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=44262287&sv=2)

*   Setup your Accounts so you'll have all of your emails and passwords to each account readily accessible. Some modules like Walmart have items that you'll need an account to purchase and is less likely for your orders to get canceled if you use an account
    

![](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1122101074-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MQL0BOlyqRRf7zyNKSf%252Fuploads%252FfzNARdsNHjKq5gC4K0xx%252F3.0accountstab.PNG%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D38027656-81a1-406e-8888-371a60aaeffa&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=7ce3336b&sv=2)

*   Setup your [Sessions](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/navigating-stellaraio/what-is-the-identities-tab/how-do-i-add-a-session-or-mass-import-sessions)
     so you'll be able to stay logged into your accounts for a longer period of time and less errors going forward
    

![](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1122101074-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MQL0BOlyqRRf7zyNKSf%252Fuploads%252FkonSMYXg1bjRpb7yxTIR%252F3.0sessionstab.PNG%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dcc8796bf-cfdc-4d57-ad67-e763135196f7&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=76590c88&sv=2)

*   Setup your [Proxies](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/navigating-stellaraio/what-is-the-proxies-tab/how-do-i-add-proxies)
     (proxies are required) so you're able to mask your local/server IP and you'll be able to monitor and checkout multiple items. Having multiple proxy groups (ISPs, DCs and Resis) is recommended so if you're having issues with one group then you can use another as your backup
    
    *   If you're looking for proxies, check out [Stella Proxies](https://help.stellaraio.com/stellar/getting-started-with-stellaraio/stella-proxies)
         for high quality ISP and Residential proxies
        
    

![](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1122101074-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MQL0BOlyqRRf7zyNKSf%252Fuploads%252FpFPCPV8BhR5T6kmsjl54%252F3.0proxiestab.PNG%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D59226604-f4d6-4bae-b360-71f532c5212d&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=776eebe&sv=2)

*   Setup your [Profiles](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/navigating-stellaraio/what-is-the-profiles-tab/how-do-i-add-a-profile)
     so all of your personal information (billing, shipping and payment information) is saved in Stellar. Each task and session you make is tied to a certain profile so make sure you're choosing wisely or you may receive shipping/payment issues.
    

![](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1122101074-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MQL0BOlyqRRf7zyNKSf%252Fuploads%252FtnGalMa1SmRtDiulDcWR%252F3.0profilestab.PNG%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dee985b7b-2fa5-40e0-9de2-15585eeedb06&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=e6926ee4&sv=2)

*   Setup your [Tasks](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/navigating-stellaraio/what-is-the-tasks-tab)
     there is where you will be able to monitor and checkout items! It's very important when creating tasks to know what all of the fields and checkboxes mean. If you're ever unsure what a field is then you can go to that actual modules guide where all of the fields are explained there. For example, if you're unsure what each mode does you can go to the [Target](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/retailers/targetgo)
     and there's a breakdown.
    

![](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1122101074-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F-MQL0BOlyqRRf7zyNKSf%252Fuploads%252F2vLLZUv8EfMj2CLUWXvk%252Fpkc1.PNG%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dbee72d6b-df48-471f-a84d-6615715041fe&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=3662a0fb&sv=2)

Important Notes:

*   We always recommend to be on the latest version of Stellar. There are always stability and antibot improvements on the later versions
    
*   Take full advantage of the StellarAIO Guide it's constantly updated and has very general baseline setups for most major sites
    
*   Proxies are required to run Stellar
    
    *   If you're looking for proxies, check out [Stella Proxies](https://help.stellaraio.com/stellar/getting-started-with-stellaraio/stella-proxies)
         for high quality ISP and Residential proxies
        
    
*   You may find it beneficial to join a [cookgroup](https://guides.stellaraio.com/stellar/getting-started-with-stellaraio/cook-groups)
    , this group will supply you with everything you need to know when botting. They provide product information, upcoming releases, early access links, passwords for sites and many other benefits
