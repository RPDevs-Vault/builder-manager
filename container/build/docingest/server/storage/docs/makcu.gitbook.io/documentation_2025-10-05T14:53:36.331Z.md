Welcome to the MAKCM setup


---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

#### 

[](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu#steps-to-flash-your-unit)

**Steps to Flash Your Unit:**

1.  **Run from Your 2nd PC** : To avoid leaving temp files, make sure to run the AIO tool from your second PC (where files will be unpacked into a local temp folder).
    
2.  **Download the AIO Tool:** Get the tool from [here](https://github.com/terrafirma2021/MAKCM_v2_files)
     or [Download](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/makcu-board/download)
    .
    
3.  **Open the AIO Tool:** Let it update automatically.
    
4.  Requirement USB cable: You’ll need one USB cable to flash the PCB. This master cable will be connected to the second PC.
    

Flash Steps

![](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3822774255-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FOFta9wUfh5yxL98PENBK%252Fuploads%252FMGg0HGhVJ1N4Gssbei5b%252FA605c203429d949feb486a15ed515be78V.png_960x960.png_-removebg-preview.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Ded82a76b-bdb6-4a5c-8b26-3475631db721&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=c6446968&sv=2)

**WHEN FLASHING REMOVE ALL CABLES flash mode is only done via the 2nd pc never the main.**

Flash Step 1

![](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3822774255-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FOFta9wUfh5yxL98PENBK%252Fuploads%252FQQYHJVLSDD9OnVGoT0OO%252Fimage3.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D59538a5d-faee-4e37-a4e2-8b0503513262&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=9744c665&sv=2)

hold the **LEFT** button and plug the blue cable into the **LEFT** USB port.

After MAKCM is in flash mode, you should see "MCU connected in flash mode"

![](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3822774255-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FOFta9wUfh5yxL98PENBK%252Fuploads%252FHvEnmObCXnReMw6h5dYG%252Fimage%2520%283%29.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D10ac1328-6a1f-44e0-a8de-4babea57a01b&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=f757eb9f&sv=2)

Flash Step 2

![](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3822774255-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FOFta9wUfh5yxL98PENBK%252Fuploads%252Fn8md2bHEJzveApQXLWRF%252Fimage2.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dd6e92b82-fcb2-4558-8276-c1d0b86784cf&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=8b1b543&sv=2)

Com Port


---------------------------------------------------------

On your second PC, go to **Device Manager** and open **COM**.

![](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3822774255-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FOFta9wUfh5yxL98PENBK%252Fuploads%252FgUyP2wwoJ8yDeKccvw8x%252Fimageda.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dc915cdf3-d1c7-4f28-ba2c-b22446d57d3d&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=48342ea0&sv=2)

Make sure the COM port number for **Makcu** is below 1 (shown as **CH343**).

Right click and click properties.

![](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3822774255-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FOFta9wUfh5yxL98PENBK%252Fuploads%252FBPBacJ2UbmYMbJJKvQOo%252Fimagedwad.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Db7d9e698-eb9d-4e19-af5b-836ef20beccc&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=60a60002&sv=2)

Click on advance

Click Advance...

![](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3822774255-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FOFta9wUfh5yxL98PENBK%252Fuploads%252FFV3HLNu4sgisKZfLUGaI%252Fimageddd.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dbb21bdef-b60c-42dc-9c25-74f6c6e146de&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=f0ed8b60&sv=2)

Pick a lower com port than 10, then press Ok to save.

If you have the **Makcu AIO tool** open, close it and restart **Makcu** by unplugging and replugging the **left most cable**.

After completing all the steps, the flash process should be complete. ✅

Wired Connection


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   Connection-Connect the USB-C to the main pc as the middle image from above.
    
*   Connect Controller to the right side Xim port.
    
*   Connect the MAKCU USB to the USB port NEXT to the USB-C as shown on the picture below:
    

![](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3822774255-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FOFta9wUfh5yxL98PENBK%252Fuploads%252FLoLSh2vCsIivHaqZHkix%252FXim%2520Matrix%2520Guide%2520%28Controller%2520only%29.pdf-image-001.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D2805887e-054f-4468-ae33-796fcfba0a3d&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=6adde8f0&sv=2)

Wireless Connection


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

connect the Xim Matrix only to Main PC according to the picture below and pair your gamepad.

When pairing, you will need to long-press the XIM MATRIX button (about 2 seconds)

![](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3822774255-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FOFta9wUfh5yxL98PENBK%252Fuploads%252FFHIKhExt2aTtkJn1kMR8%252Fxim%25201112.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D679a301b-70b1-405a-9b43-677cc1932511&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=ec530f6d&sv=2)

Until it starts blinking blue. At that point, you can then pair your gamepad by activating pairing mode.

**In Case of a Mouse Bug:**


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If you encounter a mouse bug, follow these steps:

*   **Ensure the flashing process has been followed correctly.**
    
*   Press **Log Enabled**. This will reboot the device.
    
*   The device will start logging in the terminal. You can disconnect the left USB port and the mouse, keeping the 2nd PC connected.
    
*   Connect the **left USB port** to the **main PC**, then connect the mouse. Make a ticket in the server, Press **User Logs** and submit the logs in the ticket.
    

The dev will review the logs and work on fixing the mouse bug.

[](https://makcu.gitbook.io/makcu/makcu-board/troubleshooting#disable-the-log-button)

Disable The Log Button
