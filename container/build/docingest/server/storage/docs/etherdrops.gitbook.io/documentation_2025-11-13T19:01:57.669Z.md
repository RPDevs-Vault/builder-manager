[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/troubleshooting-and-support/general-inquiries#how-to-link-your-dropstab-account-with-drops-bot)

How to Link Your DropsTab Account with Drops Bot?


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Answer:** Linking your DropsTab and Drops Bot accounts is essential for participating in Fomo App tasks or [receiving specific alerts](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/settings#miniapp-alerts)
. Follow these steps to establish the connection:

1.  **Access DropsTab Website:** Open the [DropsTab](https://dropstab.com/)
    .
    
2.  **Log In:** Authorize your account if you haven't already.
    
3.  **Navigate to Profile Settings:**
    
    *   Click on your **avatar** (profile picture).
        
    *   From the dropdown menu, select **Profile Settings**.
        
    
4.  **Initiate Connection:**
    
    *   In the **Linked Accounts** section, click the **Connect** button.
        
    *   A menu will appear, offering two options: **Open Bot to Connect** or **copy the Verification Code**.
        
    
5.  **Complete Linking (Recommended Method):**
    
    *   The most reliable method for linking accounts is to **copy the Verification Code** from the DropsTab menu.
        
    *   Paste and send this code in a private chat with the Drops Bot.
        
    
6.  **Confirmation:** Upon successful linkage, you will receive the following message from the bot:
    

> ✅ Your Telegram Account has been successfully linked with your DropsTab Account.

Your linked account will also be displayed in the Linked Accounts section within your DropsTab Profile Settings.

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/troubleshooting-and-support/general-inquiries#how-do-i-disable-the-green-buy-button-caller-mode-in-alerts)

How do I disable the green **Buy** button (Caller Mode) in alerts?


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Solution:** To completely disable the **Buy button (Caller Mode)** in Drops Bot, follow these steps:

1.  Open the bot’s **Main Menu**
    
2.  Tap "⚙️ **Settings"**
    
3.  Tap the "**☎️ Caller Mode"** button
    
4.  Set it to "**Off"**
    

Once disabled, the green **Buy** button will no longer appear in **any of your alerts or profiles**. You can re-enable it at any time by returning to the same setting.

> **Note:** Disabling Caller Mode affects **all groups and notifications** linked to your current bot instance.

This button is part of the **Caller Mode** feature, which embeds your **referral link** into Solana token alerts. For a detailed explanation of how Caller Mode works and how to earn with it, see the [**Caller Mode section**](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/caller-mode)
.

# Common Issues | Drops Bot

If you don't find a solution to your specific problem here or require immediate assistance, we recommend reaching out to our dedicated support team directly via Telegram: [**@drops\_support**](https://t.me/edrops_support)

* * *

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/troubleshooting-and-support/common-issues#alerts-or-swaps-are-not-being-received)

Alerts or Swaps Are Not Being Received


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Description:** You're experiencing delays or a complete absence of alerts for your tracked wallets (e.g., on BSC or BASE networks) or token swaps for an extended period (e.g., several hours).

**Solution:** This issue is often related to Telegram's internal messaging limits or your bot’s subscription plan.

1.  **Switch Bot Instances:** Try switching to a different bot instance. You can view available bots by sending the `/check` command in a private chat with your active bot. Switching instances may help you bypass temporary Telegram messaging limits.
    
2.  **Check Plan Limits:** It's possible you have reached the hourly message limit according to your bot’s current **subscription plan**. When this limit is reached, alerts will temporarily cease. An example of a message you might receive when hitting a message limit is:
    

> ⚠️ **Limit Reached**
> 
> You have reached the maximum number of groups/channels for your **Basic** plan.
> 
> To add the bot to more groups, please **upgrade your plan**.

If alerts are still not being received after trying the above solutions, please try disabling the **Filter Scam** option in the bot's settings.

* * *

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/troubleshooting-and-support/common-issues#bot-not-responding-to-commands-in-a-group)

Bot Not Responding to Commands in a Group


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Description:** After successfully adding the bot to a Telegram group, it fails to react to any commands.

**Solution:** This issue typically indicates an incomplete removal or improper connection of the bot.

1.  **Remove Bot from Group:** Begin by completely removing the bot from your Telegram group.
    
2.  **Verify Complete Deletion:** Ensure the bot has been fully removed from your profile's tracked groups:
    
    *   Navigate to: **Main menu** → **Tracking** → **My Profiles**.
        
    *   Open the editing menu for any profile by tapping the `/EDIT` command.
        
    *   If the group still appears, tap **Delete Group** and select the group you want to remove.
        
    
3.  **Change Bot Instance:** After confirming the bot is entirely removed from the group and your profile, you will need to change your active bot instance. Send the `/check` command in a private chat with your current bot to see available bot instances.
    
4.  **Re-add Bot:** Once you have switched to a new bot instance, attempt to connect the bot to your group by following the instructions in the [Add Bot to a Group or Channel](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels/create-and-manage-profiles#how-to-add-the-bot-to-a-group)
     guide.
    

* * *

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/troubleshooting-and-support/common-issues#unwanted-notifications-in-your-private-bot-chat-how-to-manage-and-disable-them)

Unwanted Notifications in Your Private Bot Chat: How to Manage and Disable Them


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

If you are consistently receiving messages from the bot in your private chat, this indicates that you have previously configured or activated specific alerts. The bot is informing you about events you've specified, such as:

*   **Coin Price Changes or Transactions:** Alerts regarding swaps, significant price fluctuations for cryptocurrencies you are tracking.
    
*   **Wallet Activity:** Notifications about incoming or outgoing transactions on your monitored cryptocurrency wallets.
    
*   **NFT, Gas Alerts, or Other Specific Notifications:** Depending on the bot's functionality, these could include other types of customized alerts you've set up.
    

**Steps to Disable Notifications:**

To manage these notifications, you need to identify which type of alert the incoming message belongs to (e.g., a [coin alert](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/coins/coin-management)
, [wallet alert](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/wallets/wallet-management)
, [NFT alert](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/nft)
, [gas alert](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/gas-alerts)
, etc.).

Then:

1.  **Navigate to the corresponding settings section** within the bot's interface (e.g., "Coins," "Wallets," "NFTs").
    
2.  **Locate and either disable or remove** the specific alert that you no longer wish to receive.
    

Detailed instructions on how to navigate and disable specific alert types can be found in the relevant sections of this documentation.

Global Settings

When viewing your list of tracked coins, you'll find these general management buttons:

*   **➕ Add new** – Add a new coin to your watchlist.
    
*   **🔗 Coin Link** – Choose your preferred data provider for links in token notifications. When an alert is triggered, the link in the notification will direct you to the selected data source.
    
    *   **Available Providers:**
        
        *   **DexCheck** – Analytics and data for decentralized tokens.
            
        *   **Gecko** – Provides price, market cap, and other token data.
            
        *   **Blockscan** – Tracks addresses and transactions linked to the coin.
            
        *   **GMGN** – Offers in-depth market insights and token movement analysis.

    *   _Note: This option is only available when two or more coins are being tracked._

📝 Individual Coin Settings (/EDIT Mode)


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F2854945133-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FE1xatl7EJgINFUiyMW2P%252Fuploads%252F58shapyKTUyH0GTx2XwS%252FCoinSettings.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D3186f6b1-9f30-46b6-8137-1b29fde81d32&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=4b7af051&sv=2)

Coin Settings

Once you're in the **/EDIT** mode for a specific coin, you can configure the following detailed settings:

*   **🔀 Change Alert** – Adjust the percentage price change that triggers a notification.
    
*   **📈 Price Alert** – Set a notification when the coin reaches a specific price target (default: not set).
    
*   **♻️ Alert Swaps** – Enable notifications for swaps exceeding a specified amount (available only for DEX networks).
    
*   **💹 Funding** – Configure alerts for futures funding rate changes (alerts when the rate is above or below a set value, default: not set).
    
    *   _Enter values from -1 to 1 (e.g.,_ `_< -0.3_` _or set a range like_ `_< -0.3 > 0.5_`_)._
        
    
*   **💵 Price In** – Select the currency for price display (USD, BTC, ETH, BNB). (Default: USD)
    
*   **🔉 Notifications** – Enable or disable all alerts for this coin (ON/OFF).
    
*   **🚦 Swap Direction** – Set alerts for specific swap types:
    
    *   **Buys:** Receive alerts only for buy transactions of this coin.
        
    *   **Sells:** Receive alerts only for sell transactions of this coin.
        
    *   **Both:** Receive alerts for both buy and sell transactions.
        
    
*   **🗑️ Delete** – Remove this specific coin from your watchlist.

Open the **topic (thread)** you want to configure

Send the command:

Copy

    /use_thread Main

Replace `Main` with the name of the Profile you want to link.

> You can find the correct Profile name in the bot’s menu under **“Profiles”**, or by sending the `/profiles` command in your **private chat with the bot**.

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels/linking-profiles#important-clarification)

*   If **User A** added DropsBot to the group, only **User A’s Profiles** can be connected.

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels/linking-profiles#key-notes-to-remember)

🧠 Key Notes to Remember

*   **Only the user who added the bot** can manage Profiles for that group
    
*   **You can connect different Profiles to different topics** within the same group
    
*   **Other users cannot add another Drops Bot** or connect unrelated Profiles

# Bot for Groups and Channels | Drops Bot

The **Profiles** feature in Drops Bot is designed to give you full control over **what notifications you receive and where they are sent** — whether it’s to your **private chat**, a **Telegram group**, or a **channel**.

It’s especially useful for **influencers**, **community admins**, and active users who want their audience to receive real-time alerts about **wallet activity**, **whale movements**, **liquidity changes**, **token listings**, and other key crypto events.

Only **one Drops Bot instance** can be added to a group or channel

* * *

### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels#what-is-drops-bot-for-groups-and-channels)

🔍 What Is Drops Bot for Groups & Channels?

If you:

*   Manage a crypto group or niche Telegram community
    
*   Want to automatically broadcast important on-chain events
    
*   Or need to separate personal notifications from public ones
    

You can **add Drops Bot to a group or channel**, apply filters, and let it handle smart, real-time alerts.

💡 All configuration is done from a **single interface**, whether it's for personal use or public broadcasting.

* * *

### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels#what-are-profiles)

👤 What Are Profiles?

A **Profile** is a bundle of settings that defines:

*   **What to track** — coins, swaps, NFTs, wallets, pools, etc.
    
*   **Where to send alerts** — personal chat, group, or channel
    

Think of each Profile as a **dedicated alert stream** linked to a specific destination.

* * *

### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels#types-of-profiles)

🧩 Types of Profiles

**Main Profile (Default)**[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels#main-profile-default)

*   Created automatically
    
*   Used by default for **private notifications**
    
*   **Can be linked** to a group or channel
    
    > If linked, all alerts you receive privately will also be **mirrored** to the group/channel
    

📌 Ideal if you want to receive the **same alerts in both your private chat and your group**

**Custom Profiles**[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels#custom-profiles)

*   You can create **multiple Profiles** for different purposes
    

*   Each Profile can be linked to:
    
    *   A **separate group**
        
    *   A **channel**
        
    *   Or even a **specific topic** inside a group (Telegram topics are supported)
        
    

![](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F2854945133-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FE1xatl7EJgINFUiyMW2P%252Fuploads%252FyPf8mWgUSYtIXXnfMStU%252FProfileList.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D23267b67-f332-4af0-8f66-a166aae71dc2&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=acf3f144&sv=2)

📌 Example setup:

*   **Profile 1** – sends Ethereum whale wallet activity to _Topic A_
    
*   **Profile 2** – sends DEX swap alerts to _Topic B_
    
*   **Profile 3** – monitors NFT mints in a _separate group_
    

* * *

### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels#can-i-use-drops-bot-for-both-private-and-group-notifications)

🔁 Can I Use Drops Bot for Both Private and Group Notifications?

**Yes!** You can:

*   Get **personal alerts** via the Main Profile
    
*   And manage **group or channel alerts** at the same time
    

All from **one centralized interface**, no need to switch bots.

* * *

### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels#short-commands-for-groups-channel)

💬 Short Commands for Groups/Channel

Once Drops Bot is added to a group/channel, admins can use these slash commands:

1.  **Search a coin** `/name`, `/ticker`, or `/contract` — Shows real-time data like price, % change, etc.
    
2.  **Help** `/help` — Displays general bot usage guide
    
3.  **Coin Watchlist** `/coins` — Shows coins being tracked and their price movements
    
4.  **NFT Collections** `/nft` — Lists tracked NFT collections and floor price changes
    
5.  **Liquidity Pools** `/pools` — Displays tracked pools and total liquidity
    
6.  **Funding Rates** `/funding` — Shows Binance Futures funding rates
    
7.  **Gas Price** `/gas` — Displays current Ethereum gas prices
    
8.  **GMX Positions** `/gmx_positions` — Shows open GMX derivatives or margin positions
    

* * *

### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/bot-for-groups-and-channels#summary)

🧭 Summary

*   **Profiles** manage what to track and where to send alerts
    
*   **Main Profile** is your default and supports both private and group usage
    
*   **Custom Profiles** help separate alerts by topic, group or channel
    
*   You can use Drops Bot **simultaneously** for private and public alerts
    
*   All configuration is done from **one control panel**
    
*   Only **one Drops Bot** per group/channel — but with **many Profiles** inside
    

* * *

The **Profiles system** makes Drops Bot an ideal solution for anyone who wants to receive the **right signals at the right time**, without clutter or confusion.

**Error Handling in Drops Bot: Common Issues & Solutions**


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

When using blockchain-based features like swaps, liquidity tracking, and smart contract interactions, errors may occur due to various factors. Here's a detailed breakdown of the most common errors, their causes, and how to resolve them.

### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-eng/error#id-1.-slippage-control)

**1\. Slippage Control**

#### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-eng/error#error)

Error

#### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-eng/error#failed-due-to-executing-swap-was-failed-on-the-blockchain.-reason-exceeds-desired-slippage-limit)

**“Failed due to ‘executing swap was failed on the blockchain. Reason: Exceeds desired slippage limit’”**

📍 Cause

💡 What is Slippage?

✅ Solution

Your slippage tolerance is set too low. The actual market price changed more than your allowed slippage.

The difference between the expected execution price and the actual price when the transaction goes through. It helps protect users from volatile market moves.

Increase the slippage tolerance in [settings](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-eng/settings)
 to allow successful transaction execution during market volatility.

### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-eng/error#id-2.-solana-fee-reserve-required)

#### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-eng/error#error-1)

Error

#### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-eng/error#failed-due-to-balance-is-not-enough-to-transfer-and-cover-solana-fees)

**“Failed due to 'Balance is not enough to transfer and cover Solana fees’”**

📍 Cause

✅ Solution

Insufficient SOL balance to cover transaction fees.

To successfully execute transactions, you need to have an additional **0.01 SOL** in your account as a reserve. These funds are intended to cover any unexpected issues that may arise during the transaction process.

### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-eng/error#id-3.-account-is-frozen)

**“Executing swap was failed on the blockchain. Reason: account is frozen”**

📍 Cause

✅ Solution

🔒 Recommendation

The wallet contains suspicious or unauthorized tokens, triggering a "frozen" status on-chain.

*   Check your wallet's token list
    
*   Remove suspicious or unknown tokens
    
*   Avoid manually adding unchecked tokens
    
*   If compromised, **create a new wallet** and transfer safe assets only
    

Use tools like **Token Sniffer**, **DexTools**, or **CoinGecko** to verify token safety before interacting with them.

**“Failed due to ‘there was an error while executing transfer’”**

📍 Cause

✅ Solution

Issues with the recipient address:

*   Incorrect format
    
*   Wrong network

📍 Cause

✅ Solution

The swap aggregator (e.g., Jupiter) cannot find a route between the selected tokens due to:

📍 Cause

*   Low priority fee

✅ Solution

A generic failure due to:

*   Blockchain/network issues
    
*   Smart contract errors
    
*   Insufficient funds
    
*   Invalid input or swap parameters
    
*   Router malfunctions
    

*   Retry the transaction — sometimes it’s a temporary issue
    
*   Ensure **sufficient balance** for gas
    
*   Recheck all inputs (token pairs, values, routes)
    
*   Wait and confirm **network stability**

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

При использовании функций Drops Bot, связанных с блокчейном — таких как свапы, отслеживание ликвидности и взаимодействие со смарт-контрактами — могут возникать ошибки. Ниже собраны самые частые из них, их причины и способы устранения.

### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-rus/oshibki#id-1.-upravlenie-proskalzyvaniem-slippage)

#### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-rus/oshibki#oshibka)

Ошибка

#### 

[](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-rus/oshibki#failed-due-to-executing-swap-was-failed-on-the-blockchain.-reason-exceeds-desired-slippage-limit)

**«Failed due to ‘executing swap was failed on the blockchain. Reason: Exceeds desired slippage limit’»**

📍 Причина

💡 Что такое проскальзывание?

✅ Решение

Указано слишком низкое значение проскальзывания. Рыночная цена изменилась сильнее, чем разрешено настройками.

Разница между ожидаемой и фактической ценой исполнения транзакции. Помогает защититься от волатильности рынка.

Увеличьте допустимое значение проскальзывания в [настройках](https://etherdrops.gitbook.io/etherdrops-bot/trading-rus/settings)
, чтобы снизить риск неудачного исполнения транзакции.
