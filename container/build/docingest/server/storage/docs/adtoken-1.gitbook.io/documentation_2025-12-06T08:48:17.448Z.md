### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/#id-1.-introduction-to-adtoken)

📄 **1\. Introduction to AdToken**

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/#what-is-adtoken)

**What is AdToken?**

AdToken is a **next-generation, peer-to-peer advertisement network** built on **blockchain technology**, designed for **Web3 ecosystems**. It connects **advertisers, influencers, micro-influencers, and publishers** in a **decentralized, transparent, and automated advertising system**.

Built on the **Sui Network**, AdToken leverages blockchain for **enhanced transparency, fraud prevention, and cost efficiency**, enabling **seamless ad transactions without intermediaries**.

Unlike traditional ad networks, AdToken offers **on-chain, real-time, event-based payments**, **interactive ad formats**, and a **dynamic pricing system** — making advertising fair, scalable, and efficient for the Web3 world.

* * *

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/#why-adtoken)

**Why AdToken?**

In today's fragmented and opaque ad industry, both advertisers and publishers face key challenges:

*   Lack of **transparent payment systems and real accountability**.
    
*   High **middlemen fees**, reducing actual payouts to publishers and influencers.
    
*   **Fraudulent traffic and click fraud**, leading to wasted ad spend.
    
*   **Delayed payments** to affiliates and publishers.
    
*   Limited options for **interactive and Web3-specific ad formats**.
    

**AdToken solves all these problems** with a **fully decentralized, automated, and transparent system** — where advertisers pay **only for valid engagements** and publishers receive **instant on-chain payments** for their contributions.

* * *

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/#our-mission)

**Our Mission**

To **revolutionize the digital ad space** by enabling **direct, transparent, and fair advertising**, powered by blockchain and decentralized on-chain payments — making advertising accessible, cost-effective, and interactive for all participants in the Web3 ecosystem.

* * *

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/#our-vision)

**Our Vision**

*   **Empower Web3 businesses** to reach global audiences through **cost-effective, measurable ad campaigns**.
    
*   **Enable influencers and publishers** to monetize traffic and influence without intermediaries, using **real-time, event-based crypto payouts**.
    
*   Build a **decentralized marketplace** where **ads, traffic, and conversions** are powered by **smart contracts and transparent event-driven logic**.
    

* * *

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/#key-highlights-of-adtoken)

**Key Highlights of AdToken**

✅ **Decentralized peer-to-peer ad network** — no middlemen, fully automated. ✅ **Gas-free, real-time on-chain payments** based on verified ad events like clicks, impressions, or interactions. ✅ **Dynamic Pricing** to optimize campaign ROI and reward distribution. ✅ **Supports multiple ad formats** — display, video, interactive, and click-based. ✅ **Simple publisher integration** via API & Iframe (AdSense-like solution for Web3). ✅ **Social verification system** for **authentic influencers and publishers**. ✅ **Detailed analytics & performance dashboards** for campaign monitoring. ✅ **On-chain event-based smart contracts** for **transparent and automated payments** (replacing traditional escrow systems).

# How AdToken Works | Adtoken Docs

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/adtoken-docs/overview/how-adtoken-works#platform-structure)

**Platform Structure**

AdToken is a decentralized advertising platform built on the Sui network, connecting advertisers, publishers, and influencers. It leverages blockchain technology to ensure transparency, reduce fraud, and enhance cost efficiency in digital advertising.

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/adtoken-docs/overview/how-adtoken-works#user-roles-and-onboarding)

**User Roles and Onboarding**

**Advertisers**: Businesses aiming to promote their products or services.

*   **Sign-Up Process**:
    
    *   Select "Continue as Advertiser" during sign-up.
        
    *   Complete the registration form with basic details.
        
    *   Connect a Sui-compatible wallet (e.g., Walrus, Stashed); the public key serves as a unique identifier.
        
    
*   **Post Sign-Up**:
    
    *   Create and manage campaigns.
        
    *   Monitor dashboards and manage balances.
        
    

**Partners**: Publishers and influencers looking to monetize their platforms.

*   **Sign-Up Process**:
    
    *   Select "Continue as Partner" during sign-up.
        
    *   Complete the registration form with basic details.
        
    *   Connect a Sui-compatible wallet; the public key serves as a unique identifier.
        
    
*   **Post Sign-Up**:
    
    *   Add multiple websites or social accounts.
        
    *   Integrate AdToken widgets or iframes.
        
    *   Access dashboards, analytics, and manage earnings.
        
    

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/adtoken-docs/overview/how-adtoken-works#campaign-participation)

**Campaign Participation**

**For Advertisers**:

*   Create campaigns by allocating funds from their wallet balance.
    
*   Define campaign objectives and target audience.
    
*   Monitor performance through real-time analytics.
    

**For Partners**:

*   Browse and select campaigns that align with their platform or audience.
    
*   Integrate ads using provided widgets or iframes.
    
*   Track earnings and performance metrics.
    
*   Withdraw earnings once a minimum threshold of $10 is reached.
    

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/adtoken-docs/overview/how-adtoken-works#payment-and-rewards)

**Payment and Rewards**

*   **On-Chain Event-Based Payments**: Utilizes automated smart contracts to process payments based on verified user interactions without the need for escrow accounts.
    
*   **Real-Time Earnings**: Partners' wallets are credited instantly upon valid user engagements.
    
*   **Withdrawal Process**:
    
    *   **Advertisers**: No minimum withdrawal limit.
        
    *   **Partners**: Minimum withdrawal limit of $10.
        
    *   Payouts are processed instantly, typically within 3-5 minutes.
        
    

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/adtoken-docs/overview/how-adtoken-works#security-and-transparency)

**Security and Transparency**

*   **Blockchain Integration**: Ensures all transactions and engagements are recorded on-chain, providing transparency and reducing potential fraud.
    
*   **User Verification**: Partners can add social links during sign-up and verify them later (currently supporting Twitter, with plans to add more platforms).

# Campaign Types | Adtoken Docs

**Initiating a Campaign**:

*   **Access Points**:
    
    *   "Create Your Campaign" button on the top navigation bar.
        
    *   First option in the left-side navigation menu.
        
    

**3-Step Campaign Creation Process**:

**Step 1: Campaign Details**

*   **Fields**:
    
    *   **Campaign Title**
        
    *   **Description**
        
    *   **CTA Name** (e.g., "Join Now")
        
    *   **CTA Link** (destination URL)
        
    *   **Start Date** (campaigns commence at 12 PM UTC the following day)
        
    *   **End Date** (optional; defaults to running until budget exhaustion)
        
    *   **Audience Category** (e.g., DeFi, Gaming, NFT, DAO)
        
    

**Step 2: Upload Ad Creatives**

*   **Supported Banner Sizes**:
    
    *   Medium Rectangle: 300x250 px
        
    *   Banner: 468x60 px
        
    *   Wide Skyscraper: 160x600 px
        
    *   Leaderboard: 728x90 px
        
    *   Mobile Leaderboard: 320x50 px
        
    *   Mobile Banner: 300x50 px
        
    

\*Uploading all sizes is recommended for broader reach.\*

**Step 3: Budget & Compensation**

*   **Fields**:
    
    *   **Total Budget** (minimum $100)
        
    *   **Daily Cap** (percentage of total budget; optional)
        
    *   **CPC Rate** (minimum $0.10)
        
    *   **CPM Rate** (minimum $1.00)
        
    

**Notes**:

*   CPC is fixed; CPM may vary by ±20% based on placement size and relevance.
    
*   A 10% fee on total ad spend is applied to facilitate on-chain transactions and partner management; no commissions are charged to partners.
    

**Review & Launch**:

*   **Review**: Preview campaign details and assets.
    
*   **Funding**:
    
    *   If wallet balance is sufficient, allocate funds directly.
        
    *   If insufficient, add funds during campaign creation.
        
    
*   **Activation**: Campaigns start automatically at the scheduled time.
    
*   **Budget Rollover**: Unspent budget after campaign end is refunded to the advertiser's wallet.

# Campaign Management | Adtoken Docs

Effective campaign management is crucial for maximizing the impact of your advertising efforts on the AdToken platform. This section provides an overview of the tools and features available to advertisers for managing and optimizing their campaigns.

* * *

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/adtoken-docs/advertiser-guide/campaign-management#id-5.3.1-dashboard-navigation)

**5.3.1 Dashboard Navigation**

Upon logging into the AdToken platform, advertisers are presented with a user-friendly dashboard featuring a left-side navigation panel. The primary sections relevant to campaign management include:

1.  **My Balance**
    
2.  **My Campaigns**
    
3.  **Analytics Dashboard**
    
4.  **Asset Library**
    

Each section is detailed below:

* * *

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/adtoken-docs/advertiser-guide/campaign-management#id-5.3.2-my-balance)

**5.3.2 My Balance**

The **My Balance** section provides a comprehensive view of your financial status on the platform:

*   **Total Balance**: The aggregate amount of funds in your account.
    
*   **Available Balance**: Funds readily available for new campaigns or withdrawals.
    
*   **Allocated Balance**: Funds currently assigned to active campaigns.
    

**Actions Available**:

*   **Deposit Funds**: Add USDC (on the Sui network) to your account.
    
*   **Withdraw Funds**: Retrieve available funds back to your connected wallet.
    

**Transaction History**:

A detailed log of all financial transactions, including deposits, withdrawals, and allocations, is available for transparency and record-keeping.

* * *

#### 

[](https://adtoken-1.gitbook.io/adtoken-docs/advertiser-guide/campaign-management#id-5.3.3-my-campaigns)

**5.3.3 My Campaigns**

The **My Campaigns** section offers insights and management options for all your advertising campaigns:

**Overview Statistics**:

*   **Campaigns**: Displays the number of active campaigns alongside the total campaigns created.
    
*   **Budget ($)**: Shows the remaining budget in relation to the total allocated budget across all campaigns.
    
*   **Clicks**: Total number of clicks garnered by your ads.
    
*   **Views**: Total number of impressions or views your ads have received.
    
*   **Conversion Rate (CTR)**: The average click-through rate across all campaigns.
    

**Campaign Table**:

**5.4.1 Best Practices for Campaign Optimization**

1.  **Define Clear Goals and Metrics**
    
    *   **Objective Setting**: Clearly outline what you aim to achieve with your campaign—be it brand awareness, lead generation, or direct sales.
        
    *   **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**: Align your goals with measurable metrics such as click-through rates (CTR), conversion rates, or return on ad spend (ROAS).
        
    
    > _Example_: If the goal is lead generation, focus on metrics like cost per lead (CPL) and the number of qualified leads.
    
2.  **Align Strategy with the Customer Journey**
    
    *   **Awareness Stage**: Utilize broad targeting to introduce your brand to a wider audience.
        
    *   **Consideration Stage**: Implement retargeting strategies to engage users who have previously interacted with your brand.
        
    *   **Decision Stage**: Offer compelling calls-to-action (CTAs) to convert prospects into customers.
        
    
    > _Insight_: Tailoring your ad content to each stage of the customer journey enhances engagement and conversion rates.
    
3.  **Diversify Tactics, Channels, and Media Types**
    
    *   **Multi-Channel Approach**: Leverage various advertising channels to broaden reach and mitigate platform-specific risks.
        
    *   **Creative Variations**: Use a mix of ad formats—such as banners, videos, and native ads—to appeal to different audience preferences.
        
    
    > _Recommendation_: Regularly test new channels and ad formats to discover additional avenues for audience engagement.
    
4.  **Utilize Effective Tools and Technologies**
    
    *   **Analytics Platforms**: Employ robust analytics tools to monitor campaign performance and gather actionable insights.
        
    *   **Automation**: Use automated bidding and ad placement to optimize budget allocation and improve efficiency.
        
    
    > _Note_: The AdToken platform offers integrated analytics and automation features to streamline campaign management.
    
5.  **Continuously Test and Optimize**
    
    *   **A/B Testing**: Experiment with different ad creatives, headlines, and CTAs to identify what resonates best with your audience.
        
    *   **Performance Analysis**: Regularly review campaign data to pinpoint areas for improvement and implement necessary adjustments.
        
    
    > _Strategy_: Adopt a test-and-learn approach to continually refine your campaigns for better performance.
    
6.  **Focus on Proven Strategies**
    
    *   **Audience Targeting**: Refine your targeting criteria based on demographic data, user behavior, and engagement metrics.
        
    *   **Budget Allocation**: Allocate more resources to high-performing campaigns while scaling down underperforming ones.
        
    
    > _Tip_: Regularly update your targeting parameters to adapt to changing audience behaviors and market trends.
    
7.  **Implement Retargeting Campaigns**
    
    *   **Engagement**: Re-engage users who have previously interacted with your brand but haven't converted.
        
    *   **Personalization**: Deliver tailored ads that address the specific interests or behaviors of retargeted users.
        
    
    > _Benefit_: Retargeting can significantly improve conversion rates by focusing on an audience already familiar with your brand.

# Publisher FAQs | Adtoken Docs

This section addresses common questions and provides clarifications for publishers and partners using the AdToken platform.

* * *

**1\. Can I add multiple websites or accounts under the same profile?**

Yes, you can add multiple websites to your profile. After signing up, navigate to the **"My Websites"** section and use the **"Add Website"** feature to include additional sites. Each site will undergo an approval process before ads are served.

* * *

**2\. Do you support social links (like Twitter, YouTube) verification during sign-up or later?**

During sign-up, you can provide your social media links. Currently, we support Twitter verification, which can be completed later in the **"Edit Profile"** section. Support for additional platforms like YouTube will be added in the future.

* * *

**3\. How do I integrate the AdToken widget into my website?**

After your website is approved, an **API Key** will be generated. Use this key to integrate the AdToken widget by following the [Widget Integration Guide](https://github.com/AdToken-2024/widget_integration)
. This involves adding specific code snippets to your site's HTML where you want the ads to appear.

* * *

**4\. Are there any minimum requirements for website traffic to join as a publisher?**

While there isn't a strict minimum traffic requirement, providing accurate **monthly visitor** statistics during the website submission helps us assess and optimize ad placements effectively.

* * *

**5\. How long does the website approval process take?**

The approval process typically takes **24-48 hours**. For expedited approval, you can contact our administrators via the official Telegram channel.

* * *

**6\. How are earnings calculated for publishers?**

Earnings are calculated based on the **Cost Per Click (CPC)** and **Cost Per Mille (CPM)** models. Each click or impression on the ads displayed on your platform contributes to your earnings, which accumulate in real-time in your AdToken wallet.

* * *

**7\. Is there a minimum withdrawal limit for publishers?**

Yes, the minimum withdrawal threshold for publishers is **$10**. Once your available balance meets or exceeds this amount, you can initiate a withdrawal.

* * *

**8\. How long does it take to process a withdrawal request?**

Withdrawals are processed instantly, with funds typically reflecting in your connected wallet within **3-5 minutes**. Processing times may vary slightly depending on network conditions.

* * *

**9\. Can I participate in multiple campaigns simultaneously?**

Yes, you can participate in multiple campaigns at the same time. All active campaigns available for your audience category will be listed in the **"My Campaigns"** section, where you can choose to participate.

* * *

**10\. How can I track the performance of the campaigns I'm participating in?**

The **"Dashboard / Analytics"** section provides detailed insights into your performance, including metrics like impressions, clicks, earnings, device splits, and more. You can filter these analytics by specific websites or social profiles.

* * *

**11\. What should I do if I encounter issues with ad display or earnings discrepancies?**

If you experience any issues with ad displays or notice discrepancies in your earnings, please contact our support team via the official Telegram channel or email us at [admin@adtoken.com](mailto:admin@adtoken.co)
.

* * *

**12\. Are there any guidelines or restrictions on the content of my website to qualify as a publisher?**

Yes, websites must comply with our content guidelines, which prohibit illegal activities, adult content, and other inappropriate material. Detailed guidelines are available in the **"Terms and Conditions"** section of our website.

* * *

**13\. How do I update my profile information or add new social media links?**

You can update your profile information and add new social media links in the **"Edit Profile"** section of your dashboard. Ensure that all information is accurate to maintain account integrity.

* * *

**14\. What types of ad formats are supported by AdToken?**

AdToken supports various ad formats, including **banner ads**, **leaderboards**, and **medium rectangles**. Detailed specifications are available in the [Widget Integration Guide](https://github.com/AdToken-2024/widget_integration)
.

* * *

**15\. How can I prioritize certain campaigns over others?**

In the **"My Campaigns"** section, under the **"Actions"** column, you have the option to **"Prioritize"** specific campaigns. This allows you to focus on campaigns that align best with your audience or offer higher returns.

* * *

*   **Step 1: Basic Details & Wallet Connection**
    
    *   **Username:** Choose a unique identifier for your profile.
        
    *   **Wallet Connection:** Connect a Sui-compatible wallet; the public key serves as your unique identifier.
        
    *   **Social Links:** Provide links to your social profiles (e.g., Twitter). Verification can be completed later in the profile settings.
        
    *   **Point of Contact:** Enter contact details such as name and email.
        
    *   **Audience Category:** Select the category that best describes your audience (e.g., NFT, DeFi, Gaming, DAO).
        
    *   **Publisher Toggle:** If you wish to add your website immediately, enable this option to proceed to the next step. This step can be skipped and completed later if preferred.
        
    
*   **Step 2: Website Details (Optional)**
    
    *   **Website Link:** Enter the URL of your website.
        
    *   **Description:** Provide a brief description of your website.
        
    *   **Monthly Visitors:** Specify the average number of monthly visitors.
        
    *   **Continue:** Submit the information to complete the sign-up process.
        
    

**Post Sign-Up:**

*   **Profile Creation:** Your partner profile is created, with an optional publisher tag if website details were provided.
    
*   **Whitelisting:** All new profiles undergo a whitelisting process, typically completed within 24-48 hours. For expedited approval, contact our administrators via the official Telegram channel: [https://t.me/+AYVmBsDUnZk0ZDBl](https://t.me/+AYVmBsDUnZk0ZDBl)
    
*   **Dashboard Access:** Upon approval, you gain full access to the partner/publisher dashboard and tools.
