Welcome to the Four Meme Points Summer Season 1 campaign!

From **August 21st** **to October 20th, 2024**, complete various tasks on different platforms, accumulate points, and redeem them for exciting rewards including tokens and exclusive airdrops！

Here’s everything you need to know to make the most of this season.

#### 

[hashtag](https://four-meme.gitbook.io/four.meme/four.meme-points-summer-season-1#what-is-four.meme-points-summer-season-1)

**What is Four.Meme Points Summer Season 1?**

The Four.Meme Points Summer Season 1 is an event where participants can complete specific tasks on various platforms to earn points. These points can then be exchanged for valuable rewards, including tokens and special airdrops.

This campaign is part of our effort to boost Four.meme’s community engagement and promote our overall growth.

**How Do I Earn Points?**

You can earn points by completing various tasks. Points are accumulated based on the type of tasks you complete:

**Types of tasks**:

1.  **Basic Tasks**: These include daily logins, using core platform functions, and following our social media accounts. Completing these tasks earns you points.
    
    **1a) One-Time Tasks**: These can only be completed once during the campaign.
    
    **1b) Recurring Tasks**: These can be completed multiple times throughout the campaign.
    

**2\. Point-Boost Tasks**: These include actions like wallet connections, cross-chain swaps, and trading on DEXs. Completing these tasks earns you additional points based on a boost percentage.

#### 

[hashtag](https://four-meme.gitbook.io/four.meme/four.meme-points-summer-season-1#what-are-the-different-types-of-points)

**What are the different types of points?**

**Types of points:**

*   **Initial Points**: Existing users receive double points for specific tasks. For example, posting a comment earns 10 points.
    
*   **Task-Based Points**: Earn points by connecting wallets, completing swaps on DEX platforms, or participating in staking on specified platforms. These tasks have varying point values and may include additional bonuses.
    
*   **Boosted Points**: Some tasks offer points boosts, such as connecting specific wallets or using referral links. These boosts can be stacked, increasing your total points accumulation.

To qualify, you need to be a recent token creator or trader on pump.fun, or a BSC user who meets one of the following criteria: 1) top 500 on the Lista dao cac s2 leaderboard or 2) top 1000 in trading volume on PancakeSwap’s Orderbook Perps. Multi-Token Trading

With multi-token trading feature, when you create a token on Four.meme, you can now choose one of the supported tokens—$BNB, $USDT, $WHY, or $CAKE —as the trading pair in the internal liquidity pool. Once the pool reaches 100%, 20% of your tokens and all the collected supported tokens will be paired on PancakeSwap to establish liquidity.

# #5 Product Update 25-10-18 | Four.meme

[hashtag](https://four-meme.gitbook.io/four.meme/product-update/5-product-update-25-10-18#token-name-protection)

⚙️ Token Name Protection

**Overview** Token Name Protection prevents duplicate or similar token names in Fair Mode, keeping launches clean, fair, and original.

**🔧 How It Works**

*   When a token reaches 100+ holders during its bonding curve phase, its name and ticker are automatically locked for 72 hours.
    
*   During this 72-hour period, no new Fair Mode tokens can be created with the same or similar name/ticker.
    
*   The system checks both Fair Mode and Free Mode to prevent duplication across modes.
    
*   Free Mode tokens are not restricted by this rule and can use any name.
    

**💬 Notes**

*   If two tokens are created almost at the same time, both may succeed — this prevents bots from mass-locking names.
    
*   Once protection is triggered, it continues even after the token officially launches.
    
*   The 72-hour window and 100-holder threshold may be adjusted based on ecosystem feedback.
    

**💚 Purpose T**o ensure fairness, originality, and transparency — giving every meme project its own unique identity in the Four.Meme ecosystem.

🚀 Product Update: X Mode

####

**Overview**

Fair Mode has been upgraded and rebranded as **X Mode**, launching at **8 AM UTC, Oct 30**. X Mode delivers a fairer and smarter token launch experience — now open to **all wallets** for smoother participation and improved transparency.

#### 

[hashtag](https://four-meme.gitbook.io/four.meme/product-update#key-upgrades)

**⚙️ Key Upgrades**

**1️⃣ Dynamic Fee System** To ensure fair launches and counter bots, transaction fees automatically decrease block by block after token creation:

The parameters may be adjusted for future launches.

![](https://four-meme.gitbook.io/four.meme/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1270958763-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FMKYhtLfncF7vyCOOt0Ef%252Fuploads%252FhL57XIz1h89QDkCMXyWw%252Fphoto_2025-10-29_17-05-55.jpg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D98123e30-03dd-46b5-b3e2-5c0b74d9d612&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=666524b&sv=2)

**2️⃣ Automatic Buyback & Burn** After a token graduates from X Mode and its liquidity migrates to PancakeSwap:

*   All launch fees above 1% are used to **immediately buy the token and burn it**, permanently removing it from circulation.
    
*   For launches that don’t complete successfully, fees are redirected to a community address for later buy-and-burn or liquidity support.
