#### We’re hosting the first global Perplexity hackathon, powered by Perplexity’s Sonar API!

Sonar lets developers build apps with the best AI answer engine: a fast, affordable API offering with the features that millions of Perplexity users love, including real-time internet search and trusted citations.

Advanced Sonar offerings such as Sonar Reasoning, Sonar Reasoning Pro, and Sonar Deep Research let developers build custom research agents that support follow up questions and expose chain-of-thought.

We’re looking for developers to push the boundaries of what’s possible with Sonar and dream up entirely new product categories. If you’ve been thinking about shipping the “Perplexity for \_\_\_\_”, there’s never been a better time to start building.

**Check out the following:**

*   [More info about Sonar](https://sonar.perplexity.ai/)
    
*   [How to generate your API key and start building](https://docs.perplexity.ai/guides/getting-started)
    
*   [Sonar developer docs](https://docs.perplexity.ai/home)
    

### Requirements

#### What to Build

*   Build or update an app powered by [Perplexity’s Sonar API](https://docs.perplexity.ai/home)
    

#### What to Submit

*   Include a video (about 3 minutes) that demonstrates your submission. Videos must be uploaded to YouTube, Vimeo, or Facebook Video and made public.
    
    *   Judges will not be required to watch beyond 3 minutes
        
*   Provide a URL to your private code repository for judging and testing.
    
    *   Include a detailed README.md
    *   Share your private repo with [james.liounis@perplexity.ai](mailto:james.liounis@perplexity.ai)
         & [testing@devpost.com](mailto:testing@devpost.com)
        
*   Include an explanation of how Perplexity API was used.
    
*   Identify which category you are submitting into.
    
*   Your completed Devpost submission form.

 for full details.

### Judging Criteria

*   **Technological Implementation**  
    Does the project demonstrate quality software development? Does the project thoroughly leverage the required tool? How is the quality of the code?
*   **Design**  
    Is the user experience and design of the project well thought out? Is there a balanced blend of frontend and backend in the software?
*   **Potential Impact**  
    How big of an impact could the project have on the target community? How big of an impact could it have beyond the target community?
*   **Quality of the Idea**  
    How creative and unique is the project? Does the concept exist already? If so, how much does the project improve on it?

##### 6\. Judges & Criteria

Eligible submissions will be evaluated by a panel of judges selected by the Sponsor (the “Judges”). Judges may be employees of the Sponsor or third parties, may or may not be listed individually on the Hackathon Website, and may change before or during the Judging Period. Judging may take place in one or more rounds with one or more panels of Judges, at the discretion of the sponsor. 

Stage One) The first stage will determine via pass/fail whether the ideas meet a baseline level of viability, in that the Project reasonably fits the theme and reasonably applies the required APIs/SDKs featured in the Hackathon.

Stage Two) All Submissions that pass Stage One will be evaluated in Stage Two based on the following equally weighted criteria (the “Judging Criteria”):

Entries will be judged on the following equally weighted criteria, and according to the sole and absolute discretion of the judges:

*   Technological Implementation
    

Does the project demonstrate quality software development? Does the project thoroughly leverage the required tool? How is the quality of the code?

*   Design
    

Is the user experience and design of the project well thought out? Is there a balanced blend of frontend and backend in the software?

*   Potential Impact
    

How big of an impact could the project have on the target community? How big of an impact could it have beyond the target community?

*   Quality of the Idea
    

How creative and unique is the project? Does the concept exist already? If so, how much does the project improve on it?

The scores from the Judges will determine the potential winners of the applicable Prizes. The Entrant(s) that are eligible for a Prize, and whose Submissions earn the highest overall scores based on the applicable Judging Criteria, will become potential winners of that Prize.

Tie Breaking 

For each Prize listed below, if two or more Submissions are tied, the tied Submission with the highest score in the first applicable criterion listed above will be considered the higher scoring Submission. In the event any ties remain, this process will be repeated, as needed, by comparing the tied Submissions’ scores on the next applicable criterion. If two or more Submissions are tied on all applicable criteria, the panel of Judges will vote on the tied Submissions.

Frequently asked questions
--------------------------

Expand All | Collapse All

*   ### What is a Microsoft Experience Center?
    
    We have two Microsoft Experience Centers around the world. In New York City and Sydney these spaces serve all customers and enable them to experience our products and services with the opportunity to purchase select Microsoft products onsite.
    
*   ### What can I do at the Microsoft Experience Centers?
    
    At the Microsoft Experience Centers in New York and Sydney, you can browse products, see product demos, learn about our technology, and purchase products. And we can do more than just find the right device for you. From setup to training, our product experts are here to provide in-depth, one-on-one help when you need it.
    
    Feel free to bring in a device for service and repairs at our Answer Desk. You can book an Answer Desk appointment [online](https://aka.ms/AA979a)
    .  
    
*   ### How do I get help if I am unable to come in to the Microsoft Experience Center?
    
    We’re here to help when you need it. If you have a question about an experience, service, or purchase made at the Microsoft Experience Center in New York, you can contact us directly at [1 800-642-7676](tel:1%20800-642-7676)
    .
    
    You can also contact our Microsoft Store customer service team for help with quick questions and existing Microsoft Store orders. Get help at [Microsoft Store](https://support.microsoft.com/en-US/microsoft-store-and-billing?OCID=MEC23_NYC_FAQ)
    .
    
*   ### Can I return products at the Microsoft Experience Center?
    
    Yes, we’re able to assist customers with returns of items purchased at the Microsoft Experience Center. Returns for products purchased on [microsoft.com](https://aka.ms/AA979e)
     can also be processed through [Microsoft Support](https://aka.ms/AA979f)
    . Our Microsoft Experience Center associates are happy to answer your questions about the return process if needed.
    
*   ### Can I purchase products? Or buy-online-pickup-in-store (BOPIS)?
    
    Yes, customers will be able to purchase select Microsoft products onsite. For additional products not available at the Microsoft Experience Center, our team of associates will help customers purchase online at [microsoft.com](https://aka.ms/AA979e)
    . At this time, we do not offer buy-online-pickup-in-store at any of our Microsoft Experience Centers.
    
*   ### Will you reopen any other stores?
    
    We are focused on our existing Microsoft Experience Centers and investing in our ongoing digital transformation journey to create a best-in-class experience for our customers. We have no plans to reopen physical retail stores.
    
*   ### Will the gaming floors reopen to customers?
    
    Our gaming lounge will be open to the public with limited hours, and we will continue to follow health and public safety experts and guidance from local governments.
    
*   ### What are you doing to ensure employees and customers are safe at work?
    
    Our greatest priority is the health and safety of our employees and customers. We are implementing additional safety measures based on the guidance of health officials. These include limiting the number of customers within Microsoft Experience Centers and providing clear signage and instruction to facilitate safe distancing, eliminating in person events, providing masks and gloves to customers as needed, implementing additional cleaning procedures, and adding hand sanitation stands.
    
*   ### How do I redeem a gift card that can only be used in the physical Microsoft Store?
    
    Customers who have a Microsoft Store gift card can contact support at [1 800-642-7676](tel:%201%20800-642-7676)
     for a refund of the remaining value of the card.
