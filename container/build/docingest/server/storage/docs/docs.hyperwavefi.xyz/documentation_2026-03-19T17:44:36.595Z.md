# Fee implementation | Hyperwave

hwHLP is built on the Boring Vault architecture, which does not support performance fees natively and only supports management fees. To align hwHLP with its intended 10% performance fee, the management fee is dynamically adjusted at each reprice so that the net effect is mathematically equivalent to charging 10% of realized gains. Although the fee is technically implemented as a management fee, the economic outcome for users is the same as a 10% performance fee, and this can be verified on-chain.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-1-points-program/referral-faq#id-6.-can-i-activate-a-code-my-referred-hyperfren-has-created)

6\. Can I activate a code my referred Hyperfren has created?

Cheeky, but no you can't. You can't use the code your Hyperfren has created, or your Hyperfren's Hyperfren's Hyperfren.... you know what I mean, no referral loops.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-1-points-program/referral-faq#id-7.-how-are-the-referral-leaderboard-points-calculated)

7\. How are the "Referral Leaderboard" points calculated?

The Referred Wave Points represent the **sum** of the base Wave Points (from holding) earned by a user's Hyperfrens. If Alice (10 pts) refers Bob (10 pts) and Charlie (10 pts). Alice should appear on the leaderboard as 20 pts. If Charlie(10 pts) refers David (15 pts), then Alice still appears as 20pts but Charlie would appear as 15pts. David who was Charlie's referral doesn't count towards Alice so no compounding.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-1-points-program/referral-faq#id-8.-how-are-the-your-hyperfrens-points-calculated)

8\. How are the "Your Hyperfrens" points calculated?

These are just the base Wave Points your Hyperfrens have been earning by holding. It's there so you can see a breakdown of how much each of your Hyperfrens are earning.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-1-points-program/referral-faq#id-9.-what-does-referral-boost-mean-in-the-ui)

9\. What does "Referral boost" mean in the UI?

This is a calculation in Wave Points of the 5% you have earned from the Wave Points your Hyperfrens have earned during the campaign

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-1-points-program/referral-faq#id-10.-how-often-is-the-referral-leaderboard-refereshed)

10\. How often is the Referral Leaderboard refereshed?

Every 10 minutes, there's a timer in the bottom left.

# Technical Details | Hyperwave

Hyperwave’s Hyperwave HLP is built on the [boring-vaultarrow-up-right](https://github.com/Se7en-Seas/boring-vault)
 standard, a battle tested Vault standard currently facilitating ~3.5 Billion in TVL Across multiple vaults. The boring vault has been audited by [Spearbit arrow-up-right](https://spearbit.com/)
and [0xMacroarrow-up-right](https://0xmacro.com/)
. In addition to this periphery contracts have undergone further audits by [ThreeSigma arrow-up-right](https://threesigma.xyz/)
and [CodeSpectarrow-up-right](https://codespect.net/)
.

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/assets/hyperwave-hlp/technical-details#process-flow)

Process flow


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

*   User deposits USDe/USDT/USDHL to token contract
    
*   hwHLP is minted based on latest exchange rate
    
*   Hyperwave strategists swaps USDe/USDT0 for USDC & deposits into HLP Vault
    
*   Onchain oracle reads the state of vault reprices exchange rate

# Season 2 & Referral FAQ | Hyperwave

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program/season-2-and-referral-faq#id-1.-what-is-currently-in-season-2)

1\. What is currently in Season 2?

Currently you will earn Wave Points for hwHYPE/hwHLP use in DeFi (including holding) and the referral program which continues in Season 2, please check [points program](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program)
 for more updates during the Season.

The bonuses remain the same. 5% boost on your Wave Points for activating a Hyperfren Code and 5% on the combined Wave Points balance of your HyperFrens.

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program/season-2-and-referral-faq#id-2.-in-season-2-do-i-have-to-activate-my-hyperfren-code-and-refer-my-hyperfrens-again)

2\. In Season 2, do I have to activate my Hyperfren Code and refer my Hyperfrens again?

No, you retain your code activation and your Hyperfrens from Season 1.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program/season-2-and-referral-faq#id-3.-if-i-have-season-1-referral-points-will-they-continue-in-season-2)

3\. If I have Season 1 referral points will they continue in Season 2?

No, the points start again from 0 but your current Hyperfrens will continue to contribute to your bonus.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program/season-2-and-referral-faq#id-4.-when-does-the-referral-program-end)

It ends when the [points program](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program)
 ends.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program/season-2-and-referral-faq#id-4.-can-i-change-which-hyperfren-code-i-activate)

4\. Can I change which Hyperfren code I activate?

No you can't, once you sign it. It's there forever. Choose wisely.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program/season-2-and-referral-faq#id-5.-can-i-activate-a-code-my-referred-hyperfren-has-created)

5\. Can I activate a code my referred Hyperfren has created?

Cheeky, but no you can't. You can't use the code your Hyperfren has created, or your Hyperfren's Hyperfren's Hyperfren.... you know what I mean, no referral loops.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program/season-2-and-referral-faq#id-6.-how-are-the-referral-leaderboard-points-calculated)

6\. How are the "Referral Leaderboard" points calculated?

The Referred Wave Points represent the **sum** of the base Wave Points (from holding) earned by a user's Hyperfrens. If Alice (10 pts) refers Bob (10 pts) and Charlie (10 pts). Alice should appear on the leaderboard as 20 pts. If Charlie(10 pts) refers David (15 pts), then Alice still appears as 20pts but Charlie would appear as 15pts. David who was Charlie's referral doesn't count towards Alice so no compounding.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program/season-2-and-referral-faq#id-7.-how-are-the-your-hyperfrens-points-calculated)

7\. How are the "Your Hyperfrens" points calculated?

These are just the base Wave Points your Hyperfrens have been earning by holding. It's there so you can see a breakdown of how much each of your Hyperfrens are earning.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program/season-2-and-referral-faq#id-8.-what-does-referral-boost-mean-in-the-ui)

8\. What does "Referral boost" mean in the UI?

This is a calculation in Wave Points of the 5% you have earned from the Wave Points your Hyperfrens have earned during the campaign

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/dao/season-2-points-program/season-2-and-referral-faq#id-9.-how-often-is-the-referral-leaderboard-refereshed)

9\. How often is the Referral Leaderboard refereshed?

Every 10 minutes, there's a timer in the bottom left.

# Referral bonus | Hyperwave

1% of the supply is allocated linearly to the top 100 users on the S1 referral leaderboard, according to how many Wave Points the people they referred earned (Referral Points).

To make the distribution more balanced, these adjustments were applied:

1.  The maximum number of referral points taken into account for each referrer is 40 million (twice the average of the top 100).
    
2.  Those in the top 100 who referred 20 or more users get full credit for their referral points, while those with fewer than 20 referrals receive partial credit (between 50% and 100%) based on the number of referred users.
    
3.  Addresses identified as sybils were removed from the leaderboard.
    
4.  Addresses with one or two referrals were removed to prevent disproportionate rewards from self-referrals or single large wallets.

# hwHLP Integrations | Hyperwave

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers/hwhlp-integrations#overview)

Overview

`hwHLP` (Hyperwave HLP) is the first liquid, yield-bearing token representing shares in the Hyperliquidity Provider (HLP) vault on HyperEVM. `hwHLP` enables composability and liquidity for HLP positions while automatically accruing yield from market-making, liquidations, and trading fees.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers/hwhlp-integrations#contract-architecture)

Contract Architecture

The `hwHLP` system uses a modular architecture with the following key contracts:

*   **Boring Vault**: Main vault contract (ERC20 + custom logic)
    
*   **Teller**: Users deposit assets through the Teller contract
    
*   **Accountant**: Handles exchange rate and fee accounting
    
*   **AtomicQueue**: Manages deposits/withdrawals
    
*   **Manager**: Manages vault strategies with Merkle verification
    
*   **Roles Authority**: Role-based access control

Basic Deposit

Users deposit assets through the `Teller` contract to receive `hwHLP` tokens.

![](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F2213769390-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBrgaYIcgXLJRhZfsWo0V%252Fuploads%252Fq6cX9EC9fslA4HipmFbQ%252FDeposit-flow.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D65c2ac64-af96-470c-b6b0-bdc274daccc4&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=239a92d2&sv=2)

#### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers/hwhlp-integrations#step-1-calculate-minimum-mint-amount)

**Step 1: Calculate Minimum Mint Amount**

First, you need to call the `**AccountantWithRateProviders**` `getRateInQuoteSafe()` method [https://hyperevmscan.io/address/0x78e3ac5bf48dcaf1835e7f9861542c0d43d0b03e#readContract#F7arrow-up-right](https://hyperevmscan.io/address/0x78e3ac5bf48dcaf1835e7f9861542c0d43d0b03e#readContract#F7)

Copy

    minimumMint = depositAmount * 10**6 / accountant.getRateInQuoteSafe(assetAddress)

circle-info

The deposit amount must be in decimal form of the deposit asset. Refer to the list for decimals. hwHLP has 6 decimals, so the formula normalizes to this precision.

#### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers/hwhlp-integrations#step-2-approve-asset-to-the-hwhlp-vault)

Step 2: Approve Asset to the `hwHLP` vault

The user needs to approve the `depositAsset` to the `hwHLP` contract ([`0x9FD7466f987Fd4C45a5BBDe22ED8aba5BC8D72d1`arrow-up-right](https://hyperevmscan.io/address/0x9FD7466f987Fd4C45a5BBDe22ED8aba5BC8D72d1#code)
)

Copy

    // Approve Vault to spend your deposit asset
    IERC20(depositAsset).approve(BORING_VAULT, depositAmount);

#### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers/hwhlp-integrations#step-3-call-deposit)

Step 3: Call Deposit

To deposit into the vault, call the `deposit()` method on the `TellerWithMultiAssetSupport` contract:

`depositAsset` = address of the asset you want to deposit

`depositAmount` = the amount you want to deposit with the decimal of the deposit asset

`minimumMint` = calculated in [Step 1arrow-up-right](https://www.notion.so/hwHLP-Integration-Documentation-External-23011e01a88380bbb72dc73190728fde?pvs=21)

[HyperEVM Mainnet address details for 0xfEFF6652e393Df46f88CDAcF5cd05DBbb227214arrow-up-right](https://www.hyperscan.com/address/0xfEFF6652e393Df46f88CDAcF5cd05DBbb227214e?tab=read_write_contract#0x0efe6a8b)

![](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F2213769390-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FBrgaYIcgXLJRhZfsWo0V%252Fuploads%252FuyW1hSES0P9Y6CfBXEUp%252Fdeposit-func.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Da3e5ad8e-ed0b-403a-92d5-116e7fc57354&width=768&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=f274704f&sv=2)

    interface ITeller {

    // Execute deposit

2\. Deposit and Bridge Flow

(Deposit on one chain and mint `hwHLP` on another supported chain)

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/assets/hyperwave-hlp/redemption-guide#redemption-faq)

Redemption FAQ

**Why is there a queue?**

When someone requests a withdrawal, the request is placed in a queue. The reason is that assets in the vault are deployed in the HLP vault, and therefore may not be immediately liquid. The queue ensures fairness, prevents certain attack vectors (especially MEV or arbitrage), and allows bulk processing / batching of withdrawals. It also helps smooth out liquidity demands.

**Should I adjust the slippage tolerance?**

hwHLP withdrawals trigger HLP withdrawals and swap events in Hypercore, which cause slippage and incur trading fees. We generally recommend keeping the default slippage tolerance of 0.15. If you lower this, the request may still be filled, but not always, as it depends on whether the vault shares accrue enough to cover these costs within the 5-day period.

**Can I cancel my withdrawal?**

On withdrawing, you must wait a minimum of one hour before cancellation is enabled. Similarly, you must wait 1h before starting another withdrawal. This helps prevent abuse and protect the vault from manipulation.

**How many withdrawals can I make at once?** Only one.

Basic Deposit

Users deposit assets through the `Teller` contract to receive `hwHLP` tokens.

#### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers#step-1-calculate-minimum-mint-amount)

**Step 1: Calculate Minimum Mint Amount**

First, you need to call the `**AccountantWithRateProviders**` `getRateInQuoteSafe()` method [https://hyperevmscan.io/address/0x78e3ac5bf48dcaf1835e7f9861542c0d43d0b03e#readContract#F7arrow-up-right](https://hyperevmscan.io/address/0x78e3ac5bf48dcaf1835e7f9861542c0d43d0b03e#readContract#F7)

Copy

    minimumMint = depositAmount * 10**6 / accountant.getRateInQuoteSafe(assetAddress)

circle-info

The deposit amount must be in decimal form of the deposit asset. Refer to the list for decimals. hwHLP has 6 decimals, so the formula normalizes to this precision.

#### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers#step-2-approve-asset-to-the-hwhlp-vault)

Step 2: Approve Asset to the `hwHLP` vault

The user needs to approve the `depositAsset` to the `hwHLP` contract ([`0x9FD7466f987Fd4C45a5BBDe22ED8aba5BC8D72d1`arrow-up-right](https://hyperevmscan.io/address/0x9FD7466f987Fd4C45a5BBDe22ED8aba5BC8D72d1#code)
)

Copy

    // Approve Vault to spend your deposit asset
    IERC20(depositAsset).approve(BORING_VAULT, depositAmount);

#### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers#step-3-call-deposit)

Step 3: Call Deposit

To deposit into the vault, call the `deposit()` method on the `TellerWithMultiAssetSupport` contract:

`depositAsset` = address of the asset you want to deposit

`depositAmount` = the amount you want to deposit with the decimal of the deposit asset

`minimumMint` = calculated in [Step 1arrow-up-right](https://www.notion.so/hwHLP-Integration-Documentation-External-23011e01a88380bbb72dc73190728fde?pvs=21)

4\. Withdrawal Flow(Only on HyperEVM Right Now)


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Withdrawals use the _AtomicQueue_ contract with a delayed execution model.

### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers#step-1-create-withdrawal-request)

Step 1: Create Withdrawal Request

Copy

    interface IAtomicQueue {
        struct AtomicRequest {
            address offer;              // hwHLP address
            address want;               // Asset to receive (USDe/USDT0/USDHL)
            uint64 deadline;            // Expiry timestamp
            uint256 minPrice;           // Minimum exchange rate
            uint256 offerAmount;        // hwHLP amount to withdraw
        }
        
        function safeUpdateAtomicRequest(
            address offer,
            address want,
            AtomicRequest calldata userRequest
        ) external;
    }

#### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers#parameters)

Parameters

*   **offer**: `hwHLP` address
    
*   **want**: Desired asset address (USDe/USDT0/USDHL, see [listarrow-up-right](https://www.notion.so/hwHLP-Integration-Documentation-External-23011e01a88380bbb72dc73190728fde?pvs=21)
    )
    
*   **deadline**: `block.timestamp + 5 days` (recommended)
    
*   **atomicPrice**: Get from `accountant.getRateInQuote(wantAsset)`
    
*   **offerAmount**: Amount of `hwHLP` to withdraw
    
*   **discount**: Slippage tolerance in basis points
    
    *   15 bps (1500) for `USDe/USDT0`
        
    *   20 bps (2000) for `USDHL`
        
    

#### 

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers#important-notes)

Important Notes

*   Withdrawals have a processing delay (typically executed within 5 days)
    
*   The discount parameter accounts for hypercore trading fees and potential slippage
    
*   Users must wait for their request to be fulfilled by solvers
    
*   You need to **let the user** call the request **from their own wallet**

[hashtag](https://docs.hyperwavefi.xyz/developers#key-considerations)

Key Considerations


---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  **Decimals**: `hwHLP` has 6 decimals - ensure proper decimal handling in calculations
    
2.  **Rate Queries**: Always use `getRateInQuoteSafe()` for current operations
    
3.  **Slippage**: Set appropriate minimum amounts for deposits and discounts for withdrawals
    
4.  **Gas for Bridging**: Always simulate bridge transactions to determine accurate gas requirements
    
5.  **Withdrawal Delays**: Inform users that withdrawals are not instant and may take up to 5 days
