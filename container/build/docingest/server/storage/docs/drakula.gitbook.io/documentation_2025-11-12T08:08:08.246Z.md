# Swarms Explained | Drakula Docs

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/swarms-explained#the-technical-architecture-behind-swarms)

The Technical Architecture Behind Swarms


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

> If EVERY rule passes, the call enters. If ANY rule fails, the call is skipped.

#### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/swarms-explained#what-is-a-swarm)

What is a Swarm?

A Swarm is a packaged trading strategy that monitors sources you connect, applies the filters you configure, and tracks which tokens qualify based on your criteria.

Think of it as your personal trading team that never sleeps — aggregating calls from your Telegram channels, private groups, and X accounts, then filtering each token through your custom strategy. Only tokens passing all conditions enter your Swarm's qualified pool, where they're tracked for backtesting and ready for Auto-Bat.

#### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/swarms-explained#how-swarms-work)

How Swarms Work?

**Step 0: Data Aggregation**

Drakula monitors & aggregates data from:

*   3000+ public Telegram channels (auto-tracked)
    
*   Your connected private Telegram groups/channels
    
*   X accounts you link (KOLs, traders)
    

All token mentions (by link or CA) are captured 24/7, in real-time, **where each data point** is timestamped to the millisecond for accurate lifecycle tracking.

Raw messages get processed through our engine to separate calls from the rest, looking for:

*   Token contract address
    
*   Any trading link for the token
    

Afterwards it extracts:

*   Call timestamp (millisecond precision)
    
*   Caller identity (user/bot/admin)
    
*   Message context (topic, type: reply/forward/bot/admin)
    

Then instantly queries on-chain data, such as MC, liquidity, volume... [+32 more](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/swarms-explained#on-chain-data-we-request-for-every-call)
.

**Step 1: Data Parsing**

**Step 2: Data Sorting & Classification**

Drakula automatically tracks the performance lifecycle of every call in the system.

*   Entry point: Price/MC at first call
    
*   Peak performance: ATH and timestamp
    
*   ROI across timeframes: 5m, 15m, 1h, 4h, 24h, 7d
    
*   Outcome: Win, Loss, Rug, Honeypot, No Movement
    

This creates an immutable, verifiable record of every call and source's performance.

Strategists define [custom filters](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/swarms-explained#on-chain-data-we-request-for-every-call)
 that tokens must pass before entering a Swarm.

Tokens flow through them in real-time once Drakula gets the call. For example:

Copy

    IF 3+ sources call the same token
    AND MC < $100k
    AND Liquidity > $20k
    AND Pair Age < 12h
    AND Not Honeypot
    
    THEN → Token enters the Swarm
    ELSE → Token is ignored

Only tokens passing **every** criteria enter your Swarm's accepted pool, visible in backtests and ready for Auto-Bat execution if you choose to activate it.

**Step 3: Filter Application** (The Strategy Layer)

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/swarms-explained#what-can-swarms-offer-you)

**Automation.** Your Swarm monitors sources 24/7, applying your filters to every incoming call automatically. What would take 15 hours of manual tracking daily, happens in milliseconds.

**Privacy.** Your filter logic and source combinations stay encrypted. Followers see your results, not your strategy.

Channel Stats


---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

> You shouldn't rely on trust when it comes to sources. Drakula shows you the verified record, so you can **know** who's the best.

* * *

### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/channel-stats#what-are-channel-stats)

What are Channel Stats?

Channel Stats track every single call from every source in the system. Telegram channels, X accounts, your private groups & even users — all logged permanently.

Every token KOLs mention get tracked from the moment of call. All public. All verifiable.

You finally know who to follow and who to ignore.

* * *

### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/channel-stats#what-gets-tracked-for-each-source)

What Gets Tracked For Each Source

Metric

What It Means

**Total Calls**

Every token mentioned

**Wins**

Calls that hit 2x+

**Losses**

Calls that didn't hit 2x

**Win Rate**

% of calls that won (hit 2x+)

**Avg ROI**

Avg multiplier across all calls

**Highest ROI**

Best single call

**Rugs**

Tokens that rugged after call

### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/channel-stats#every-call-gets-logged-the-second-its-posted)

Every call gets logged the second it's posted.

Someone posts a token in Telegram. We capture it instantly:

*   Who posted it (admin? bot? user? which user?)
    
*   When they posted it
    
*   The token's price at that moment
    

Then we track that token for the next 30 days:

*   Did it 2x? 5x? 100x?
    
*   Did it rug?
    

Everything gets recorded under that source's stats. Updated in real-time. No deleting. No editing. No hiding bad calls.

### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/channel-stats#you-filter-by-performance)

You Filter By Performance

Building a Swarm? You pick sources based on real data.

Before Drakula:

*   You guess based on follower count
    
*   You trust whoever posted a 10x yesterday
    
*   You have no idea who's actually consistent
    

With Channel Stats:

*   Sort your sources by win rate
    
*   Filter out sources with high # of rugs
    
*   Add only sources that post consistently

**How does auto-trading work if you don't hold my keys?**[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/roadmap-and-faq/faq#how-does-auto-trading-work-if-you-dont-hold-my-keys)

Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs). Think of it as a secure enclave on our servers that can execute trades on your behalf without us ever seeing your private keys.

Here's how it works:

1.  You create a session key with limits (e.g., " 0.5 SOL per trade, these Swarms")
    
2.  This session key gets loaded into a TEE - hardware-isolated, tamper-proof environment
    
3.  TEE signs trades within your **approved** limits
    
4.  All execution happens inside the secure enclave - we can't access it, modify it, or extract keys from it
    

You approve once. TEE executes within boundaries.

How Auto-Bat Works

#### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/auto-bat-mechanics#step-0-signal)

Step 0: Signal

A token gets called by one of the sources of the Swarm & it passed all of the Swarm's filters.

Auto-Bat gets the signal the same second — and creates an order.

You define when Auto-Bat buys:

**Immediately.** Places the market order that **e**xecutes as soon as a signal qualifies

**Buy on Dip.** Places a dip order and waits for the price to drop X% from the time of the call.

#### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/auto-bat-mechanics#step-1-entry)

Step 1: Entry

#### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/auto-bat-mechanics#step-2-position)

Step 2: Position

You choose how much to invest. The Auto-bat will by {entry amount} of every token that qualifies for the Swarm. You can see in the Swarm data how many calls it had on average in the last 3 / 7 / 14 / 30 days to estimate how much you'd like to invest.

You also define when Auto-Bat sells:

**Take Profits.** Set as many as you want. Want to sell 30% at 2x, then 50% at 3x & the rest at 5x — you got it.

**Stop Losses.** A.k.a. your risk management. When the price drops by X% from the time of your entry (or ATH after entry if using trailing) — sell X% of your tokens.

#### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/auto-bat-mechanics#step-3-exit)

Step 3: Exit

#### 

[](https://drakula.gitbook.io/drakula-docs/ml2CMO3U9wrXU0iHnveq/drakula-breakdown/auto-bat-mechanics#step-4-optimization)

Step 4: Optimization

Auto-Bat allows you to set your desired gas & slippage separately for buys & sells for maximum precision.

**Gas.** Choose between Low / Med / High or Auto. The latter monitors network conditions, executes on low gas when possible, and pays premium only when necessary.

**Slippage.** Sets max slippage tolerance for the token price. Auto will adjust based on volatility but you can use a custom setting if you're confident.

What Auto-Bat Can Offer You

**Speed.** Auto-Bat trades 24/7. Every qualified signal gets executed — whether you're at work, at dinner, or asleep. The Swarm you chose catches a 100k MC token at 2am. Auto-Bat buys at your 20% dip target. You wake up to a 3x.

**Consistency.** Your rules are locked before the trade. No FOMO buying tops. No panic selling bottoms. Token pumps 200% in 10 minutes. You'd normally ape in. Auto-Bat ignores it — doesn't match your dip entry rule.

**Discipline.** Every trade follows the same risk rules. No more betting 0.05 SOL on a 10x then 2 SOL on a rug. Every trade = 0.15 SOL. Win or lose, your risk stays consistent. No emotional sizing.

**Automation.** Take profit automatically at your targets. Cut losses at your stops. Zero emotional decisions. Token hits 2.1x. You'd hold for 10x. Auto-Bat sells 70% at 2x like you programmed. Token rugs an hour later — you're still up.

**Focus.** Your strategy runs itself. You focus on life, not copy-pasting contract addresses at 2am. 14 qualified signals while you're at dinner with family, Auto-Bat executes all of them. You check the results when convenient for you.
