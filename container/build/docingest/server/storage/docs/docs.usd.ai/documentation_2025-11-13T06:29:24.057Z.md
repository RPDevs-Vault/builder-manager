# Buy / Stake | USD.AI

[](https://docs.usd.ai/app-guide/buy-stake#buying-or-redeeming-usdusdai)

Buying or Redeeming $USDai


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

USDai does not accrue yield. Stake USDai to earn yield on deposits.

1.  Select the [**BUY**](https://app.usd.ai/buy)
     tab in the transaction modal
    
2.  Select the Currency and Network ($USDai for Redeem)
    
3.  Enter the Amount
    
4.  Review the Terms
    
5.  Click `Approve` to allow the USDai contract to use your tokens
    
6.  Click `Buy`
    

USDai is 100% backed by yield bearing stablecoins, so it can always be instantly redeemed for any of the deposit currencies.

* * *

[](https://docs.usd.ai/app-guide/buy-stake#staking-your-usdusdai)

Staking your $USDai


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The USDai unstaking period is approximately 30 days.

1.  Select the [**STAKE**](https://app.usd.ai/stake)
     tab in the transaction modal
    
2.  Select the Amount of $USDai you want to stake
    
3.  Review the Terms
    
4.  Click `Approve` to allow the USDai contract to spend your $USDai
    
5.  Click `Stake`
    

USDai to sUSDai is not a 1:1 conversion as sUSDai is the yield-bearing token that passively earns income. When staking USDai, you will receive a lower amount of sUSDai and when unstaking sUSDai you should receive a higher amount of USDai.

[](https://docs.usd.ai/app-guide/buy-stake#unstaking-your-usdsusdai)

Unstaking your $sUSDai


------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Select the [**UNSTAKE**](https://app.usd.ai/unstake)
     subtab within the **STAKE** tab in the transaction modal
    
2.  Select the Amount of $sUSDai you want to unstake
    
3.  Review the Terms
    
4.  Click `Approve` to allow the USDai contract to spend your $sUSDai
    
5.  Click `Unstake`
    
6.  Go to Activity and click `Withdraw` USDai once the unstaking period ends
    

* * *

[](https://docs.usd.ai/app-guide/buy-stake#bridging-your-usdusdai-or-usdsusdai)

Bridging your $USDai or $sUSDai


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

USDai can be bridged to/from any chain

*   Mint on Abritrum or Ethereum or any other EVM chain available in the future
    

sUSDai can be bridged to/from any chain

*   Stake USDai for sUSDai on Arbitrum
    
*   Mint on Ethereum or any other EVM chain available in the future
    

1.  Navigate to the [BRIDGE](https://app.usd.ai/bridge)
     page on the left sidebar
    
2.  Select the token and network you want to bridge from
    
3.  Select the Amount of $USDai or $sUSDai you want to bridge
    
4.  Review the Terms
    

*   Click `Approve` to allow the contract to spend your $USDai or $sUSDai
    
*   Click `Bridge`

# Borrow (+Repay) | USD.AI

Borrowing via USD.AI is not publicly available. Reach out to the USD.AI team on Telegram if you would like to inquire or get more information.

[](https://docs.usd.ai/app-guide/borrow-+repay#borrowing)

Borrowing


------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Select [BORROW](https://app.usd.ai/borrow)
     page in the Navigation Bar
    
2.  View all available assets to borrow under UPCOMING LOANS (bottom section)
    
3.  Click `Borrow` to open the Borrow interface
    

![](https://docs.usd.ai/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F81871421-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F1xUmTJppLJHObr52ucZQ%252Fuploads%252FAi21XzGIGvsajlp53xMW%252Fborrow.svg%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D25ba2987-57e6-495a-aae4-64ee5b28878d&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=621da57f&sv=2)

1.  Review the Borrow Terms
    

**Borrow Terms**

*   Asset Details = List of NFTs availlable to borrow
    
*   Collateral Value = Total value of collateral
    
*   \# of Assets = # of assets used as collateral for loan
    
*   Currency = Borrow currency for loan
    
*   Principal = Amount being borrowed for loan
    
*   Term = Length of loan
    
*   APR = Interest rate paid by borrower for loan
    

1.  Review the Repayment Terms
    

**Repayment Terms**

*   Maturity Date = Date the final loan payment is due
    
*   Repayment Frequency = How often principal and interest payments must be made
    
*   Repayment Amount = The amount of principal and interest to be paid each period
    
*   Default Grace Period = Amount of time after each repayment period the borrower has to prevent a default on a loan (once the grace period ends, the borrower will default)
    
*   Grace Period APR = Interest rate the borrower must pay during the Default Grace Period
    

1.  Complete all transactions to Borrow
    

* * *

[](https://docs.usd.ai/app-guide/borrow-+repay#making-repayments)

Making Repayments


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

![](https://docs.usd.ai/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F81871421-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F1xUmTJppLJHObr52ucZQ%252Fuploads%252FCT15Jwam2eSNufC3sMfO%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D9da64392-aef4-403c-802c-47cd90e5ba0f&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=7c38e4f0&sv=2)

2.  Click `Make Payment` to view the current loan details
    

![](https://docs.usd.ai/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F81871421-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F1xUmTJppLJHObr52ucZQ%252Fuploads%252FQRXini8cimG51O3olq3b%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D82e65280-b640-4cb6-be86-f8c0adf8bdd0&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=6522d969&sv=2)

**Loan Status**

*   Current = Loan is current as all repayments have been made on time
    
*   Default Grace Period = The current repayment is late and the user must make repayment to avoid default (days remaining shown next to Default Grace Period)
    
*   Default = Repayment was not completed in time and the borrower has defaulted
    

1.  Review the repayment details and complete all transactions to Repay

# Loans (FiLo) | USD.AI

Deposits into a USD.AI loan pool are not publicly available. The only two parties that can deposit into the loan pool are:

1.  The First Loss curator wallet
    
2.  The USD.AI protocol
    

![](https://docs.usd.ai/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F81871421-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F1xUmTJppLJHObr52ucZQ%252Fuploads%252FiScbLqUJu404v6nOBffp%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D5f747307-a4c3-4e1a-a6a1-b98e90607d16&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=a8a01c4b&sv=2)

Anyone can view the loans, terms, tranches that are being funded by FiLo and USD.AI by selecting View Loan on the [LOANS](https://app.usd.ai/loans)
 page.

Active Loans = Loans that have been funded and are currently outstanding

Upcoming Loans = Loans expected to be closed within 30 days

![](https://docs.usd.ai/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F81871421-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F1xUmTJppLJHObr52ucZQ%252Fuploads%252FvtKZPx4kHoeajtQEDwTn%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dd905461f-3bb8-4bb1-a287-d671235c1e3f&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=23b949c2&sv=2)
