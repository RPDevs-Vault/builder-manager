# │ The Math | gliquid

![Page cover](https://gliquids-organization.gitbook.io/gliquid/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1520101409-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FJaM2kplq7JUt4adtRI9U%252Fuploads%252FTZxV3Nej4jcdYEUBORdo%252Fbg-2.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D66055a23-d313-4e72-a10a-5356d85503d5&width=1248&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=e9968bc4&sv=2)

To start off, the base points per hour of each pool are defined as:

Base Points per Hour (pool)\=10.000⋅Multiplier (pool)\\text{Base Points per Hour} \\ (pool) = 10.000 \\cdot Multiplier \\ (pool)Base Points per Hour (pool)\=10.000⋅Multiplier (pool)

Multipliers are pool-dependant and defined by the protocol to represent the trading activity and liquidity of each pool — pools with higher multipliers receive more points.

You can always check a pool's multiplier at the [app](https://www.gliquid.xyz/swap)

These points are fixed and limited, which means they are and simple and yet reward liquidity providers who add earlier.

Then, your share gets computed:

Share\=User FeesPool FeesShare = \\frac{\\text{User Fees}}{\\text{Pool Fees}}Share\=Pool FeesUser Fees​

And your raw points per hour also get calculated:

User Points per Hour\=Share ⋅ Base Points per Hour (pool)\\text{User Points per Hour} = Share \\ \\cdot \\ \\text{Base Points per Hour} \\ (pool)User Points per Hour\=Share ⋅ Base Points per Hour (pool)

And boosts explained earlier get applied:

Final Points per Hour\=User Points per Hour ⋅ (1+∑Boosts)\\text{Final Points per Hour} = \\text{User Points per Hour} \\ \\cdot \\ (1+ \\sum Boosts) Final Points per Hour\=User Points per Hour ⋅ (1+∑Boosts)

* * *

Its very important to understand, points are calculated at the backend; behind scenes. The UI is designed to update the points live (in a nearly seconds matter), but due to the big volume these can stop or lag time to time. This doesnt mean your points arent being added.

If your points fail to update on the app's UI, please, just wait and let our system calculate them and display them. Waiting for a few hours or reconnecting your wallet can help

* * *

Let's say we want to calculate the points earned for 4 hours in a pool. _We will do 4 hours and not 24 for sake of simplicity, but it would work the same._

For each hour, the pool will generate some fees - and our positions will too. By computing the ratio between those two fees we will get our share. Our points then, are just the share times the points earned by the entirety of the pool. _Assuming a multiplier of 1x in this case_ **it would be 8000 points**

To finally obtain the points earned in a day or in a week, we just sum the points earned in each hour

### 

[](https://gliquids-organization.gitbook.io/gliquid/about-us/points-2.0/the-math#the-average-issue)

The "average" issue

It's very important to note **you can't estimate your daily points by just dividing between your user fees and the pool fees**. The "sum of ratios" (points in each hour) it's different than the "total ratio" (points daily)

In the before example, if we calculated the points by doing:

Points\=100+50300+100∗10000\=3750Points = \\frac{100 + 50}{300+ 100} \* 10000 = 3750Points\=300+100100+50​∗10000\=3750

We would **get 3750 points.**

This effect is aggravated in pools with low activity and low liquidity, due to the nature of [non-linearity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nonlinear_system)

Context

Gliquid's new points system was created to offer incentives where they really matter and open the doors to new communities.

The system is based on the user's own activity, and the **basic requirement for accumulating points is through the generation of trading fees.**

To participate in this system, **you only need to create your LP and generate trading fees**. **Staking is not required.**

Farming was possible and existed in Season 1, but was optimized and removed

* * *

### 

[](https://gliquids-organization.gitbook.io/gliquid/about-us/points-2.0#emissions)

Emissions

We will quickly explain how points are generated and distributed, for the complete math refer to [the math](https://gliquids-organization.gitbook.io/gliquid/about-us/points-2.0/the-math)

1.  The base pool points per hour are generated depending on the pool's specific multiplier
    
2.  Each user has a share (a part) of the pool fees depending on its personal fees
    
3.  Your final points per hour obtained are proportional to your boosts, your shares, and the base pool points per hour
    

Season 1 balances are multiplied by a single scaling factor to align with the new hourly emission rate—no user action required.

It's very important to note points are based on hours, this means that **doing an average of 24 hours to estimate your points won't work**. This phenomena is also more explained in [the math.](https://gliquids-organization.gitbook.io/gliquid/about-us/points-2.0/the-math)

# │ Points | Gliquid

![Page cover](https://gliquids-organization.gitbook.io/jojo-gliquid/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcontent.gitbook.com%2Fcontent%2F0zH2jBOdorahs3onXh86%2Fblobs%2FFLeUxCk3BoTza6YFC7o7%2FGliquid%2520BG%25201920%252002.png&width=1248&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=43444448&sv=2)

### 

[](https://gliquids-organization.gitbook.io/jojo-gliquid/mechanisms/points#context)

Context

Gliquid's new points system was created to offer incentives where they really matter and open the doors to new communities.

The system is based on the user's own activity, and the **basic requirement for accumulating points is through the generation of trading fees.**

To participate in this system, **you only need to create your LP and generate trading fees**.

* * *

### 

[](https://gliquids-organization.gitbook.io/jojo-gliquid/mechanisms/points#point-emissions)

Point Emissions

We will quickly explain how points are generated and distributed, but full details will not be public this season.

1.  Points are generated from the fees you generate for the DEX. **\+ Fees = + Points**
    
2.  There may be pools with a higher multiplier than another so that the generation of points is higher.
    
3.  Certain boosts can be applied by performing actions within the app or certain milestones.
    

The user's pGLQ balance is taken into account.

Points are calculated hour by hour in our system, however they are sent to the user **every 24 hours.**

**For points to appear in the interface, the user must claim the fees earned.** If this is not done, points will continue to accumulate until the user claims their fees and they appear in the immediate daily distribution.

* * *

[](https://gliquids-organization.gitbook.io/jojo-gliquid/mechanisms/points#id-2--referral-mechanics)
