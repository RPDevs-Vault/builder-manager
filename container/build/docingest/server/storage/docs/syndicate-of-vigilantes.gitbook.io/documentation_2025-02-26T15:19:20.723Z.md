# Market Analysis | Syndicate of Vigilantes

The digital gaming industry has experienced exponential growth over the last decade, becoming one of the most dynamic sectors of the digital economy. As of 2025, the global gaming market is projected to exceed $200 billion, with a user base that spans across all continents and demographics. This surge is fueled by technological advancements, increasing internet penetration, and the rising popularity of mobile gaming.

Syndicate of Vigilantes positions itself within this booming market by integrating blockchain technology to provide a unique value proposition: a gaming platform where entertainment meets real economic benefits. Our target audience includes not only traditional gamers but also crypto enthusiasts and AR tech-savvy individuals who are looking for more than just gameplay; they seek a platform where their gaming skills and strategic decisions can lead to competitive rewards.

**Current Trends in Gaming** The gaming industry is at the forefront of technological innovation, increasingly blending immersive experiences with emerging technologies like blockchain. Here we outline several key trends that validate and highlight the potential of Syndicate of Vigilantes: **Augmented Reality** The Augmented Reality (AR) gaming market is a rapidly growing sector, driven by the widespread adoption of AR-enabled devices like smartphones and smart glasses. Syndicate of Vigilantes leverages AR technology to create immersive, real-world experiences, bringing digital collectibles, missions, and gameplay to life. This approach enhances player engagement, bridges virtual and physical worlds, and unlocks monetization opportunities through in-app purchases, and location-based gamified brand partnerships.

**Increase Blockchain Integration** The integration of blockchain technology into gaming is not just a trend, but a paradigm shift. This technology offers transparency in transactions, verifiable scarcity of virtual items, and real ownership of digital assets. Players can now own, buy, sell, and trade their gaming assets outside the gaming environments, creating a tangible value proposition that extends beyond the virtual worlds.

**Rise of Play-To-Own Models** Following the success of games like Axie Infinity, there is a clear shift towards play-to-own models in the gaming industry. These models allow players to generate real income through their gameplay, making gaming a more valuable use of time and providing an economic incentive to play.

**Demand for Decentralized Gaming Experiences** Gamers are increasingly aware of the benefits of decentralization, such as reduced censorship, player-owned economies, and democratized game development. There is a growing demand for games that offer these features, as players seek more control and input into the game worlds they invest their time in.

# Key Innovations | Syndicate of Vigilantes

Syndicate of Vigilantes stands out in the vast universe of video games with its unique twist in the competitive FPS gaming scene by bringing it into the real world on a global scale through GPS-located AR and social elements with incentives through prizes to hunt.

**GPS-located AR Gaming** One of the main features of Syndicate of Vigilantes is the addition of Augmented Reality to the game, projected over the real physical world through a smartphone or tablet. This allows players to uncover hidden AR items in the real world, with loot crates, Web3 rewards, and tangible real-world prizes.

**Integration of Real-World Assets (RWA)** A significant innovation of Syndicate of Vigilantes is incorporating real-world rewards into our Syndicate’s missions. Unlike traditional games where rewards are limited to digital items, we offer players the chance to win real physical and digital prizes through collaborations with other brands. Prizes include gadgets, exclusive collectibles, and more. This integration not only enriches the gaming experience but also bridges the thrill of digital entertainment with tangible real-world benefits.

**Event-Based Gaming** One key feature of the game is the event-based gaming model, allowing players to participate individually or as a clan in multiplayer mode to engage in the Syndicate’s missions. This approach fosters a sense of global competition and enhances the game’s intensity. By requiring players to secure their AR items while being hunted by PvE drones and PvP players lurking to capture their rewards, Syndicate of Vigilantes keeps the excitement and competition high while maintaining player engagement in each new event.

**Participative Governance** Syndicate of Vigilantes takes a new approach to game governance, directly involving players in key decisions and improvements in shaping the game. $VIGI holders have the power to vote on proposals ranging from new skins and items to strategic gameplay features, ensuring that game development reflects the community’s desires and needs. This participatory governance not only ensures transparency and inclusion but also strengthens the bond between developers and the community.

These key innovations are the cornerstone of Syndicate of Vigilantes, distinguishing it not just as a game, but as an ecosystem where a passion for gaming, blockchain technology, and rewards with ownership converge to create a new gaming experience in the real world.

# Storyline | Syndicate of Vigilantes

In the aftermath of the First World War, a series of ancient Egyptian and Mayan artifacts were unearthed, revealing not mere static relics, but functioning pieces of a lost technology. While these discoveries were quickly hidden from the public, the intrigue they sparked within influential circles would not be extinguished.

Thus, a radical idea began weaving its way through enlightened aristocratic minds: Bring these artifacts together and use the secrets hidden within to covertly enlighten the nations of the world and bring everlasting peace. From this collective desire, the Syndicate was born.

For a time, all was good. Monuments to the Syndicate’s creed sprang forth in major cities across the globe, each paying homage to the design of the artifacts from ground floor to shining spire. These monuments became regional hubs known as Sanctums from which operatives spread the Syndicate’s vision of light and beauty.

But one building outshined all others, the Syndicate’s global headquarters, the New York Sanctum. A massive complex of soaring towers, glass facades, and integrated transportation systems suspended high above the city streets. Arguably the most influential of the organization’s founders, Arthur DeCosta, called it home until it was razed to the ground and extinguished from existence. His body was never found.

The resulting ebb and flow, working to bring light to the world versus prioritizing self-interest at any cost, took hold. The creed that was once crystal clear to all in the Syndicate became opaque, requiring a new kind of operative.

Thus began the meticulous recruitment of those who would come to be known as Vigilantes. No longer driven by a common purpose, Vigilantes adapted to navigating a web of half-truths within the Syndicate while watching their backs on the streets which continues to this day.

Syndicate of Vigilantes: The story is just beginning…

# Gameplay & Game Modes | Syndicate of Vigilantes

Syndicate of Vigilantes is a disruptive Play-to-Own AR-game with GPS and FPS elements, set on the largest game map ever created; the entire real world, players step into the role of Vigilantes, tasked with completing high-stakes missions assigned by the Syndicate

After downloading the free smartphone app and creating an account, players are challenged to go on thrilling GPS-located missions to find AR-items in the real world. It will require them to be creative and resourceful to avoid their mission being disrupted by other players who received a mission to stop them: Oh yes! Other players can and will disrupt each others missions (and might even shoot each other), so players need to stay on their toes!

When it comes to gameplay experience, and following our Free to Play principle, we will steer away from 'Pay-to-Win' mechanics.

Some prefer being the lone-wolf, some prefer to move as a pack. We think players should get to choose! For those that prefer a collaborative gameplay, they can form a Clan and collaborate towards achieving their mission goals.

Whether solo or with (new found) friends, the goal will remain the same: Enjoy it, kick ass & get to the top of the leaderboard!

**PvE** Our PvE mode embodies an adventure which takes the player on a combination of search and battle missions within the games' storyline. They will be challenged to search for the underlying truth about the existence of the Syndicate and its power in the world.

Players are on their own, being dared to participate in game missions where they, while enjoying the thrill of the game, can gather in-game items and tangible real-world assets and benefits.

**PvP** Players will also be able to battle each other in the hunt for rewards. We have multiple mission templates based on popular First-Person Shooter game modes. Think of game modes like Capture the Flag, King of the Hill, Last Man Standing etc.

Instead of obstructing others, players can also decide to forge alliances with each other and build their own clan, facing other clans in exciting real world battles.

**Tournaments & Events** We'll also will host tournaments and events, for the best vigilantes of the leaderboard. They will be invited to compete in special tournaments and other events, which will all be streamed.

**Hosted Syndicate Mission Hunts/Partner Program** Through our online partner program, players will have the opportunity to get rewards from other projects or companies.

# Token Utility | Syndicate of Vigilantes

The $VIGI token has multiple utilities within the Syndicate of Vigilantes eco-system:

*   In-game currency
    
*   In-game purchases
    
*   Play-to-Own
    
*   Governance
    

**In-game currency** $VIGI tokens can be used to purchase in-game items that can enhance their gameplay experience. They can either buy it first-hand via the in-game store, or from other players from the ‘Black Market’ marketplace. The marketplace also offers players the option to sell their previously bought items.

In-game items are more than mere tools—they’re the lifeblood of survival and supremacy, empowering players to rise through the ranks of this shadowy world. These virtual assets, encompassing gear and armor, are crafted to accelerate progress and tilt the odds in a player's favor. To give some examples:

Wearing an invisibility cloak, to avoid being seen by enemy drones or other vigilantes. Equip gun enhancers to turn a standard firearm into a lethal instrument of precision, perfect for taking down heavily armed foes. For those hunting hidden threats, the invisibility detector cuts through deception, exposing cloaked adversaries in the chaos of the streets. And with booby traps, players can lay cunning ambushes—rigged explosives or tripwires that punish the unwary. Beyond these, imagine kevlar vests to shrug off enemy fire, or hacking devices to seize control of security systems and break into their cipher. These items don’t just enhance gameplay—they blend strategy, stealth, and raw power in a world where every move counts.

**Play-to-Own** A big chunk of the token total supply will be available to intensify new players to play Syndicate of Vigilantes. As stated, we believe we should motivate players to grow their position in the game in an organic way, yet we acknowledge that not everybody in the world is fortunate enough to focus their off-work time on a game. Syndicate of Vigilantes will be launched as a Free-to-Play game so anyone who owns a smartphone or tablet will be able to play the game.

**Governance** It is our intention to encourage players to participate in the building of the game & the project. We set up a governance model where our $VIGI token holders and NFT holders will be able to vote on propositions that have an influence on the game such as the storyline, new skins, game feature development prioritization, etc.

**In-game marketplace** Syndicate of Vigilantes will provide players with an in-game NFT marketplace. We call it: the ‘Black Market’. This is where players can buy and sell the NFTs they either earned in-game as rewards or that they previously bought via one of our periodic sales (new assets releases). Players can bid on items at the price of their liking, or buy them directly at the listed price.

We believe that the vast majority of players will use the Black Market to buy or sell their game related NFTs. Items may also be traded on external marketplaces such as OpenSea.

# Game Features | Syndicate of Vigilantes

**Augmented Reality** Augmented reality (AR) is an extra layer of visual effects which are projected over the real physical world. Players can only see them by using a sufficient device which is equipped with a digital camera, such as a smartphone or tablet.

One of the main features of Syndicate of Vigilantes is its immersive missions, where players search for hidden AR items in the real world. Using their smartphones, players can track, locate, and capture these items

Next to the visual effect of AR there are possibilities of including inciting sound effects which can be experienced during the missions to complete the unique experience Syndicate of Vigilantes is aiming for.

**Clans** The social aspect of Syndicate of Vigilantes will be a key element in the game experience. In the interactive gaming community, we perceive a need for social interaction between players. But as clans get established inside the game, the features offered to the players aren’t extensive enough to satisfy their needs to communicate on a clan level and therefore clan-communication often takes place outside the game facilities; Clans create their own websites and communicate through external forums and social media channels.

Although Syndicate of Vigilantes embraces that enthusiasm and determination and we don’t want to interfere with the organic fulfilling of players need to communicate in their own way, we believe it’s a game developers’ task to provide players with the tools to create their own clan, get invited to a clan and/or become members of a clan but also communicate as a clan within the game app. We believe that being part of a clan will increase the commitment of players as they collaborate and elaborate strategies together, a bonding experience that allows them to create new virtual (and IRL?) friendships.

**Clan Options** Syndicate of Vigilantes will provide players with a very comprehensive package of clan-building options to stimulate players to build a clan, join forces, combine missions and play against other clans. Clan-members will be invited to attend tailor-made Clan missions.

Not only will we provide them with the right gaming tools to play as a clan, but we will also make sure they can identify as a clan-member by adding a numerous number of visual components to adjust their game avatar, game profile and clan page.

Syndicate of Vigilantes believes that the integration of a VOIP during game play will be an indispensable feature that we intend to make available at some point in time.

**Ani & Player Behavior** ANI (Artificial Narrow Intelligence) is the most commonly applied type of AI. This Intelligence system is designed to carry out one or two tasks. Using previous training data and learning experiences it changes the result for each specific user.

Our game software will have a built-in ANI that learns the individual player's behavior, and tailors the experience to each user by understanding the right time of day to introduce a mission, as well as locating missions with the highest percent chance of individual user play through.

# Node Sale | Syndicate of Vigilantes

Syndicate of Vigilantes will lay a virtual grid over the entire world and each grid will have a size of 500x500 meters.

When a player creates an account, he has to choose a grid which he will call his home base. Multiple players can choose the same grid as their home base grid, even better; Clans can choose a grid as their home base too.

**The Grid** By creating the grid and making sure every player chooses their home base, we can establish the distance between the home base of the player and the location they finish a mission. Syndicate of Vigilantes will reward players multiplied by the distance factor; I.e the further a player is from its home base, the more they will be rewarded. But there's a catch: if another player eliminates you, your mission stakes are also multiplied by the same distance factor.

So, the further a player ventures from its home base, the bigger the target it becomes. High risk, high reward: we dare players to dominate the distance!

### 

[](#three-nft-layers)

Three NFT layers

**Three Layers** On top of the Syndicate of Vigilantes’ global Grid will be added 3 layers of Land/Nodes: Countries, States & Cities, all represented by NFTs. E.g: a node is composed of all the Grids on top of which it sits and its borders are determined by the real-world country’s borders.

**Piece of the action** Node-owners get a share of a total 10% fee over every reward that is distributed on their node. A distribution key will be used to determine the value of every transaction in one of the three layers.

The exact number of Country, State and City nodes are being determined as we’re working on the in-game rewards system and tokenomics. E.g. A mission is completed by a player in a certain grid: the player earns a reward.

10% of that reward will be granted as a fee to Node owners of that grid as follows: 0.5% goes to the Country owner, 3% goes to the State owner and 6.5% goes to the City owner.

Country node-owners do not automatically own any State or Cities, similar to the fact that State node-owners do not automatically own Cities nodes. Nodes are freely tradable within the in-game marketplace (the Black Market) and on open marketplaces like OpenSea.

This gives node owners a unique passive income by just holding their node. Owners can distribute their earnings to clans to incentivize them to choose their node as a ‘home-base’. Further details of all of the perks of being a node owner will be released as we move closer to the node sale date.

# In-Game Economy | Syndicate of Vigilantes

**Node NFTs** Syndicate of Vigilantes Node NFTs - Country, State & Cities - have a limited supply. We intend to give NFT owners the option to either sell or rent a part of their node, by fractionalizing the NFT.

**In-game Items** In-game assets can be found as mission rewards as well as purchased from our marketplace from both players as from our periodical sales. Players are encouraged to burn NFTs representing in-game assets in order to generate ‘greater’ assets, clans can specifically benefit from putting clan members’ resources together. This incentivized burning mechanism ensures supply reduction.

We will balance the available marketplace supply according to the number of active players and total available supply.

**$VIGI Token** Syndicate of Vigilantes sale of NFTs/in-game assets, trading fees and other in-game transactions will fuel the Ecosystem Development Fund with $VIGI tokens that will be used for rewards, staking & liquidity incentives etc.

Our token economics may include burning of $VIGI tokens for the purpose of deflation, or additional reward emission, to ensure a healthy circulating supply supporting a smooth game experience for our players.

**Assets Sales** In-game assets will be regularly released into the game through sales or as in-game rewards. Considering the deflationary nature of our in-game assets - via the mechanisms described above - and therefore their rarity, we can assume the resell market prices of those assets to increase on a consistent basis.

**Marketplace** Our marketplace - the Black Market - will allow players to buy and sell the assets they earned in-game or previously bought via our periodic sales (new assets releases). Players can bid on items at the price of their liking, or buy them directly at the listed price. Our NFTs deflationary mechanics allow those in-game assets to be limited in supply.

# ABSTRACT | Syndicate of Vigilantes

We're Syndicate of Vigilantes, the world’s largest free-to-play mobile battle for awesome rewards and treasuries that takes you beyond the screen and into the real world. Combining the excitement of Augmented Reality (AR) and first-person shooter (FPS) gameplay, players get involved in missions from the Syndicate, physically exploring their surroundings to search for AR items that unlock loot crates with in-game assets, Web3 rewards, and tangible real-world prizes; or choose to use the in-game GPS-located radar to track other players to savor the thrill of disrupting other players' missions. Instead of obstructing others, players can also decide to forge alliances with each other and build their own clan and face other clans in battle. Developed by a distinguished team of gaming and blockchain industry veterans, the Syndicate of Vigilantes pushes the boundaries of AR innovation and cutting-edge blockchain technology to empower its players with a seamless and captivating user experience that allows them to own and influence the game they love to play. Syndicate of Vigilantes' core team has extensive experience in both the blockchain space and (AR) games, previously working on VR titles like Space Pirate Trainer along with other AAA titles such as Battlefield, Crysis, Jedi Survivor, Cyberpunk 2077 and The Witcher as well as countless mobile titles. Next to that, Galactic Entertainment consists of Hollywood veterans who have previously worked on blockbuster titles like Star Wars, Blade Runner, Batman and Marvel movies.

Through chain abstraction and on/off-ramp solutions, gamers who are unfamiliar with blockchain and cryptocurrencies can enjoy the full game experience without any barriers.

The Syndicate of Vigilantes redefines how we think about the future of gaming. Combining the thrill of GPS-located AR and FPS gameplay with real-world rewards, this free-to-play mobile treasure hunt offers players the chance to have fun and win prizes, seamlessly blending digital entertainment with real-world benefits.
