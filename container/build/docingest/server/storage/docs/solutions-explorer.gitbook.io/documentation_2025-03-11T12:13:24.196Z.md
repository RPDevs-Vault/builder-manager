# Solutions Interaction | Solutions Explorer Resources

* * *

Solutions Interaction provides a set of features designed to help you engage with solutions on our platform, enabling you to personalise your experience and connect directly with innovators.

Note that in order to access solutions interaction features, you need to login to your Solar Impulse account.

### 

[](#key-features)

Key Features

*   **Add to Favorites**: You can star solutions on their card or solution page to save them for later. Access your favorite solutions quickly from the navbar to revisit and manage your selections.
    
*   **Connect with Innovator**: On the solution page, you can request a formal connection with the innovator by clicking "Connect with Innovator." This notifies our team that you'd like an introduction. We'll contact the innovator on your behalf and organize a matchmaking session. With our strong relationships, we can facilitate the adoption effort and help you engage directly with solution providers.
    

### 

[](#how-to-get-started)

How to Get Started

1.  **Log In to Your Account**: Ensure you're logged in to access these interactive features.
    
2.  **Explore Solutions**: Browse the solutions and use the features to favorite solutions
    
3.  **Engage with Innovators**: When ready, request a connection to start collaborating.
    

By utilizing Solutions Interaction, you can streamline your exploration, stay informed about new developments, and build meaningful connections with innovators to drive sustainable change.

# Report a bug | Solutions Explorer Resources

* * *

Encountering an issue? Help us improve the Solution Explorer by reporting any bugs or technical problems you come across. Your input is invaluable in ensuring a smooth and reliable experience for all users.

**How to Report a Bug**

To report a bug, please follow these guidelines to provide a clear and actionable report:

* * *

#### 

[](#bug-report-format)

**Bug Report Format**

1.  **Describe the Issue**:
    
    *   Clearly explain what happened.
        
    *   Include details about what you were trying to do when the issue occurred.
        
    
    _Example_: "When I searched for 'solar panels', the page did not load and displayed an error message."
    
2.  **Steps to Reproduce**:
    
    *   List the exact steps you took leading up to the issue.
        
    *   Be as detailed as possible to help us replicate the problem.
        
    
    _Example_:
    
    1.  Go to the Solution Explorer homepage.
        
    2.  Enter "solar panels" in the search bar.
        
    3.  Click on the search button.
        
    4.  Observe the error message.
        
    
3.  **Expected Behavior**:
    
    *   Describe what you expected to happen.
        
    
    _Example_: "The search results for 'solar panels' should have been displayed."
    
4.  **Actual Behavior**:
    
    *   Explain what actually happened instead.
        
    
    _Example_: "The page remained blank, and an error message appeared."
    
5.  **Additional Information**:
    
    *   Include details such as:
        
        *   Browser and device used (e.g., Google Chrome on Windows 10).
            
        *   Date and time the issue occurred.
            
        *   Screenshots or error messages, if available.

### 

[](#what-is-smart-search)

**What is Smart Search?**

The Smart Search is an advanced, AI-driven search feature designed to simplify and enhance the way users interact with the Solution Explorer. Powered by a **hybrid semantic search engine**, it combines traditional keyword matching with contextual understanding to deliver highly relevant results.

**How It Works:**

*   **Semantic Search**: Uses AI to understand the meaning behind user queries, rather than just matching keywords.
    
*   **Technology Stack**:
    
    *   **Vectorization**: Powered by OpenAI’s `text-embedding-3-large` model, which converts solutions and queries into vector representations for contextual matching.
        
    *   **Vector Store**: Built on [**Typesense**](https://cloud.typesense.org/)
        , a high-performance vector database optimized for semantic and hybrid search.
        
    

> _In simple terms_: The system doesn’t just look for exact matches but understands what you’re asking for, even if the words don’t match perfectly.

### 

[](#before-vs.-after-the-difference)

**Before vs. After: The Difference**

Previously, the search relied purely on keywords, often returning irrelevant or no results for nuanced queries. Now, with the Smart Search feature, integrated in the default search bar, contextual understanding ensures a significantly better user experience.

**Example Query:**

*   **Before (Keyword Search)**:
    
    *   Query: _"Alternative materials for insulation in France"_
        
    *   Result: 1 **result**, as the phrase contained many keywords that did not match solution titles or descriptions.
        
    

*   **After (Smart Search)**:
    
    *   Query: _"Alternative materials for insulation in France"_
        
    *   Result: **Multiple relevant solutions (102)**

### 

[](#id-1.-login-or-signup)

1\. Login or Signup

In order to get the full Solution Explorer's experience, we strongly advise you to [Login](https://solarimpulse.com/my-login)
 or [Create your profile](https://solarimpulse.com/account-type)
 on our website.

While the standard smart search is available to all visitors, several features like the AI Assistant or Solutions Interactions are kept behind a log-wall.

### 

[](#id-2.-access-the-showcase)

2\. Access the showcase

You can access [the showcase view](https://solarimpulse.com/solutionsexplorer/showcase)
 and browse the key new functionalities directly from here.

On this page you can perform a search, or get a global overview of:

1.  A sample of our **Solutions Collections**: those are high level & ready-made thematic or geographic portfolios.
    
2.  Our latest **Labelled Solutions**: more about the label [here.](https://solarimpulse.com/label)
    
3.  The latest **Use Cases**: those are articles written internally or by innovators to demonstrate their adoptions by clients.
    
4.  Our latest **Initiatives**: Similar to collections, these are portfolios of solutions that we used for specific projects.
    

### 

[](#id-3.-perform-your-first-search)

3\. Perform your first search

You can now perform your first search using our Smart Search feature. Find the search bar on the showcase page and input a search query. This new smart search lets you find solutions not only by keyword but also by context or intent. This enables you do be more natural and verbose in your searches and still get relevant results

#### 

[](#query-ideas)

💡 Query Ideas

Behind the scenes the smart search is using an Hybrid approach leveraging both standard key word match as well as Semantic matching. Learn more about Semantic Search [here.](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Semantic_search)

You can now freely browse your search results!

The colour of the card of the solution is dependent of the solution type.

Red solutions are **Products**

Orange solutions are **Services**

Blue solutions are **Processes**

Green solutions are **Behaviours**

Grey solutions are **Policies**

### 

[](#id-4.-lookup-a-solution-page)

4\. Lookup a Solution Page

Click on a solutions you find interesting.

You can now view a solution page. Every page starts with the solution name, then a clear one sentence description. Then, you have the stage at which the solution is, which lets you see whether a solutions is labeled or not.

This is followed by several informations about the solution and company behind it, like it's labelling date, company name, country of origin as well as maturity stage.

Then, a supporting section explaining further the concept of the solution, the key features (environmental and financial) are further detailed.

Finally, you can discover several tags associated with the solutions, the profile of the company, as well as related solutions.

You can share any solution on your social medias platform by using the relevant links at the bottom of any solution page.

### 

[](#id-5.-refine-your-search-with-a-filter)

5\. Refine your search with a filter

Now, go back using the upper left button to the smart search view. Locate on the right side the panel: **\+ Filters**

After clicking on it, you should see a filtering panel sliding into view

For more informations about the smart search feature, including the filters at your disposal, you can check out the dedicated section

### 

[](#id-6.-switch-to-use-cases)

6\. Switch to Use Cases

On the standard solutions view, you have a **toggle switch** that enables you to switch view between solutions and use cases.

Use case are a new addition, they are articles, linked to our solutions portfolio, that demonstrate their real world applications.

Note that when performing a search, we query on both solutions and use case at anytime, offering you the opportunity to switch from one view or the other with only one search performed.

A range of filters is also available specific to the use cases. You can filter by a range of possibilities including the solution provider, country, city, client and others.

An interactive map enables you to visualise the localisation of each use case as displayed on the left panel.

Use Cases are implementation stories written by innovators and validated by the Solar Impulse technical team. They link a solution provider with an adopter, showcasing real-world applications of clean technologies. Use Cases are tailored to inspire action among potential adopters, attesting that solutions not only exist but are effectively applied in practical settings.

### 

[](#what-are-use-cases)

What Are Use Cases?

Use Cases are:

*   **Implementation Stories**: Detailed accounts of how a solution has been adopted and implemented by an end-user or organization.
    
*   **Validated Examples**: Reviewed and confirmed by our technical team to ensure accuracy and relevance.
    
*   **Solution-Adopter Links**: They highlight the connection between the innovator and the adopter, demonstrating successful collaboration.
    

### 

[](#why-use-use-cases)

Why Use Use Cases?

*   **Inspire Action**: Serve as real-world examples to motivate potential adopters to implement solutions.
    
*   **Proof of Application**: Show that solutions are not just theoretical but are actively making a difference.
    
*   **Impact Metrics**: Focus on the tangible results and benefits achieved through adoption.
    

### 

[](#how-to-explore-use-cases)

How to Explore Use Cases

*   **Smart Search**: Use the global search function and switch from "Solutions" to "Use Cases" to find relevant stories.
    
*   **On Solutions pages:** Solutions with use cases available are highlighted on their cards. If a solution has use cases, they will be available for browsing on the left blue panel.
    
*   **Filtering Options**: Apply filters such as solutions, adopters, and regions (city or country) to narrow down your search.
    
*   **Interactive Map**: Browse Use Cases geographically through an available map interface.
    

### 

[](#who-are-use-cases-for)

Who Are Use Cases For?

*   **Potential Adopters**: Organizations or individuals looking for proven solutions to implement.
    
*   **Investors**: Those interested in backing solutions with demonstrated success.
    
*   **Policymakers and Advocates**: Seeking examples of effective technologies for policy development or promotion.
    
*   **Educators and Researchers**: Looking for case studies of real-world applications.
    

* * *

### 

[](#why-do-use-cases-matter)

Why Do Use Cases Matter?

Use Cases are crucial because they:

*   **Demonstrate Real-World Impact**: Showcasing actual implementations validates the effectiveness of solutions.
    
*   **Build Trust**: Validated stories provide confidence to potential adopters about the feasibility and benefits.
    
*   **Facilitate Adoption**: By illustrating successful collaborations, they encourage others to adopt similar solutions.
    
*   **Highlight Measurable Results**: Focusing on impact metrics helps stakeholders understand the tangible benefits.
    

By providing concrete examples of solutions in action, Use Cases bridge the gap between innovation and implementation, inspiring stakeholders to take meaningful steps toward adoption.

The Solutions Explorer is a next-generation search platform designed to empower decision-makers, innovators, and organizations with access to transformative cleantech solutions. The Solar Impulse Foundation's Solutions Explorer, represents the evolution of years of research and development into an intuitive, AI-powered tool for finding, evaluating, and adopting Clean & Efficient Solutions that address global environmental challenges while driving economic progress.

* * *

[](#the-solutions-explorer-a-journey)

The Solutions Explorer, a Journey


----------------------------------------------------------------------------

1

### 

[](#id-2021.-creation-of-the-solutions-explorer)

**2021\. Creation of the Solutions Explorer**

Launched by the Solar Impulse Foundation, the Solutions Explorer began as a database of technical innovations rigorously assessed for their environmental and economic performance. This milestone came after five years of work involving:

*   **400+ Experts**: Carrying out 4,700 assessments.
    
*   **1,000+ Solutions**: Curated across 72 countries.
    
*   **Transparency and Trust**: Solutions were evaluated through a verified and transparent process.
    

Solutions were labeled based on their ability to provide environmental benefits while remaining cost-competitive and enabling economic growth, setting a standard for innovation.

2

### 

[](#id-2023.-beyond-1000-solutions)

2023\. Beyond 1000 solutions

The Solutions Explorer has grown beyond its initial milestone of 1,000 labeled solutions, expanding to over **1,500 impactful innovations** that address global environmental challenges. In 2023, the platform introduced the **Labeling Update Program**, a dynamic initiative to ensure all solutions remain cutting-edge and aligned with evolving market needs and sustainability goals. This program revalidates after 3 years existing solutions and incorporates new insights, maintaining the database’s credibility and relevance for decision-makers worldwide. These advancements solidify the explorer as a trusted, ever-evolving resource for climate action.

3

### 

[](#id-2024.-scope-expansion-featured-and-non-tech-solutions)

2024\. Scope Expansion, Featured and Non-tech Solutions

In 2024, the Solutions Explorer broadened its horizons with the introduction of **Featured Solutions**, offering a pre-labeling system to highlight promising early-stage innovations. This initiative accelerates the discovery process, enabling faster access to impactful solutions. The **Label 2.0** program revamped the evaluation process, shortening timelines while maintaining rigor to support rapid adoption. Additionally, the platform expanded beyond purely technological solutions to include **behavioral and policy-driven climate actions**, recognizing the vital role of non-technical approaches in driving meaningful and systemic change.

4

### 

[](#id-2025.-redefining-the-solutions-explorer-experience)

2025\. Redefining the Solutions Explorer Experience

Starting 2025, the Solutions Explorer underwent a transformative evolution, integrating **artificial intelligence** to enhance usability and search precision. The platform’s **refonte** reimagined it as a standalone, comprehensive tool, transcending its origins as a solutions marketplace to become a full-fledged discovery and impact platform and support its goal to become the Global Repository of Solutions for Climate Action. A key innovation was the introduction of **Collections**, curated bundles of solutions grouped by themes, geography, or impact areas, offering users a higher-level, intuitive browsing experience. Additionally, the launch of the **Use Case Search** feature brought the platform to life by showcasing real-world deployments of solutions, enabling users to explore tangible impacts and learn from practical implementations, bridging the gap between discovery and actionable insights.

# AI Architecture Overview | Solutions Explorer Resources

* * *

Welcome to the architecture documentation for the Smart Search / AI Explorer. This section provides a high-level overview of how the system is structured and the technologies powering its intelligent search and retrieval capabilities.

* * *

### 

[](#key-components)

Key Components

🚀 Solution Database[](#solution-database)

Every solution in our database is vectorized, enabling semantic understanding and advanced search functionalities. This preprocessing ensures that solutions can be effectively retrieved based on context, relevance, and similarity.

*   **Vectorization Model:** [`text-embedding-3-large`](https://openai.com/index/new-embedding-models-and-api-updates/)
     from [OpenAI](https://openai.com/)
    
*   **Vector Database:** Powered by [Typesense](https://typesense.org/)
    , optimized for hybrid search capabilities.
    

🔍 Search Engine[](#search-engine)

The Smart Search utilizes a hybrid approach, combining **keyword-based search** with **semantic search** to maximize retrieval effectiveness:

*   **Keyword Search:** Traditional exact or partial matching for direct queries.
    
*   **Semantic Search:** Contextual matching using vector embeddings.
    
*   **Ranking Factors:**
    
    *   Length of query
        
    *   Weights assigned to keyword and semantic search results
        
    

This approach ensures accurate and relevant results for both simple and complex queries.

🤖 AI-Powered Agent[](#ai-powered-agent)

At the heart of the system is an adaptive **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** agent that enhances interaction and search capabilities. The agent architecture includes:

**LLM Layer**

The agent leverages the [`**GPT-4o-mini**`](https://openai.com/index/gpt-4o-mini-advancing-cost-efficient-intelligence/)
 model for natural language understanding and response generation.

**Vector Store Integration**

The agent connects to the vector store to retrieve relevant context and solutions, ensuring precise and informative responses.

**Adaptive Toolset**

Unlike standard RAG implementations, the agent includes:

*   A layer of intelligence to determine the best tools to use for each request.
    
*   Integration with [**LangChain**](https://www.langchain.com/)
    **/**[**Langraph**](https://www.langchain.com/langgraph)
     for dynamic workflow execution.
    
*   Deployment on the [**LangSmith Platform Cloud**](https://www.langchain.com/langsmith)
     for scalability and reliability.
    

📝 Summarization and Token Control[](#summarization-and-token-control)

To minimize costs and reduce environmental impact, the agent incorporates:

*   **Summarization:** Condenses retrieved information to optimize token usage before processing by the LLM.
    
*   **Token Control:** Limits token usage intelligently by summarizing conversations and deleting irrelevant messages to ensure efficient operation.
    

This approach not only saves resources but also aligns with sustainability goals.

🔀 Agent Workflow[](#agent-workflow)

The agent's workflow follows the architecture below:

1.  **Start:** The user input is analyzed.
    
2.  **Agent Decision:** The agent determines the best action using tools or interacting with the vector store.
    
3.  **Summarization:** If required, retrieved data is summarized to reduce token consumption.
    
4.  **Token Filtering:** Irrelevant or redundant data is filtered and removed.
    
5.  **Error Handling:** If issues arise (e.g., recursion limits), the agent invokes an error handler.
    
6.  **Final Response:** A concise and informative response is generated.
    

* * *

### 

[](#system-workflow)

System Workflow

1.  **Query Processing:**
    
    *   User inputs are processed to identify query type, length, and context.
        
    *   Query is sent to the hybrid search engine.
        
    
2.  **Retrieval:**
    
    *   The vector database provides semantic matches.
        
    *   Keyword matches are ranked and combined with semantic results.
        
    
3.  **Agent Interaction:**
    
    *   The RAG agent processes retrieved data.
        
    *   Summarization reduces token usage for efficient LLM processing.
        
    *   Adaptive tools may be invoked as needed.
        
    
4.  **Response Generation:**
    
    *   `GPT-4o-mini` generates natural language responses enriched by retrieved solutions.

ℹ️ **General Questions**

What is the Solutions Explorer AI Platform?[](#what-is-the-solutions-explorer-ai-platform)

The Solutions Explorer is a next-generation search platform designed to empower decision-makers, innovators, and organizations with access to transformative cleantech solutions. Built on the Solar Impulse Foundation's Solutions Explorer, it represents the evolution of years of research and development into an intuitive, AI-powered tool for finding, evaluating, and adopting technologies that address global environmental challenges while driving economic progress.

For more information visit [What is the Solutions Explorer?](/resources/introduction/what-is-solution-explorer)

How can I access the Solutions Explorer?[](#how-can-i-access-the-solutions-explorer)

You can access the Solutions Explorer on the Solar Impulse Foundation's [website](https://solarimpulse.com/)
. Alternatively, you can access the Solution Explorer directly using [this link](https://solarimpulse.com/solutions-explorer/showcase)
.

Is the Solutions Explorer free to use?[](#is-the-solutions-explorer-free-to-use)

The Solar Impulse Foundation being a non-profit, we provide the Solutions Explorer free of charge to any user. However, we require a free user signup to access our most advanced features on the platform.

Do I need an account to use the Solutions Explorer?[](#do-i-need-an-account-to-use-the-solutions-explorer)

No, you don't need an account to use the Solutions Explorer. You can browse all the solutions freely as a an unauthenticated user. However, features like the saving solutions as favorites requires an account.

What are the new changes compared to the previous version?[](#what-are-the-new-changes-compared-to-the-previous-version)

We've integrated several changes in this version:

*   AI is now used to perform searches both on the devault solutions and use case browsing.
    
*   We've introduced use cases, linked to solutions to showcase the implementation of solutions
    
*   We've created Collections, which are bundled portfolios of solutions by thematic
    
*   We've added a new landing page to showcase the Solutions Explorer new features
    

Who can benefit from using the Solution Explorer?[](#who-can-benefit-from-using-the-solution-explorer)

*   **For Businesses**: Discover cost-efficient materials for thermal insulation or precision irrigation technologies.
    
*   **For NGOs**: Identify solutions tailored to providing water in developing regions.
    
*   **For Policymakers**: Explore technologies for large-scale land use reduction or renewable energy adoption.
    
*   **For Individuals**: Get inspired by solutions, technical or behavioral, that you can implement today in your lifestyle and reduce your impact.

Am I restricted in the number of searches?[](#am-i-restricted-in-the-number-of-searches)

No, we do not impose restrictions on the number of searches you can perform. However, all users must adhere to the **Terms and Conditions of Use** and respect the system’s intended functionality. Specifically:

1.  **Fair Usage Policy:** Users are expected to use the search system responsibly. Overloading the system with excessive or automated requests, or attempting to maliciously saturate the server, is strictly prohibited.
    
2.  **Monitoring and Enforcement:** To ensure the platform remains accessible to all, we carefully monitor system usage. If we detect behavior that constitutes abuse or violates our Terms and Conditions, such as attempting to overload the server, we reserve the right to take appropriate action.
    
3.  **Access Restrictions:** In cases of abuse, actions may include limiting a user’s access or implementing an **access ban** to safeguard the platform’s stability for other users.
    

By using the system, you agree to use it responsibly and in compliance with our Terms and Conditions. If you have any questions about proper usage, feel free to contact our support team.

For more information about our terms and conditions, please visit: [Terms and Conditions](/resources/additional-resources/terms-and-conditions)

How does the Smart Semantic Search work?[](#how-does-the-smart-semantic-search-work)

The Smart Semantic Search uses advanced AI to understand the meaning behind your query, not just the exact words you type. This is called **semantic search**, which helps find relevant solutions even if they don’t match your exact keywords. For example, searching for “eco-friendly home insulation” might show results for “sustainable insulation materials.”

The system uses a **hybrid approach**:

*   **Keyword Search**: Finds exact or partial matches for your words.
    
*   **Semantic Search**: Uses AI to understand the context and meaning of your query.
    

Powered by **OpenAI’s text-embedding model** and **Typesense**, it combines these methods to give you fast and accurate results, whether your query is simple or complex.

What are Collections, and how can I use them?[](#what-are-collections-and-how-can-i-use-them)

**Collections** are curated bundles of solutions grouped by specific themes or filters, such as **Behavioral Solutions**, **Solutions from Africa**, or other categories. They serve as high-level entry points for browsing the database without needing to perform a specific search.

Curated by our team, these Collections allow you to:

*   Quickly explore solutions within a particular theme or interest.
    
*   Discover insights and innovations grouped by region, sector, or focus area.
    
*   Access relevant solutions without needing detailed queries.
    

Simply select a Collection that interests you to start browsing and find inspiration!

How can I browse solution implementations on the map?[](#how-can-i-browse-solution-implementations-on-the-map)

We’ve added a feature to the default solution search that allows you to browse solutions' **use cases** on a map.

Here’s how it works:

*   Use the **switch** in the search interface to toggle to the map view.
    
*   Explore implementation stories geographically using our **Mapbox-powered map**.
    
*   Search and filter use cases by region, client to find relevant implementations near you or around the world.
    

This feature makes it easy to visualize real-world applications of our solutions and find location-specific insights.

Does the platform support multiple languages?[](#does-the-platform-support-multiple-languages)

Yes, the AI Assistant supports queries in any language. However, we recommend using **English** for the best experience and accuracy.

The platform’s general localization is available in **English**, **French**, **Italian**, and **German**, ensuring users can explore solutions in these key languages. We advise to use the right localisation with the right prompt/search for the best experience.

How can I stay updated on new features and solutions?[](#how-can-i-stay-updated-on-new-features-and-solutions)

Create a [**Solution Explorer account**](https://solarimpulse.com/account-type)
 to stay informed! By signing up, you’ll receive notifications about:

*   **New solutions** added to the platform.
    
*   **Feature updates** or new releases to enhance your experience.
    
*   General platform improvements and announcements.
    

It’s the easiest way to ensure you’re always up-to-date with the latest innovations and tools available.

🚀 **Solution Listings**

I want to be featured on the Solution Explorer. How can I apply?[](#i-want-to-be-featured-on-the-solution-explorer.-how-can-i-apply)

To have your solution featured on the Solution Explorer, follow these steps:

1.  **Sign Up**: Join the [World Alliance for Efficient Solutions](https://solarimpulse.com/submit-a-solution)
     by creating an account and completing your company profile.
    
2.  **Submit Your Solution**:
    
    *   Log in to your account and click on “Create a Solution” to access the Solution Submission Form.
        
    *   Complete **Part 1** of the form, providing essential details about your solution. This typically takes 20-40 minutes.
        
    *   Submit Part 1 for review. If validated, your solution will be displayed on the Solution Explorer as a Featured Solution.
        
    
3.  **Apply for the Efficient Solution Label** (Optional but Recommended):
    
    *   After your solution is featured, proceed to **Part 2** of the submission form, accessible via your Dashboard. This section requires more detailed information and may take several hours to complete.
        
    *   The Solar Impulse Foundation’s Technical Team will review your submission. If it meets the criteria, it will be evaluated by independent experts.
        
    *   Upon successful evaluation, your solution will receive the Solar Impulse Efficient Solution Label, enhancing its credibility and visibility.
        
    

For more information on the application process and benefits, visit the [Submit a Solution](https://solarimpulse.com/submit-a-solution)
 page.

_Note: The entire process, including evaluation, can take up to 14 weeks, depending on the completeness of your submission and the availability of experts._

Can I suggest a solution that is not currently listed?[](#can-i-suggest-a-solution-that-is-not-currently-listed)

Yes, absolutely! If you know of a solution that is not yet listed, you can:

1.  **Reach Out to Our Team**: Send an email to **alliance@solarimpulse.com** with details about the solution you’d like to suggest.
    
2.  **Contact the Solution Provider**: Encourage the solution provider to submit their solution directly by following the process outlined on our [Submit a Solution](https://solarimpulse.com/submit-a-solution)
     page.
    

We’re always looking to feature innovative solutions and appreciate your contributions!

If you have specific questions about the labelling process, you can go to [our dedicated FAQ page on the Solar Impulse's website](https://solarimpulse.com/faq/solution-application)
.

Can the Solar Impulse Foundation view my searches on the platform?[](#can-the-solar-impulse-foundation-view-my-searches-on-the-platform)

Yes, the Solar Impulse Foundation monitors search activity and platform usage to enhance functionality and improve the user experience. Here’s how your data is handled:

1.  **Search and Usage Monitoring**:
    
    *   All searches and interactions with the assistant are logged for analysis and stored in our private database.
        
    *   Cookies are used to track website usage and session activity.
        
    
2.  **Assistant Monitoring**:
    
    *   Data from interactions with the AI Assistant is monitored through the LangSmith platform, ensuring continuous improvement and reliability.
        
    
3.  **Purpose of Monitoring**:
    
    *   To improve search accuracy and refine features based on user behavior.
        
    *   To optimize user experience by analyzing platform activity and preferences.
        
    

Rest assured, all data is handled in compliance with privacy policies, and no personal information is shared or used for purposes beyond platform enhancement. For more details, refer to our [**Privacy Policy**](https://solarimpulse.com/pdf/Solar_Impulse_Foundation_Website_Privacy_Policy_10.2017.pdf)
.

How is my personal data protected?[](#how-is-my-personal-data-protected)

The Solar Impulse Foundation takes the protection of your personal data seriously. Here’s how we ensure your information remains secure:

1.  **Data Collection and Use**:
    
    *   We collect only the necessary personal data to provide services, improve user experience, and monitor platform functionality.
        
    *   Data such as IP addresses, access times, browser types, and interactions with the platform may be collected.
        
    
2.  **Storage and Security**:
    
    *   Personal data is stored securely in compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

3.  **Use of Cookies**:

4.  **Third-Party Services**:
    
    *   Monitoring of interactions with the AI Assistant is performed through trusted third-party platforms like LangSmith, which also comply with data protection standards.
        
    
5.  **Privacy Policy Compliance**:
    
    *   All data handling is conducted in strict adherence to our [Privacy Policy](https://solarimpulse.com/pdf/Solar_Impulse_Foundation_Website_Privacy_Policy_10.2017.pdf)
        , which details your rights, including access, correction, and deletion of your personal data.
        
    
6.  **Your Rights**:

For further information, please review our **Privacy Policy** or contact us directly for assistance.

# Environmental Impact | Solutions Explorer Resources

* * *

At the Solar Impulse Foundation, we strive to align our tools and technologies with our mission of sustainability. Efficiency and minimizing environmental impact are at the heart of our development processes. Here’s how we ensure our digital infrastructure stays efficient and low impact:

* * *

### 

[](#hosting-on-sustainable-infrastructure)

**Hosting on Sustainable Infrastructure**

Our AI and platform infrastructure are hosted on **Microsoft Azure**, a cloud provider committed to carbon neutrality. Azure actively [offsets the CO2 emissions generated by its data centers](https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/explore/global-infrastructure/sustainability)
, ensuring that our operations contribute to a more sustainable future.

* * *

### 

[](#reducing-ais-environmental-footprint)

**Reducing AI’s Environmental Footprint**

To limit the environmental impact of generative AI, we use **OpenAI’s GPT-4o-mini**:

*   **Smaller Model Size**: This model is less resource-intensive than larger generative AI models, reducing energy consumption during operations.
    
*   **Faster Processing**: It delivers responses efficiently, striking a balance between quality and environmental responsibility.
    

* * *

### 

[](#token-optimization-for-lower-impact)

**Token Optimization for Lower Impact**

We’ve optimized how tokens are processed in conversations to further reduce the environmental burden:

*   **Conversation Summarization**: By summarizing interactions, we keep token usage within efficient limits, reducing computational resource requirements. While this may slightly impact the interaction depth, it substantially lowers the environmental footprint.
    
*   **Streamlined Queries**: We’ve designed interactions to be as efficient as possible, avoiding unnecessary computational overhead.
    

* * *

### 

[](#future-commitment-to-open-source-llms)

**Future Commitment to Open Source LLMs**

As part of our ongoing commitment to sustainability, we are exploring opportunities to adopt **open-source large language models (LLMs)** for future product releases. Open-source models that offer better transparency and environmental impact metrics may provide a viable alternative to proprietary models like OpenAI, ensuring we continue to lead by example in environmental responsibility.

* * *

### 

[](#balancing-quality-and-sustainability)

**Balancing Quality and Sustainability**

While these measures may occasionally hinder the quality or depth of interactions, they are intentional trade-offs designed to align our technology with our broader environmental goals.

By using our tools, you’re part of a collective effort to advance sustainability in both the digital and physical worlds. Together, we’re proving that technology and environmental responsibility can go hand in hand.
