The Season Pass represents an elevated level of access, granting you a host of exclusive in-game benefits. It serves as a key to unlock a world of privileges that will greatly enhance your journey through the game.

**Benefits of the Season Pass:**

1.  **Additional Discounts:** Season Pass holders will enjoy an extra 10% discount on shop offers within the game, providing an opportunity to acquire valuable items at a reduced cost.
    
2.  **Elimination of Training Session Usage:** With the Season Pass in your wallet, there is no need to expend Training Sessions to use your NFT Cards in the Arenas.
    
3.  **Priority in Tiebreaker Situations:** In the event of tiebreakers on leaderboards, Season Pass holders will receive priority consideration, affording them a competitive edge.
    
4.  **Additional Card Slot in Albums:** Season Pass holders have the privilege of adding an extra player, manager, or team card when utilizing any of their albums within Arenas, including the basic ones.
    
5.  Bench Wallet: Season Pass holders have the privilege of adding an extra wallet to accumulate more NFT cards. For more info go to Bench.
    

The Season Pass represents your exclusive access to these remarkable benefits, ensuring that you maximize your enjoyment and engagement with the game.

# Fantasy Points | 5TARS WHITEPAPER

[PreviousPrediction Points](/5tars-whitepaper/arenas-game/prediction-points)
[NextSpecial Arenas](/5tars-whitepaper/arenas-game/special-arenas)

Last updated 9 months ago

Fantasy Points are earned by playing your NFT Cards within the Arenas.

_Note: You can only play cards from at least one team playing the match. Only 11 cards can be played per Arena._

### 

[](#how-fantasy-points-are-calculated)

How Fantasy Points Are Calculated

Fantasy Points are determined as follows:

**Players:**

*   If the Player on your Card has **played** within the match you’ll earn **1 point.**
    
*   If the Player on your Card is on the **winning team**, you’ll earn **1 point.**
    
*   If the Player on your Card **scores a goal**, you’ll earn **10 points per goal.**
    
*   If the Player on your Card **assists** with scoring a goal, you’ll warm **5 points per assist**.
    
*   If the Player on your Card is a goalkeeper and **ends with an unbeaten fence in the time he is on the pitch (playing at least 60 minutes)**, you’ll earn **10 points**.
    
*   If the Player on your Card is a defender and **ends with an unbeaten fence in the time he is on the pitch (playing at least 60 minutes)**, you’ll earn **5 points.**
    

**Managers:**

*   If the Manager on your Card’s team **wins**, you’ll earn **5 points**.
    
*   If the Manager on your Card’s team’s match **results in a draw**, you’ll earn **3 points**.
    
*   If the Manager on your Card’s team **loses the match**, you’ll still earn **1 point**.
    

**Teams:**

*   If the Team on your Card **wins** their match, you’ll earn **10 points**.
    
*   If the Team on your Card’s match **results in a draw,** you’ll earn **5 points**.
    
*   If the Team on your Card **loses their match**, you’ll earn **1 point**.
    

Player time played and position information is provided by our data provider and will be the only data considered when calculating Arena results.

### 

[](#point-multiplier-boosts)

Point Multiplier Boosts

NFT Cards also vary in rareness which will impact the boost multipliers that are applied to your points. There are 3 types of Cards, Players, Managers, and Teams, and each type has 3 variants of rareness, Common, Rare, and Legendary.

Boosts are applied as follows:

*   **Common Cards** will add a **x1** boost
    
*   **Rare Cards** will add a **x3** boost.
    
*   **Legendary Cards** will add a **x5** boost.
    

For more information on Multiplier Boosts, you can check out our [**NFT Cards.**](https://5tars-token.gitbook.io/5tars-whitepaper/know-your-assets/nft-cards)

In the event of any data transmission errors from our data provider resulting in incorrect results generation, points calculated using the data at the time of the arena calculation will be considered valid.

# Auctions | 5TARS WHITEPAPER

[NextClans](/5tars-whitepaper/gameplay/clans)

Last updated 11 months ago

5TARS regularly schedules NFT card auctions so that users can be eligible to obtain certain players or hard-to-find rarities.

### 

[](#docs-internal-guid-f16b4dd3-7fff-6879-7774-6a796bc70965)

How it works

1.  **Auction Listings**: 5TARS will host auctions for cards of all rarities. Users can browse through the available auction listings to find cards that interest them.
    
2.  **Bidding Process**: Each auction will have a specified time limit during which users can place bids. The user with the highest bid after the auction period will be the winning bidder and will acquire the NFT.
    
3.  **Bid Requirements**: To participate in an auction, users must meet two criteria:
    
    *   Sufficient Wallet Space: Users need to have available space in their wallet to accommodate the new card.
        
    *   Adequate STARCOINS: Users must have the required amount of STARCOINS to place a bid.
        
    
4.  **Bid Deductions**: When a user places a bid on a card, the corresponding amount of STARCOINS will be deducted from their balance. Additionally, the necessary space in their wallet will be temporarily blocked to accommodate the potential acquisition of the card.
    
5.  **Outbid Refunds**: If another user outbids the current highest bid, the STARCOINS and wallet space previously allocated for the bid will be refunded to the original bidder.
    

By engaging in our auctions, users can enjoy an exhilarating bidding experience and potentially acquire coveted cards for their collection.

3.1. Starcoins Distribution

Within each Match Arena, a Starcoin budget will be earned, depending on your position within the end-of-match Leaderboard. A certain amount of Starcoins will be distributed to the Top Players of each match. When players have enough Starcoins, they can exchange them in the Shop for NFT cards or gift cards to spend outside the game on any products they want.

**In the event of a point tie, the user who joined the Match Arena first will win**

The points you earn will determine your position on the Leaderboards. You can climb the ranks of the Round, Monthly, and Seasonal Leaderboards by playing in various Arenas.

The rewards will depend on the Arenas as shown below.

### 

[](#docs-internal-guid-f16b4dd3-7fff-6879-7774-6a796bc70965-1)

Arenas Distribution

There are two types of Arenas in which a user can play in 5TARS, the **Common** Arenas and the **Sponsored** Arenas.

Both **Common** Arenas and **Sponsored** Arenas will distribute Starcoins depending on the Player’s and Clan´s position on the Leaderboard and will be awarded automatically. The only difference between both types is the prize distributed. Sponsored Arenas prizes are significantly higher.

Regardless of the type of Arena, the prize awarded will be divided into two parts. One half is for the user's leaderboard and the other is for the clan leaderboard.

### 

[](#docs-internal-guid-f16b4dd3-7fff-6879-7774-6a796bc70965-2)

Individual Users Leaderboard

**Prize Pool Allocation**

*   Half of the total Arena prize pool is allocated to the individual user leaderboard.
    
*   Each Arena has a specified prize pool, divided among individual players based on their leaderboard rankings.
    

**Distribution Among Individual Players**

*   The top-ranked player receives 20% of the total prize pool.
    
*   The second-ranked player gets 20% of the remaining prize pool after the top player’s share is deducted.
    
*   This pattern continues down the leaderboard, with each subsequent player receiving 20% of the remaining prize, ensuring a progressive distribution until the entire half of the pool is awarded.
    

### 

[](#docs-internal-guid-f16b4dd3-7fff-6879-7774-6a796bc70965-3)

Clan Leaderboards

**Prize Pool Allocation for Clans**

*   The other half of the total Arena prize pool is reserved for the Clan leaderboard.
    
*   This ensures a balanced focus between individual performance and team (Clan) cooperation.
    

**Clan Participation Criteria**

*   A Clan is only eligible for the leaderboard if at least three of its members participate in the Arena.
    
*   This requirement encourages Clan activity and collective participation.
    

**Top-10 User Scoring Rule**

*   Only the points scored by the top 10 members of each Clan in that Arena contribute to the Clan’s position on the leaderboard.
    
*   This rule is designed to level the playing field, ensuring Clans are not advantaged simply by having more members.
    
*   It emphasizes the quality of players’ predictions and strategies over the quantity of Clan members.
    

**Prize Distribution Among Clan Members**

*   The prize awarded to a Clan is distributed among all its members, not just the top 10 contributors.
    
*   The distribution is proportional to each member's individual point tally in the Arena.
    
*   The proportion is calculated based on each member's points relative to the total points scored by all Clan members in that Arena.
    
*   This system rewards every contributing Clan member, recognizing their collective effort.
    

A user will only get points for a Clan in an Arena if the user is a member of the Clan at the time of joining this particular Arena. In the same way, the user will only receive his/her share of the Clan prize if he/she got points in the Arena joining the Arena as a member of this Clan.

**Minimum Prize Threshold**

*   If the prize calculated for an individual user (both in individual and Clan leaderboards) is less than 1 STARCOIN, it is not awarded to that user.
    
*   Instead, these small amounts are aggregated and added to the prize of the top-ranked player or Clan, depending on the leaderboard.
    

By implementing these rules, 5TARS aims to create a more engaging and competitive environment. The focus is on individual excellence and strategic collaboration within Clans. This dual approach incentivizes both personal skill and teamwork, enhancing the overall gaming experience for all participants in the 5TARS fantasy football community.

### 

[](#special-arenas)

Special Arenas

During special events in the world of football, prizes will have a higher value. In these cases, the final prize will be rewarded as the in-game currency, STARCOINS.

The distribution model will be the same as for the rest of the Arenas (not taking into account clans), so the number of winners and the number of prizes awarded will increase depending on the final prize distributed. Usually, these Special Arenas (Golden Arenas, Platinum Arenas, etc.) will have individual participation without taking into account the clans.

**3.2 Prizes Expiration** Prizes that need to be claimed will remain on the user’s account for 30 days, after which they will expire and cannot be redeemed.

**3.3 Special Prizes** During special events in the world of football, prizes will have a higher value. In these cases, the final prize will be rewarded as the in-game currency, Starcoins.

How it works

To initiate a rental, users set a daily rental fee in STARCOINS (never less than 10 STARCOINS) for their NFT.

Additionally, owners can specify the rental duration, with a minimum period of 2 days and a maximum of 14 days.

Within this specified timeframe, renters have the flexibility to choose the days they wish to possess and use the NFT. Once the terms are agreed upon and the transaction is completed, the total rental fee for the selected period is deducted from the renter's balance.

Upon completion of the rental period, the NFT automatically returns to the marketplace, ready to be rented out again. Owners receive the agreed-upon rental fee, minus the marketplace fee.

Owners have the option to reclaim their NFT for their wallet at any time when it's not currently rented out. The process is the same as with NFT Cards for sale.

Power Cards are users´ gateway to supercharging Arena´s results within the game. These unique NFTs offer the ability to enhance users´ gameplay significantly.

### 

[](#permanent-vs-single-use-power-cards)

Permanent vs Single-Use Power Cards

Some Power Cards are permanent, meaning they can be used repeatedly in several Arenas, ensuring a consistent performance boost. Others are single-use, providing a one-time enhancement for a specific Arena.

### 

[](#docs-internal-guid-f16b4dd3-7fff-6879-7774-6a796bc70965)

Types of Power Cards

**Fantasy Power Card:** When activated, this card provides a 10% boost to the points a user have earned in the Fantasy section of an Arena.

**Prediction Power Card:** Activating this card, a user will enjoy a 10% increase in the points accumulated in the Predictions section of an Arena. Perfect for those who love to test their predictive skills!

Power Cards can only be used using Albums that have space for special cards.
